Section 1:
Amendment No. 4 to HB6004
Hemmer
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting subdivision (4) in ยง 49-6-3502 of Section 2 and substituting:
(4) "Private school":
(A) Means a school that is:
(i) Located in this state; and
(ii) A private school, as defined in ยง 49-6-3001(c)(3)(A), that is a
category I, II, or III school approved by the commissioner of education in
accordance with rules promulgated by the state board of education; and
(B) Does not include a school that is organized by a religious
organization that was founded in 2013, is headquartered in Salem,
Massachusetts, operates with the mission to encourage benevolence and
empathy, reject tyrannical authority, advocate practical common sense, oppose
injustice, and undertake noble pursuits, and that has adopted seven (7)
fundamental tenets that serve as the organization's guiding principles;
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting Section 6 and redesignating the subsequent sections
accordingly.
