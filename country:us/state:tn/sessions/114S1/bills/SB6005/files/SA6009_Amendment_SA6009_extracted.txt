Section 1:
Senate Finance, Ways, and Means 3
Amendment No. 3 to SB6005
Watson
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting the following language in SECTION 1:
In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act, there is
appropriated $211,327,800 (recurring) and $198,367,600 (nonrecurring) for the sole
purpose of implementing Senate Bill 1 / House Bill 4, relative to Education Freedom
Scholarships, if such bill becomes a law. Of the $211,327,800 recurring appropriation,
$148,620,000 shall be from the general fund, and $62,707,800 shall be from the
privilege tax collected under Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-49-104(e)(1).
and substituting instead the following:
In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act, there is
appropriated $208,627,800 (recurring) and $198,367,600 (nonrecurring) for the sole
purpose of implementing Senate Bill 1 / House Bill 4, relative to Education Freedom
Scholarships, if such bill becomes a law. Of the $208,627,800 recurring appropriation,
$145,920,000 shall be from the general fund, and $62,707,800 shall be from the
privilege tax collected under Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-49-104(e)(1).
AND FURTHER AMEND by adding the following new section immediately preceding the
antepenultimate section and renumbering the subsequent sections accordingly:

Section 2:
SECTION __. From funds available to the Department of Education, there is earmarked
the sum of $2,700,000 (recurring) for the sole purpose of implementing Senate Bill 1 / House Bill
4, relative to Education Freedom Scholarships, if such bill becomes a law.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting the language "$1,931,600" in SECTION 9 and substituting
instead the language "$2,319,700".
