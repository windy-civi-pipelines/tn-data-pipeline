Section 1:
Amendment No. 7 to HB6001
Dixie
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting subdivision (b)(4) in SECTION 1 and substituting instead the following:
( ) Developing strategic immigration enforcement plans that ensure the safety
and sanctity of schools, churches, and other designated safe zones, by prohibiting any
state or local law enforcement agency or other state or local governmental entity from
enforcing state or federal immigration laws within one thousand feet (1,000') of schools,
places of worship, hospitals, and other areas critical to public well-being except in cases
involving threats to public safety;
