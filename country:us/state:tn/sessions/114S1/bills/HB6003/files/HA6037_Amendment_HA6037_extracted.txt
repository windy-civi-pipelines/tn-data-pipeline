Section 1:
Amendment No. 3 to HB6003
Fritts
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting the heading "9-4-216. Governor's response and recovery fund." in SECTION 1
and substituting instead the heading "9-4-216. Governor's Helene response and recovery
fund."
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting the language "governor's response and recovery fund"
wherever it appears in subsection (a) of SECTION 1 and substituting instead the language
"governor's Helene response and recovery fund".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting the language "must not revert to the general fund but must
remain available to be used by the Tennessee emergency management agency" in subsection
(a) of SECTION 1 and substituting instead the language "may revert to the general fund but
must remain available to be allocated to, and used by, the Tennessee emergency management
agency".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting the language "The purpose of the governor's response
and recovery fund is to respond to, or recover from, an emergency, as defined in ยง 58-2-101," in
subsection (b) of SECTION 1 and substituting instead the language "The purpose of the
governor's Helene response and recovery fund is to respond to, or recover from Helene
damage in East Tennessee,".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting subsection (c) of SECTION 1 and substituting instead:
(c) Moneys must only be expended in response to Hurricane Helene. At June
30, 2030, any moneys remaining in the fund must revert to the general fund, unless this
provision is amended prior to such date providing otherwise.
