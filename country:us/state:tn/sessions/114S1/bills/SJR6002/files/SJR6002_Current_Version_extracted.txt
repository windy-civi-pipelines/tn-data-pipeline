SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 6002
By White
A RESOLUTION to recognize and commend Shawn and Christy
Hackinson, Rutherford County Chamber of
Commerce 2024 Business People of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should recognize
those individuals whose vision, ambition, and work ethic are driving forces behind the success
of local economies and the economy of this State; and
WHEREAS, two such individuals are Shawn and Christy Hackinson, who were recently
named the 2024 Business People of the Year by the Rutherford County Chamber of Commerce,
a most befitting honor that recognizes their important contributions to Rutherford County and the
State of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, with over thirty years of experience, Mr. Hackinson began working in food
service when he was only fourteen years old and has functioned in every imaginable role at
both corporate and local restaurants, giving him a comprehensive perspective and depth of
industry knowledge; and
WHEREAS, his wife, Christy Hackinson, graduated from Middle Tennessee State
University and has a background in television production and project management, making her
venture into food service a third successful career; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Hackinson's impressive aptitude for learning and mastery, combined
with her husband's abundant well of experience, has led to the formation of a remarkable
business duo in the creation of the Alley on Main restaurant in Murfreesboro; and
WHEREAS, ever looking to expand and improve, the couple have extended the Alley on
Main's capacities to include catering opportunities, which now service more than seventy
weddings and over 250 events a year; and

WHEREAS, their continuous success led them to open another restaurant in
Murfreesboro, One of Nineteen Cocktails and Raw Bar, that focuses on craft cocktails and small
plates, introducing a new dimension into the dining of Rutherford County; and
WHEREAS, the Hackinsons' twenty-year marriage has not only produced two of the
finest restaurants in Murfreesboro but also a beautiful family with three sons: Gray, Parker, and
Huck; and
WHEREAS, the Hackinsons have devoted their careers to improving the well-being of
the citizens of Rutherford County, and it is most fitting that they be recognized at this time; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we recognize and commend Shawn and Christy
Hackinson, Rutherford County Chamber of Commerce 2024 Business People of the Year,
extending our best wishes for their continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
