Section 1:
Amendment No. 19 to HB6004
Love
Signature of Sponsor
by adding the following at the end of the amendatory language in Section 2:
49-6-3513. Private school reporting.
(a) By June 30, 2026, and each June 30 thereafter, each private school that
enrolls a recipient shall report to the office of research and education accountability in
the office of the comptroller of the treasury the following information about each recipient
enrolled in the private school for the previous school year:
(1) The county in which the private school is located;
(2) The county in which the recipient resides;
(3) The recipient's household income level for the previous year; and
(4) The sex and race of the recipient.
(b) The office of research and education accountability shall submit an annual
report to the education committee of the senate and the committee of the house of
representatives having jurisdiction over education detailing the results of the reports
submitted pursuant to subsection (a).
