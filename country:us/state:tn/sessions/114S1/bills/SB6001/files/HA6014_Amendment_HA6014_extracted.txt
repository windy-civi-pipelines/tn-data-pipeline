Amendment No. 9 to HB6004
Dixie
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting subsection (b) in ยง 49-6-3504 in Section 2 and substituting instead the following:
(b)
(1) Subject to the funds appropriated for scholarships together with any
available funds returned to the state treasurer pursuant to ยง 49-6-3506, the
maximum number of scholarships available for the 2026-2027 school year or a
subsequent school year is equal to one percent (1%) of the total student
enrollment in this state's kindergarten through grade twelve (K-12) public
schools; provided, that if such number is greater than twenty thousand (20,000),
the maximum number of scholarships available for the respective school year is
capped at twenty thousand (20,000).
(2) Notwithstanding the twenty-thousand scholarship cap established in
subdivision (b)(1), the department may increase the maximum number of
scholarships available by an additional five thousand (5,000) scholarships, if a
majority of the voters of this state answer "Yes" to the following question printed
at the end of a ballot for a statewide election:
Question: Should the Department of Education increase the maximum
number of education freedom scholarships available to eligible students
in this state by an additional five thousand (5,000) scholarships?
