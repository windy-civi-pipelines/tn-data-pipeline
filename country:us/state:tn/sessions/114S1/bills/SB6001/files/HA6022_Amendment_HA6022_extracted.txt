Section 1:
Amendment No. 17 to HB6004
Johnson
Signature of Sponsor
by redesignating § 49-6-3512 in Section 2 as § 49-6-3513 and adding the following as a new §
49-6-3512 in Section 2:
49-6-3512. Requirements for private schools that enroll recipients.
A private school that enrolls recipients shall:
(1) Publish the school's lesson plans and syllabus for each class offered
by the school on its website;
(2) Comply with same requirements imposed on public schools in the
Age-Appropriate Materials Act of 2022, compiled in part 28 of this chapter,
including, but not limited to, requiring the private school to maintain a current list
of the materials in the school's library collection and to post the list on its website;
(3) Publish the academic standards for each subject in each grade level
taught by the school on the school's website;
(4) Ensure that all educators employed by, or under contract with, the
private school hold a valid license to teach that is issued by the state board of
education. Licenses issued to private school educators pursuant to this
subdivision (4) must impose the same experience, training, and educational
requirements as are required of public school educators; and
(5) Teach students to the state academic standards adopted by the state
board of education pursuant to § 49-1-302(a)(8) for the grades and subjects
taught by the private school.
