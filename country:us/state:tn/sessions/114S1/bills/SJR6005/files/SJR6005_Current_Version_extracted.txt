SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 6005
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to provide for the adjournment sine die of the
First Extraordinary Session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly.
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that the First Extraordinary Session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly stand in adjournment sine die at the close of business in both
houses on Thursday, January 30, 2025.
