Section 1:
Senate State and Local Government 1
Amendment No. 2 to SB6007
Briggs
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting Section 1(a)(B)(ii) and substituting:
(ii) Property that is assessed pursuant to part 13 of this chapter; and
AND FURTHER AMEND by inserting the following as a new subsection immediately following
subsection (d) in Section 1 and redesignating the remaining subsections accordingly:
(e) Payments under this section must be provided to only one (1) owner per property. If
more than one (1) owner seeks payment for the same property, the comptroller of the treasury
shall disburse payment to the owner who first submits all necessary information and correctly
completed documentation as required under subsection (d).
