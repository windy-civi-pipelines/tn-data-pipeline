Section 1:
House Naming & Designating 1
Amendment No. 1 to HB0325
Bulso
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting all language after the caption and substituting:
WHEREAS, Father Charles Frederick Strobel was a Nashville native, grew up in the
Germantown neighborhood, and fostered deep connections within the community and churches
in the area; and
WHEREAS, Father Strobel believed in living his faith on a daily basis, pulling inspiration
from the Sermon on the Mount, among other holy texts and divine inspirations; and
WHEREAS, upon becoming a priest, Father Strobel quickly and effectively set his sights
on bettering the lives of the homeless and impoverished by creating a single site of services
focusing on health, education, income, and housing with the heartbeat of the programs based in
sheltering congregations of many faiths throughout Middle Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Father Strobel founded the Room in the Inn, a sanctuary opened in a
church parking lot in the winter of 1985; and
WHEREAS, the Room in the Inn has since blossomed into a national model for assisting
the homeless; since 1986, hundreds of congregations and thousands of volunteers have worked
together to serve those struggling with homelessness, welcoming over 4,000 individuals each
year; and
WHEREAS, this State seeks to honor Father Strobel, a champion for the poor, and how
he worked to serve those struggling with homelessness in Tennessee and created programs for
the homeless that have become a successful model for the entire nation; now, therefore,
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
HA0001
003321
- 1 -

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 15, Chapter 2, is amended by adding
the following as a new section:
March 12 of every year is observed as "Father Charles Strobel Day", to honor his
mission, the Room in the Inn program he created, the valiant and noble steps he took to
encourage people of this state to love those experiencing homelessness, and the
positive effect his efforts have had nationwide. This day is not a legal holiday as defined
in ยง 15-1-101.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
