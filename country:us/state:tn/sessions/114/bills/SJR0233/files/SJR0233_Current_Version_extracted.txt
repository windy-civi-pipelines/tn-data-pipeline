SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 233
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Kara Shults on her
receipt of the Governor's Volunteer Stars Award.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should specially
recognize those estimable citizens who have dedicated their time and energies to helping
others; and
WHEREAS, each year, 1.3 million Tennessee volunteers give over 99 million hours of
community service, contributing $2.6 billion to Tennessee's economy; and
WHEREAS, these volunteers are the backbone of our communities, and by donating
their time and talents to fulfill needs that would otherwise go unmet, they embody the spirit of
giving; and
WHEREAS, Kara Shults is one such exemplary person, who is a recipient of the
Governor's Volunteer Stars Award in recognition of her laudable commitment to her community;
and
WHEREAS, through her dedication to helping her community during the aftermath of
Hurricane Helene, Kara Shults has been an incredible example of a true community leader; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Shults has worked with the local fire department, volunteered with
various community events, and assisted with the local fair; and
WHEREAS, through her membership in the Cosby High School FFA, Kara Shults has
supported local agriculture, been a positive influence on the younger generation, and taught the
importance of community involvement to her peers; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Shults is a model citizen; her commitment to helping others through
volunteerism epitomizes the spirit of the Volunteer State, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
SJR0233
004686
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor Kara Shults for her outstanding
community leadership, congratulate her as an esteemed recipient of the Governor's Volunteer
Stars Award, and extend to her our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 004686
