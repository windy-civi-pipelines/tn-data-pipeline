HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 132
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Raul Lopez and Men of
Valor.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
those estimable organizations and their respective leaders who are devoted to improving the
lives of people in this State; and
WHEREAS, for almost fifteen years, Raul Lopez has served as the executive director of
Men of Valor, a prison ministry operating in Middle and East Tennessee that is committed to
helping the incarcerated and the formerly incarcerated reconcile with their families, society, and
Creator; and
WHEREAS, under Mr. Lopez's leadership, the Men of Valor ministry has contributed to
Tennessee's declining recidivism rate, saving the State millions of dollars while tackling the
viciousness of intergenerational incarceration in society and our local communities; and
WHEREAS, a recognized Latino leader in Tennessee, Raul Lopez is a political refugee
from Cuba who has lived in various states during his lifetime but has called Middle Tennessee
home for more than thirty years. He has immersed himself in his local church and community
and sought ways to improve the lives of others; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Lopez's experience living under a communist regime has fueled his
passion for fighting for liberty and freedom, particularly among Tennessee's growing Latino
community; and
WHEREAS, at a time when Tennessee's Latino community is growing and flexing its
cultural, political, and economic muscle, it is vital that we recognize a Latino leader like Raul
Lopez for his many contributions; now, therefore,
HJR0132
003071
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we recognize and commend Raul Lopez and Men of Valor for their many
contributions to the State of Tennessee, extending our deepest gratitude for the organization's
important work and our best wishes for its continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
