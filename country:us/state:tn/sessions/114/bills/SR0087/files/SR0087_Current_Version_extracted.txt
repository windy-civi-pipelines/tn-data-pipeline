SENATE RESOLUTION 87
By Oliver
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Likisha Rhodes, the
2024-2025 Cane Ridge High School Teacher of
the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should specially
recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the highest-
quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Likisha Rhodes is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Rhodes was recently selected as the 2024-2025 Cane Ridge High
School Teacher of the Year, an honor most befitting her outstanding tenure as a teacher, as it
recognizes the positive influence she has on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Rhodes is an extremely talented and compassionate educator who
works assiduously to motivate her students to reach their full potential, both as scholars and as
citizens; and
WHEREAS, she is truly an inspiration to her colleagues and students alike, and her
friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Likisha Rhodes epitomizes the ideal of the dedicated teacher who is wholly
committed to the highest values of public service and quality education for all, and she should
be specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and congratulate
Likisha Rhodes on being named the 2024-2025 Cane Ridge High School Teacher of the Year,

expertise and personal excellence, and wish her all the best in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
