Section 1:
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 96
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Allen Carter of
McMinn County to the advisory board for the
University of Tennessee, Knoxville.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, established advisory boards
for the University of Tennessee, Knoxville; University of Tennessee at Martin; University of
Tennessee at Chattanooga; and University of Tennessee Health Science Center; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that the governor
shall appoint five members to each advisory board; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Allen Carter of McMinn County to serve as
a member of the advisory board for the University of Tennessee, Knoxville; and
WHEREAS, a highly successful businessman, Allen Carter is the sole owner of Athens
Insurance and holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Finance from the University of Tennessee,
Knoxville; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the advisory board for the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, shall
be confirmed by the General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby confirm the appointment of Allen Carter
to the advisory board for the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, for a term that expires on June
30, 2028.
