Signature of Sponsor
by deleting SECTION 2 and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 71, Chapter 3, Part 5, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
The commissioner of human services shall develop an attestation form that may
be used by a primary caregiver for the purpose of attesting, under penalty of perjury, to
the primary caregiver's direct family bloodline to a child enrolled in the primary
caregiver's child care agency, indicating that the child is related to the primary caregiver
as a half-niece or half-nephew. The commissioner shall make the attestation form
available in a downloadable format for free on the department's public website.

Section 3:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
