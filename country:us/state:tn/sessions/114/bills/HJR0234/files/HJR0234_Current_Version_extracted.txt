HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 234
By Faison
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the flood relief
organizations and volunteers of Cocke County
upon their receipt of the 2025 Great Smoky
Mountain Council Good Scout Award.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should specially
recognize those estimable citizens and organizations that have dedicated their time and
energies to helping others; and
WHEREAS, such noteworthy organizations include the Cocke County Emergency
Management Agency, Cocke County Fire Department, the Cocke County Sheriff's Department,
the Cocke County Volunteer Fire Department, the Newport City Police Department, the Newport
Fire Department, and the Newport Rescue Squad, all of which are recipients of the 2025 Great
Smoky Mountain Council Good Scout Award; and
WHEREAS, through their resilience, compassion, and teamwork, these estimable
organizations helped Cocke County recover and rebuild in the aftermath of Hurricane Helene,
and their members and volunteers epitomize the spirit and commitment that are characteristic of
true Tennesseans; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud the members of the Cocke County Emergency Management Agency, Cocke
County Fire Department, the Cocke County Sheriff's Department, the Cocke County Volunteer
Fire Department, the Newport City Police Department, the Newport Fire Department, and the
Newport Rescue Squad for their dedication to the State of Tennessee and its citizens; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
HJR0234
003687
- 1 -
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the flood relief organizations and volunteers of
Cocke County upon their receipt of the 2025 Great Smoky Mountain Council Good Scout
Award, thank them for their meritorious service to Cocke County and this State, and extend to
them our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 003687
