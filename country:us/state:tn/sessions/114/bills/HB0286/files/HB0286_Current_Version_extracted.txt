Section 1:
By Roberts
HOUSE BILL 286
By Lafferty
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4,
Chapter 5, relative to the Uniform Administrative
Procedures Act.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4, Chapter 5, Part 4, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
Each agency head shall complete periodic training for purposes of complying
with this part.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
