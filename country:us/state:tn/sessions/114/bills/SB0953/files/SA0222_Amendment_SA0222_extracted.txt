SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 8-36-211, is amended by adding the
following new subsections:
(i)
(1) After July 1, 2025, a political subdivision may adopt a supplemental
bridge benefit for members who are in positions covered by the mandatory
retirement provisions of ยง 8-36-205(a)(2). This supplemental bridge benefit will
apply to those members retiring on or after the effective date of the resolution
adopted by the political subdivision's chief governing body, and who have
reached fifty-five (55) years of age, but less than sixty-two (62) years of age. The
supplemental bridge benefit and any cost-of-living adjustments attributable to that
benefit must commence on the member's effective date of retirement or on the
first day of the month following the month the member reaches fifty-five (55)
years of age, whichever is later. Members retiring between fifty-five (55) and
sixty (60) years of age shall be paid the supplemental bridge benefit for a period
not to exceed seven (7) years after the member's effective date of retirement.
Members retiring after reaching sixty (60) years of age, but no later than sixty-two
(62) years of age shall be paid the supplemental bridge benefit until the member
reaches sixty-seven (67) years of age. The supplemental bridge benefit and any
cost-of-living adjustments attributable to the benefit must cease on the first day of
the month following the month in which the member dies, or on the first day of

(2) Notwithstanding ยง 8-35-251, after July 1, 2025, political subdivisions

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 8-36-211(g), is amended by deleting
the subdivision and substituting:
(g) Subdivision (f)(1) becomes effective on July 1, 1998, for service covered
under subsection (a), or upon the effective date of the mandatory retirement age
provision for service covered under subsection (b). Subdivision (f)(2) becomes effective
on July 1, 2007, for service covered under subsection (a), or upon the effective date of
the resolution adopted pursuant to subdivision (f)(2) for service covered under
subsection (b). Subdivision (i)(1) becomes available for adoption by political
subdivisions July 1, 2025, and becomes effective upon the effective date of the
resolution to adopt the supplemental bridge benefit provision for service covered under
subsection (b), which includes the supplement bridge benefit, or the effective date of the
resolution to adopt the new supplemental bridge benefit in accordance with subdivision
(i)(2).

SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
