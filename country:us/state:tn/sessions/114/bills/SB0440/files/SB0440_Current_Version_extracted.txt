AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 56,
relative to excepted benefits exemptions.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 56, Chapter 26, Part 1, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
Notwithstanding this title, excepted benefits as defined in ยง 56-7-2802 are
exempt from a requirement to provide coverage for a specific person, provider,
treatment, service, condition, or disease unless coverage of excepted benefits is
expressly required by law.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it, and
