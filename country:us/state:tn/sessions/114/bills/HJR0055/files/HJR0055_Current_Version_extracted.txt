HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 55
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Larry Riggsbee of
Sumner County for his dedicated service to the
children of Tennessee.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize a
conscientious Tennessean who is a living legend in the Tennessee school bus industry; and
WHEREAS, that esteemed individual is Larry Riggsbee of Portland, who oversaw the
transportation of generations of school children in Sumner County as the Sumner County
Schools Transportation Director for more than thirty years; and
WHEREAS, before his retirement from Sumner County Schools in 2009, Mr. Riggsbee
maintained an impeccable safety record, as, under his leadership, no child ever sustained
injuries requiring hospitalization or suffered a fatality; and
WHEREAS, respected by his peers for decades, Larry Riggsbee founded the
Tennessee Association of Pupil Transportation in 1982, and he continues to serve as the
organization's executive director; and
WHEREAS, in honor of his dedication to pupil transportation, Mr. Riggsbee was inducted
into the National Association of Pupil Transportation Hall of Fame in October 2024, making him
only the second Tennessean to receive this prestigious honor; and
WHEREAS, throughout his career, Larry Riggsbee has dedicated himself to the safe
transport of students in Sumner County and across the State of Tennessee, and we find it
appropriate to acknowledge and applaud him on a job well done; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
HJR0055
001743
- 1 -
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Larry Riggsbee for his lifetime of distinguished
service to the pupil transportation industry and the State of Tennessee.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 001743
