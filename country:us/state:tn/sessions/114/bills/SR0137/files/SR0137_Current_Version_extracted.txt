SENATE RESOLUTION 137
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to honor Hancock County Hospital on the
occasion of its twentieth anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is our privilege to recognize those premier medical facilities that
compassionately and capably serve the citizens of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such entity is Hancock County Hospital in Sneedville, which is
celebrating its twentieth anniversary this year, marking two decades of providing the highest-
quality healthcare to all who cross its threshold; and
WHEREAS, Hancock County Hospital is a critical-access facility that first opened its
doors in 2005 to provide state-of-the-art medical care to adult and pediatric members of the
Sneedville community; and
WHEREAS, the hospital's services include emergency care, inpatient care, laboratory
testing, diagnostic imaging, and physical therapy; and
WHEREAS, Hancock County Hospital has an onsite rural health clinic and recently
started an outpatient therapy program, Senior Life Solutions, that focuses on members of the
senior population who are struggling with depression and anxiety; and
WHEREAS, for three consecutive years, Hancock County Hospital was recognized by
Becker's Hospital Review as being among "67 critical access hospitals to know" in the nation for
its clinical quality and excellence in care delivery; and
WHEREAS, Hancock County Hospital has been honored by the National Rural Health
Association as one of the nation's Top 20 Critical Access Hospitals; this award recognizes the
facility as being in the top two percent in the nation for delivery of quality, compassionate care
based on patient experience; and

WHEREAS, Hancock County Hospital has truly become a pillar of the community by
providing access to quality healthcare in this rural area; and
WHEREAS, for the last twenty years, Hancock County Hospital has served its
community as an essential component of the county's growth and the well-being of its citizens;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor the administration,
physicians, medical staff, and employees of Hancock County Hospital on the occasion of the
facility's twentieth anniversary, thank them for their attention to the medical needs of the
community, and extend our best wishes for the institution's continued success in the future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
