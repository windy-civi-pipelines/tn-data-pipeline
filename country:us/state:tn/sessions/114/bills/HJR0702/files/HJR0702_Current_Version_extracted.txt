HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 702
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor Witt Kaslikowski on his achieving a
perfect ACT score.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pay tribute to those students who, through their
natural talents and unflagging commitment to academic excellence, have distinguished
themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Witt Kaslikowski of Clay County High School is one such exceptional
student, who has received a perfect composite score of 36 on the ACT exam, becoming only
the second-ever student to accomplish such a feat in Clay County; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Kaslikowski has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Witt Kaslikowski has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in a number of extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Kaslikowski, with sedulous practice and determination, took the ACT
five times before his final and impeccable attempt, exhibiting a characteristic meticulosity and
scholastic prowess; his desire to secure every possible advantage by completing this
tremendous task is a sign of his sagacious foresight and planning; and
WHEREAS, his display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for his
future success; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Kaslikowski's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride
for his family, his school, and his community; and
HJR0702
007999
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Witt Kaslikowski's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of our
respect, admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we commend and honor Witt Kaslikowski of Clay County High School on
his perfect ACT score, salute his impressive academic achievement, and extend to him our best
wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
