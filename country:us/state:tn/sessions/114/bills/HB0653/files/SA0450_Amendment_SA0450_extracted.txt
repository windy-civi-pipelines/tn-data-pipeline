Section 2:
SECTION 7. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 2-10-114(b), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision (3) and redesignating the existing subdivision (3) accordingly:
(3) Expenditures of campaign funds to defend litigation involving the candidate's
nomination for election or election to office is deemed not for personal use.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting SECTION 14 and renumbering the remaining section
accordingly.
