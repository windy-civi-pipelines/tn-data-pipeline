SENATE RESOLUTION 133
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Justin Coleman on his
appointment as director of the Selmer Utility
Division.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body give special recognition
to those individuals who have achieved significant milestones in their careers; and
WHEREAS, Justin Coleman is one such individual, who was recently selected by the
Town of Selmer Board of Mayor and Aldermen to serve as director of the Selmer Utility Division;
and
WHEREAS, the director of the Selmer Utility Division is responsible for overseeing the
daily operations of the gas, water, and sewer departments and ensuring that these services are
delivered efficiently, safely, and reliably to over 8,000 customers in the community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Coleman has served the Selmer Utility Division with acumen and
alacrity for twenty years, having initially worked as a water crewman in the water department for
five years before transitioning to a role as a gas crewman in the natural gas department in 2010;
and
WHEREAS, in 2022, Justin Coleman was promoted to the role of gas supervisor after
twelve years in the gas department and served in that position for the past three years, earning
the respect and admiration of his colleagues and the residents of Selmer; and
WHEREAS, in his new position, Mr. Coleman hopes to accomplish several key
objectives, including the enhancement of service reliability, infrastructural improvements,
financial stewardship, enhancement of crisis preparedness, and communication with the
department's community of customers; and
WHEREAS, his professional accomplishments aside, Mr. Coleman is most thankful for
the love and support of his wife, Amber, and their three sons, Matthew, Jayden, and John Luke;
and

WHEREAS, wholly committed to public service, Justin Coleman exemplifies the spirit
and allegiance to family and community that are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, his professionalism, integrity, and steadfast commitment to excellence
surely bode well for his success as director of the Selmer Utility Division; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Coleman is a public-spirited citizen of the highest order and is most
worthy of our approbation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby honor and
congratulate Justin Coleman on his appointment to director of the Selmer Utility Division and
extend to him our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
