Section 1:
Amendment No. 5 to HB0923
Powell
Signature of Sponsor
by inserting the following new subsection at the end of the amendatory language of SECTION 2:
(c) By January 1 of each year, each department, agency, or other unit of state
government must submit an annual report to the general assembly of the on the number
of individuals of each race, ethnicity, sex, and age that are employed by the department,
agency, or unit of state government.
AND FURTHER AMEND by inserting the following new subsection at the end of the
amendatory language of SECTION 3:
(c) By January 1 of each year, each county government must submit an annual
report to the general assembly on the number of individuals of each race, ethnicity, sex,
and age that are employed by the county government.
AND FURTHER AMEND by inserting the following new subsection at the end of the
amendatory language of SECTION 4:
(c) By January 1 of each year, each municipal government must submit an
annual report to the general assembly on the number of individuals of each race,
ethnicity, sex, and age that are employed by the municipal government.
AND FURTHER AMEND by inserting the following new subsection at the end of the
amendatory language of SECTION 5:
(c) By January 1 of each year, each metropolitan government must submit an
annual report to the general assembly on the number of individuals of each race,
ethnicity, sex, and age that are employed by the metropolitan government.

AND FURTHER AMEND by inserting the following new subsection at the end of the
amendatory language of SECTION 6:
(c) By January 1 of each year, each public institution of higher education must
submit an annual report to the general assembly on the number of individuals of each
race, ethnicity, sex, and age that are employed by the public institution of higher
education.
AND FURTHER AMEND by inserting the following new subsection at the end of the
amendatory language of SECTION 7:
(c) By January 1 of each year, the governor’s chief of staff must submit an
annual report to the general assembly on the number of individuals of each race,
ethnicity, sex, and age that are serving on a board, commission, committee, or other
governing or advisory entity of the executive branch following appointment by the
governor.
