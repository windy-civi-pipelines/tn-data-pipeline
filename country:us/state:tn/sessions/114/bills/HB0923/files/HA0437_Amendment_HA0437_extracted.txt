Section 2:
SECTION 37. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4, Chapter 3, Part 17, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
The department of human resources shall provide an annual report to the
governor and the general assembly of the number of male and female Caucasians,
African Americans, Latinos, Asians, and other demographic groups who are hired or
terminated based on merit during the previous year. The first report must be submitted
on or before July 1, 2026, and each July 1 thereafter.
