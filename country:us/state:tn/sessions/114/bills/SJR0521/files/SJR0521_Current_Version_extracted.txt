SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 521
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Karina Laureano Ruiz
of Walters State Community College on being
named to the All-Tennessee Academic Team.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
excellence, have distinguished themselves in their scholastic endeavors; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Karina Laureano Ruiz of Walters State
Community College, a scholar of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication; and
WHEREAS, a pre-dental hygiene major from Morristown, Karina Laureano Ruiz was
recently named to the All-Tennessee Academic Team at the Tennessee Board of Regents'
SOAR celebration, a most befitting honor that recognizes her excellence in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, the All-Tennessee Academic Team is sponsored by Phi Theta Kappa, the
international honor society for community college students, and only twenty-six Tennessee
students receive the honor; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Ruiz's notable achievements are a source of great pride for her family,
her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Karina Laureano Ruiz's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the
respect, admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Karina Laureano

Ruiz of Walters State Community College on being named to the All-Tennessee Academic
Team, wishing her much success and happiness in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared

the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
