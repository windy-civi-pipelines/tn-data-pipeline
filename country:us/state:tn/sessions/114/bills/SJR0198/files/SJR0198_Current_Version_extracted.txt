SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 198
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Coach Julie Huebner on
her 300th win as head coach of the Tusculum
University softball team.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing a
softball coaching legend who has attained a significant milestone in her coaching career; and
WHEREAS, Coach Julie Huebner, head softball coach of the Pioneers of Tusculum
University, recently attained her 300th win, setting yet another record in her outstanding career;
and
WHEREAS, Coach Huebner reached this historic mark in her career as Tusculum swept
a doubleheader against Lees-McRae at Red Edmonds Field with both games featuring an
eighth-inning walk-off victory by the Pioneers; and
WHEREAS, an exemplary coach who loves the challenge of her profession, Julie
Huebner is most proud of the academic and personal achievements of the student athletes she
has coached and mentored during her incredible career and is thankful for the opportunity to
have positively influenced so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, Coach Julie Huebner has distinguished herself as one of the greatest
coaches in Tennessee collegiate softball history, and she is most worthy of our approbation;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Coach Julie Huebner on
her 300th win as head coach of the Tusculum University softball team and extend to her our best
wishes for much continued success and happiness both on and off the softball field.
