Section 1:
By Lynn
SENATE BILL 507
By Walley
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 68,
relative to propane.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-135-103(1), is amended by
deleting "the National Propane Gas Association" and substituting "state or national associations
representing the liquified petroleum industry".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
