SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 432
By Rose
A RESOLUTION to commend Sydney Farrell for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Sydney Farrell is one such individual who has distinguished herself in her
role as a legislative intern for Senator Paul Rose; Ms. Farrell's work has resulted in innumerable
benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at the University of Memphis, where she is highly regarded by her
peers, Ms. Farrell has achieved academic success while majoring in political science with
minors in English and legal studies and participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Sydney Farrell brought her many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2025 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Farrell is a source of pride for her parents, Tim and Tena Farrell of
Germantown, as she completed four years of college in two and one-half years; after
graduation, she plans to attend law school; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2025 legislative session, Ms. Farrell has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Sydney Farrell has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight
and rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Sydney Farrell for outstanding

service as a legislative intern for Senator Paul Rose and extend to her our best wishes for every
future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared

the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
