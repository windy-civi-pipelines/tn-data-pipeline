SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 142
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to recognize John Samuel Chance on becoming
an Eagle Scout.
WHEREAS, at a time when this nation's youth face difficult challenges, the Boy Scouts
of America is one of our finest assets, providing young men and women with an educational
program that contributes significantly to their character development, citizenship training, and
improved mental and physical fitness; and
WHEREAS, the highest rank a Scout can attain in the Boy Scouts of America Scouts
BSA program is that of Eagle Scout, and John Samuel Chance, after considerable effort and
diligent preparation, recently joined the prestigious Eagle Court of Honor; and
WHEREAS, his notable record of achievement as a member of Scouting in America
Troop 198 includes numerous merit badges and distinguished service; and
WHEREAS, John's Eagle project, which amounted to approximately 400 volunteer hours
constructing furniture for a study room at the Morristown-Hamblen Library, greatly contributed to
the betterment of his community and reflected the true spirit of volunteerism; and
WHEREAS, Johnny Chance, John's father has been a Scout leader for twelve years,
beginning his own Scouting journey with his son's entry into the Cub Scouts, and in a beautiful
display of shared dedication, the senior Mr. Chance personally presented his son, John, with his
Eagle Scout rank at the induction ceremony; and
WHEREAS, currently a senior at Morristown-Hamblen High School East, John Chance
plans to attend Tennessee College of Applied Technology Morristown to study automotive
maintenance; and

WHEREAS, whether it be camping or establishing new and profound friendships, Mr.
Chance's time in Scouting has enjoyably prepared him for the commitment and service that
mark a good citizen; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should pause to recognize John Samuel Chance as
one of the most outstanding young people in our State; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate John Samuel
Chance on becoming an Eagle Scout and extend to him our best wishes for every future
success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
