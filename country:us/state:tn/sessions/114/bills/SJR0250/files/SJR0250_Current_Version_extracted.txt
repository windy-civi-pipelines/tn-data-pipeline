SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 250
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Liberty Armour, 2025
Valedictorian of Fayette Ware High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Liberty Armour is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
Valedictorian of the 2025 graduating class at Fayette Ware High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Armour has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Liberty Armour has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Armour's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for her future success; and
WHEREAS, Liberty Armour's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride
for her family, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Armour's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor the 2025 Fayette
Ware High School Valedictorian, Liberty Armour, on her impressive academic achievement and
extend to her our best wishes for a happy, successful future.

for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
