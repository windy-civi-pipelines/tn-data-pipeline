SENATE RESOLUTION 230
By Yarbro
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Dlan D Berwary, 2025
Salutatorian of Valor Flagship Academy.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Dlan D Berwary is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
Salutatorian of the 2025 graduating class at Valor Flagship Academy; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Berwary has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Dlan D Berwary has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Berwary's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for his future success; and
WHEREAS, Dlan D Berwary's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride
for his family, his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Berwary's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this legislative body; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby commend and honor
the 2025 Valor Flagship Academy Salutatorian, Dlan D Berwary, on his impressive academic
achievement and extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
SR0230

- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
