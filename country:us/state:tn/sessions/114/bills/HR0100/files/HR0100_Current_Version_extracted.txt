HOUSE RESOLUTION 100
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Dominic Reed on
being named the 2024 WILLCO Awards Football
Player of the Year and Offensive Football Player
of the Year.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Dominic Reed of Centennial High School,
an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven himself to be a
formidable competitor in football and a juggernaut on the offensive side of the ball; and
WHEREAS, in recognition of his success on the gridiron, Dominic Reed was named both
overall Football Player of the Year and Offensive Football Player of the Year at the 2024
WILLCO Awards celebration; and
WHEREAS, Dominic was named the region's Most Valuable Player after scoring thirty-
six touchdowns for the Cougars last season, and he was also honored as a Mr. Football finalist;
and
WHEREAS, Dominic Reed epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as he
has achieved a high level of success in football while evincing great character; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Reed's notable achievements are a source of great pride for his family,
his community, and his school, and it is appropriate that we recognize this accomplished
athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
congratulate Dominic Reed on being named the 2024 WILLCO Awards overall Football Player
