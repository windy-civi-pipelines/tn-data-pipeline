Section 1:
By Reedy
SENATE BILL 520
By Roberts
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 64,
Chapter 1, Part 11, relative to the West
Tennessee River Basin Authority.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 64-1-1101(b), is amended by
deleting "Hatchie, Loosahatchie, Obion and Forked Deer River basins" and substituting "river
basins in the authority service area".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 64-1-1101(b)(4), is amended by
deleting "and the creek and river basins that flow through the counties of Benton, Decatur and
Hardin into the Tennessee River" and substituting "through education, outreach, and science".

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 64-1-1101, is amended by deleting
subsection (c) and substituting:
(c)
(1) These activities are accomplished in the authority service area that is
composed of Lauderdale, Lake, Dyer, Obion, Madison, Weakley, Henry, Gibson,
Carroll, Benton, Decatur, Hardin, Haywood, Crockett, Henderson, Chester,
McNairy, Tipton, Fayette, Hardeman, Perry, Stewart, Houston, and Humphreys
counties.
(2) Notwithstanding subdivision (c)(1), when conducting activities in
Perry, Stewart, Houston, or Humphreys county, the authority shall only provide
technical assistance, engineering services, and project management support to
such county.

SECTION 4. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 64-1-1103, is amended by deleting

Section 6:
SECTION 5. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 64-1-1103, is amended by deleting

works, facilities, and programs particularly required by such county or counties. County

authority service area as situated in the respective counties, and such tax shall be
imposed on that property determined by the county as benefiting from the works of
improvement and programs of the authority;

SECTION 6. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 64-1-1103(18), is amended by
deleting "the Obion-Forked Deer basin authority" and substituting "the authority".

Section 8:
SECTION 7. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 64-1-1104(a)(3), is amended by
deleting "Hatchie, Obion and Forked Deer River basins" and substituting "authority service
area".

Section 9:
SECTION 8. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 64-1-1104(b), is amended by
deleting "for the Hatchie, Obion, and Forked Deer River basins".
