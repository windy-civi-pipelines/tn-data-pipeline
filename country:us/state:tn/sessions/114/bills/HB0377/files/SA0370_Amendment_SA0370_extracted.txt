Section 1:
Amendment No. 2 to SB0376
Yarbro
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting subsection (a) in Section 1 and substituting:
(a) As used in this section:
(1) "Donor status" means the status attributed to an applicant or student
by an institution based on whether the applicant or student, or a member of the
applicant's or student's family, has made or is expected to make a monetary or
in-kind donation to the institution or the institution's affiliated entities;
(2) "Institution" means a four-year public or private institution of higher
education located in this state; and
(3) "Legacy status" means the status attributed to an applicant or student
by an institution based on the applicant's or student's familial relationship to an
alum of the institution.
AND FURTHER AMEND by designating subsections (e) and (f) in Section 1 as subsections (f)
and (g) and adding the following as a new subsection (e):
(e) An institution shall not confer a preference in admission to the institution
based on an applicant's donor status or legacy status. An institution is not prohibited
from awarding different scholarships to students, or the same scholarship to students in
different amounts, so long as the scholarship awarded or the scholarship amount
awarded is not based on the student's donor status or legacy status.
