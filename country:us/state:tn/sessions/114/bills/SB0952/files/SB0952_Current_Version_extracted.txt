Section 1:
By Burkhart
SENATE BILL 952
By Powers
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4;
Title 5; Title 6; Title 7; Title 65; Title 68 and Title
69, relative to utilities.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 65-32-104, is amended by
designating the existing language as subsection (a) and adding the following:
(b) The utility shall publish the policy established pursuant to subsection (a) in a
publicly accessible location on the utility's website.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
