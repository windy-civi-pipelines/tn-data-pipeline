Section 1:
Senate Judiciary 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB1299
Gardenhire
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting SECTION 13(l)(1)(C) and substituting:
(C) The person's only noncompliance with the ignition interlock requirements has
been with regard to the required calibration, monitoring, or inspection of the device. The
judge must make specific findings in the order with regard to each instance of
noncompliance with the ignition interlock requirements, including, but not limited to,
noncompliance with required calibration, monitoring, or inspection of the device and
must attach documentation from the department of safety or the ignition interlock
manufacturer showing that there are no other instances of noncompliance other than
required calibration, monitoring, or inspection of the device.
