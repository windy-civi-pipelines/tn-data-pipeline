Section 7:
SECTION 5. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 37-1-130, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(f) When the court orders supervised visitation due to a finding against a parent
under § 36-6-406(a)–(d) or § 37-1-129, there is a rebuttable presumption that the parent
whose parenting time is restricted and supervised must be solely responsible for all fees
and costs incurred as a result of the supervised visitation arrangement, and that the non-
offending parent must not bear any fee or cost associated with the supervised visitation.
The court may order the offending parent to make payment directly to the relevant
supervising party or agency as required. The court may review and modify the offending
parent's financial responsibility for supervised visitation fees based on changes in
circumstances or upon motion by either party, taking into consideration the offending
parent's ability to pay.
