SENATE RESOLUTION 55
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Debbie Hamby for fifty
years of meritorious service to the Cumberland
Medical Center.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should honor those
individuals who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their
years of service in the medical profession; and
WHEREAS, one such person is Debbie Hamby, who, for fifty years, has faithfully served
the Cumberland Medical Center in Crossville with dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, in her position, Ms. Hamby has earned recognition for her hard work and
distinguished herself as a public-spirited citizen of the highest order; and
WHEREAS, through her unwavering dedication and commitment to service of hope,
health, and healing, Ms. Hamby has made an immeasurable difference in the lives of countless
patients and citizens of the Crossville and Cumberland County communities; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Hamby has proven herself to be wholly committed to the noble precepts
of public service that have earned Tennessee recognition as the Volunteer State, and she
should be specially recognized for her many years of honorable and astute service to the
citizens of this State; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby honor and
congratulate Debbie Hamby upon fifty years of distinguished service with the Cumberland
Medical Center, commend her selfless service to her fellow citizens, and extend to her our best
wishes for continued success and happiness.
SR0055

- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
