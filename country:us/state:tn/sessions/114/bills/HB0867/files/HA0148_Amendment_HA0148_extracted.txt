Section 1:
House Insurance 1
Amendment No. 1 to HB0867
Kumar
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting § 71-5-805 in SECTION 1 and substituting:
71-5-805. Pilot participation goals.
The bureau shall implement the pilot program in as many counties as necessary
to ensure participation of no more than three hundred (300) eligible participants. The
participating managed care organization shall ensure that eligible participants in the
counties selected by the bureau have access to the pilot program. The pilot program
must be operational no later than one hundred eighty (180) calendar days after the date
on which the contract is entered between the participating managed care organization
and the technology vendor, and must conclude no later than two (2) years after the date
on which the pilot program becomes operational.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting the following in § 71-5-806 of SECTION 1:
It is the legislative intent that an initial appropriation of six hundred thousand dollars
($600,000) be appropriated in fiscal year 2025-2026 and each subsequent fiscal year to
offset the costs of the pilot program.
and substituting instead:
It is the legislative intent that the pilot program costs no more than six hundred thousand
dollars ($600,000) and that an amount sufficient to offset the costs be appropriated in
fiscal year 2025-2026.
AND FURTHER AMEND by inserting the language "for the duration of the pilot program"
immediately after the word "thereafter" and preceding the comma in § 71-5-808 of SECTION 1.
