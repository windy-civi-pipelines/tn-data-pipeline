Section 1:
By Lowe
HOUSE BILL 790
By Vital
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Section
55-50-354, relative to honorably discharged
members of the national guard.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-50-354(a), is amended by adding
the language ", including an honorably discharged member of the national guard" immediately
after the language "to an honorably discharged veteran".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-50-354(b), is amended by adding
the following as a new, appropriately designated subdivision:
( ) The applicant's honorable discharge certificate, department of defense form
256 (DD 256), or report of separation and record of service, NGB form 22, that indicates
an honorable discharge characterization;

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
