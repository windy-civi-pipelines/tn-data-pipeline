Section 1:
By Towns
SENATE BILL 617
By Kyle
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 2,
Chapter 10, relative to campaign funds.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 2-10-114(a), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision:
(9) The funds may be used for the payment of any fines, fees, or penalties
assessed pursuant to this chapter or title 3, chapter 6.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 2-10-114(b)(2)(J), is amended by
deleting the subdivision.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
