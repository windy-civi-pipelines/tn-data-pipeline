Section 1:
By Briggs
HOUSE BILL 744
By Wright
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4,
relative to discrimination.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4, Chapter 21, Part 8, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
An organization or entity receiving financial assistance from this state for
programs or activities shall not deny benefits to, exclude from participation in, or
discriminate against a person otherwise eligible for the program or activity on the basis
of race, color, religion, or national origin.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
