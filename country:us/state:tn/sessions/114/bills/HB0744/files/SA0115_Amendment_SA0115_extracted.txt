by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-21-806, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(c) Notwithstanding subsection (a), an organization or entity receiving financial
assistance from this state for programs or activities shall not deny benefits to, exclude
from participation in, or discriminate against a person otherwise eligible for the program
or activity on the basis of religion.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
