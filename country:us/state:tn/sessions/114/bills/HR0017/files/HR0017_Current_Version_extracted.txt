HOUSE RESOLUTION 17
By Cepicky
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Brooke Davis, the
2025-2026 Maury County Public Schools and
Santa Fe Unit School Grades 5-8 Teacher of the
Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should specially recognize those outstanding teachers
who have devoted their careers to providing the highest-quality education for Tennessee's
students; and
WHEREAS, Brooke Davis is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Davis was recently selected as the 2025-2026 Maury County Public
Schools and Santa Fe Unit School Grades 5-8 Teacher of the Year, honors most befitting her
outstanding tenure as a teacher, as they recognize the positive influence she has on so many
young lives; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Davis is an extremely talented and compassionate educator who works
assiduously to motivate her students to reach their full potential, both as scholars and as
citizens; and
WHEREAS, she is truly an inspiration to her colleagues and students alike, and her
friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Brooke Davis epitomizes the ideal of the dedicated teacher who is wholly
committed to the highest values of public service and quality education for all, and she should
be specially recognized; now, therefore,
HR0017
004335
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
congratulate Brooke Davis on being named the 2025-2026 Maury County Public Schools and
Santa Fe Unit School Grades 5-8 Teacher of the Year, commend her exceptional service to the
children of Tennessee, salute her professional expertise and personal excellence, and wish her
all the best in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
- 2 - 004335
