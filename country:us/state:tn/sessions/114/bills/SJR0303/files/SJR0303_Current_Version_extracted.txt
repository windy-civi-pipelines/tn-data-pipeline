SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 303
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to honor the service of Jim Hysen.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we honor those citizens who have performed with
uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their careers; and
WHEREAS, Jim Hysen is one such individual, whose work in land development has
been instrumental as Middle Tennessee continues to grow; and
WHEREAS, after graduating from Howell High School in 1984 and earning a degree in
construction management from Michigan State University in 1988, Mr. Hysen entered real
estate development, where he has flourished for thirty-five bountiful years; his intrepid
entrepreneurial spirit also led him to create his own development business, LandVenues; and
WHEREAS, a leader in his field, Mr. Hysen has served with the Home Builder's
Association of Middle Tennessee for many years, and his immense talents and leadership
acumen being recognized by his peers, he was elected as the association's 2024 president; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Hysen has the utmost concern for the sustainable maintenance of the
local ecosystem, and he has always sought to balance the increasing needs of current and
future Tennessee residents with the ever-present necessity of proper habitats for our beloved
flora and fauna; and
WHEREAS, it is most fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
honor the exceptional career of Jim Hysen; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor the service of Jim Hysen, reflecting on his
contributions to his community and his enduring legacy of excellence in land development.

for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
