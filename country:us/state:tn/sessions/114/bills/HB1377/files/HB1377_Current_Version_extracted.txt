Section 1:
By Akbari
HOUSE BILL 1377
By Harris
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49,
relative to reporting student reading levels.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49, Chapter 6, Part 9, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
A student's report card must include a statement of the student's current reading
grade level.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it,
and applies to the 2025-2026 school year and subsequent school years.
