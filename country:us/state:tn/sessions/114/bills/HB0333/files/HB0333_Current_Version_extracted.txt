Section 1:
By Harshbarger
HOUSE BILL 333
By Crawford
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 8,
Chapter 36, relative to firefighter retirement age.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 8-36-205(a)(3)(C), is amended by
deleting the language "or as the chief of a police department or a fire department" and
substituting "the chief of a police department or fire department, or as a firefighter with a rank
equal to or higher than shift commander".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
