Section 1:
By Crawford
SENATE BILL 352
By Harshbarger
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 2;
Title 3; Title 4; Title 5; Title 6; Title 7; Title 8; Title
12; Title 13; Title 14; Title 41; Title 52; Title 58;
Title 66 and Title 67, relative to public officials.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 4-46-102(b), is amended by adding
the following sentence to the end of the subsection:
The chief executive officer of each state agency and department shall ensure that such
policies, practices, and procedures are posted on the respective agency's or
department's website.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
