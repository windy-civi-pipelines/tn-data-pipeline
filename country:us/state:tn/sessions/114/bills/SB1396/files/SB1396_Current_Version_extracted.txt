AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 40,
Chapter 11, relative to bail.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 40-11-304(a), is amended by
deleting the language “furnish a receipt” and substituting "furnish a written or digital receipt".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 40-11-304(a), is amended by
deleting the language “keep a duplicate copy of every receipt” and substituting "keep a duplicate
written or digital copy of every receipt".

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.

HOUSE BILL 1233
By McKenzie
SENATE BILL 1396
By Roberts
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 40,
Chapter 11, relative to bail.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 40-11-304(a), is amended by
deleting the language “furnish a receipt” and substituting "furnish a written or digital receipt".
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 40-11-304(a), is amended by
deleting the language “keep a duplicate copy of every receipt” and substituting "keep a duplicate
written or digital copy of every receipt".
SECTION 3. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
