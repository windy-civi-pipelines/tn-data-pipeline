Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 5, Chapter 1, is amended by adding the
following as a new part:
5-1-401. Definitions.
As used in this part:
(1) "County legislative body" means that body vested by either the county
charter or general law with the legislative powers of the county government; and
(2) "Self-insures" means a county that provides health insurance for its
employees and pays the associated medical care cost directly to the provider or
through a third-party administrator directly to the provider.
5-1-402. Authority for counties that self-insure.
Notwithstanding another law to the contrary, a county legislative body may
require a competitive bid process for insurance brokerage services if the county self-
insures and has adopted the County Purchasing Law of 1957, compiled in chapter 14,
part 1 of this title.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. The headings in this act are for reference purposes only and do not
constitute a part of the law enacted by this act. However, the Tennessee Code Commission is
requested to include the headings in any compilation or publication containing this act.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
