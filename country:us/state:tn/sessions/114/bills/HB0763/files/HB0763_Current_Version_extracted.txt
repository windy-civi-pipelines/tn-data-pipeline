By Walley
HOUSE BILL 763
By Littleton
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 12;
Title 12, Chapter 3 and Title 29, relative to
insurance.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 29-20-407, is amended by deleting
"governmental entities representing cities and counties" and substituting "cities, counties, or
other governmental entities as contemplated by this part".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
