HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 389
By Williams
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Velma Christine
Tidwell Bates on the celebration of her 100th
birthday.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly pay tribute to those
citizens who are celebrating special occasions in their estimable lives; and
WHEREAS, Velma Christine Tidwell Bates will celebrate her 100th birthday on March 22,
2025, a milestone that will be commemorated as yet another precious souvenir of life's rich
pageant; and
WHEREAS, the daughter of Gillie and Ella Tidwell, Velma Bates was born in the Knob
Creek area of Lawrence County, where she grew up as one of twelve siblings; and
WHEREAS, Velma Bates has lived a blessed life, having experienced firsthand the most
eventful and productive years in American history; and
WHEREAS, joined in holy matrimony on December 6, 1941, she enjoyed fifty-four years
of marriage with her husband, James Gerald Bates, also a native of Lawrence County, before
his passing in 1996; and
WHEREAS, during their years together, the Bates lived in Southern California during
World War II and worked at Firestone Tire and Rubber, making tires and rubberized gas tanks
for aircraft for the war effort; and
WHEREAS, the Bateses lived in California into the late 1950s, at which time they and
their three children, Carol, Barbara, and Doug, returned to Lawrence County; and
WHEREAS, a gracious and generous woman, Velma Bates has held many different jobs
during her lifetime, but being a homemaker and nurturer, expressing her love for all through her
preparation of large, delicious meals, has been her greatest joy; and

WHEREAS, a beloved family matriarch, Ms. Bates enjoys the love and support of her
three children, eight grandchildren, sixteen great-grandchildren, and four great-great-
grandchildren; and
WHEREAS, we wish to grasp this golden opportunity to specially recognize one of
Tennessee's most outstanding citizens on this very special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Velma Christine Tidwell Bates on the
celebration of her 100th birthday and extend to her our best wishes for much continued success
and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
