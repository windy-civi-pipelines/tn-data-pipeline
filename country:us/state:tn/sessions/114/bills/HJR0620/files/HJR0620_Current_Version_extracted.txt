HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 620
By Grills
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Dan Rodamaker for his
outstanding service as Chairman of the Board of
Trustees of the Tennessee Electric Cooperative
Association and his distinguished service to the
electric utility industry.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should recognize those
estimable public servants who have dedicated their professional careers and personal
excellence to improving the lives of Tennesseans; and
WHEREAS, one such notable individual is Dan Rodamaker, whose leadership, vision,
and commitment to excellence have profoundly impacted the electric cooperative community in
Tennessee and beyond; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Rodamaker has served with distinction as President and Chief
Executive Officer of Gibson Electric Membership Corporation (EMC) since 2003, leading the
cooperative in providing reliable electric service to thousands of members across northwestern
Tennessee and western Kentucky; and
WHEREAS, under his leadership, Gibson EMC has seen tremendous growth and
innovation, including the successful launch and completion of Gibson Connect, a broadband
initiative that has brought high-speed internet access to all eligible Gibson EMC members,
benefiting rural communities and fostering economic development; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Rodamaker has also been instrumental in implementing cost-saving
measures that have resulted in millions of dollars in annualized savings for members and
initiating solar flexibility projects that will provide substantial long-term benefits to cooperative
members; and
HJR0620
007124
- 1 -
WHEREAS, a respected leader in the electric utility industry, Mr. Rodamaker has lent his
expertise and service to numerous boards and committees, including the Tennessee Valley
Public Power Association (TVPPA) Board of Trustees, the National Rural Electric Cooperative
Association Benefits Committee, the Tennessee Valley Authority Energy Services Committee,
and the TVPPA Rates and Contracts Committee; and
WHEREAS, as Chairman of the Board of Trustees of the Tennessee Electric
Cooperative Association, Mr. Rodamaker has provided invaluable guidance and leadership,
championing the interests of electric cooperatives and advocating for policies that support rural
infrastructure and economic growth; and
WHEREAS, in addition to his professional achievements, Mr. Rodamaker is a devoted
family man and active community leader, serving on the boards of the YMCA of Trenton and the
Gibson County Joint Economic Development Board, and has previously held leadership roles in
the Trenton Rotary Club and the Chamber of Commerce of Gibson County; and
WHEREAS, throughout his remarkable career, Mr. Rodamaker has embodied the
highest ideals of public service, demonstrating unwavering dedication to the principles of
cooperative governance and the betterment of Tennessee communities; and
WHEREAS, it is most appropriate that we recognize and commend this exceptional
Tennessean for his many contributions; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Dan Rodamaker for his exemplary service as
Chairman of the Board of Trustees of the Tennessee Electric Cooperative Association, extend
our gratitude for his many contributions to the electric utility industry, and offer our best wishes
for his continued success in the electric industry.
- 2 - 007124
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
