HOUSE BILL 382
By Butler
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 39,
Chapter 17 and Title 49, Chapter 1, relative to law
enforcement-supported education programs.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-1-401, is amended by deleting the

Section 3:
section and substituting:
49-1-401. This part is known and may be cited as the "Tennessee Drug Abuse
Resistance Education and Law Enforcement Against Drugs and Violence Act."

Section 4:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-1-402(a), is amended by deleting
"drug abuse resistance education (DARE) program" and substituting "drug abuse resistance
education (DARE) program or law enforcement against drugs and violence (L.E.A.D.) program".

Section 5:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-1-402(b), is amended by deleting
"DARE America program or a comparable program" and substituting "DARE America program,
L.E.A.D. program, or a comparable program".

Section 6:
SECTION 4. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-1-403, is amended by deleting the

Section 7:

49-1-403.
(a) The department of safety shall adopt standards and qualifications, in
accordance with the requirements of the DARE America program and the
L.E.A.D. program, of law enforcement officers who apply for training as
instructors in the DARE or L.E.A.D. program.
(b) The department shall also provide for the training of law enforcement
officers as DARE instructors and L.E.A.D. instructors. The department may

Section 11:

Section 13:
SECTION 9. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-17-420(f), is amended by deleting
"DARE program" and substituting "DARE program or L.E.A.D. program".

Section 14:
SECTION 10. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
