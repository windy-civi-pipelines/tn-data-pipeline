SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 41, Chapter 24, is amended by adding
the following as a new section:
In a facility operated pursuant to a contract to provide correctional services, as
defined in ยง 41-24-102(2)(F), or in a facility operated pursuant to a contract with a county
to hold state inmates and that county has entered into a contract with a contractor to
provide correctional services, as defined in ยง 41-24-102(2)(F), if the death rate of
inmates at the facility is twice the death rate of an equivalent state-operated facility, the
department shall reduce the population at such facility by ten percent (10%). The
reduction in population must continue until the department determines that the
conditions leading to the reduction have been corrected.
