SENATE RESOLUTION 76
By Oliver
A RESOLUTION to recognize Shawndra L. Armstrong on her
selection as the 2024–2025 East Nashville
Middle School Teacher of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest-quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Shawndra L. Armstrong is one such estimable educator, who has
distinguished herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, a teacher at East Nashville Middle School, Ms. Armstrong was recently
named the school's 2024–2025 Teacher of the Year, a most befitting honor for her tenure as an
educator, as it recognizes the positive influence she has on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, Shawndra Armstrong is an extremely talented and compassionate educator
who has worked assiduously throughout her career in education to motivate her students, fellow
teachers, and staff to reach their full potential as scholars, professionals, and citizens; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Armstrong truly serves as an inspiration to the faculty and staff at East
Nashville Middle School, and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are
fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Shawndra Armstrong epitomizes the dedicated educator who is wholly
committed to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be
specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby honor and
congratulate Shawndra L. Armstrong on being chosen as the 2024–2025 East Nashville Middle

salute her professional expertise and personal excellence, and wish her all the best in her future

for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
