SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 340
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor Nikki Pruitt, Chief Operating Officer of
ALPS, on her receipt of the East Tennessee
Aging Innovation Award.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should salute those citizens who, through their
extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as community leaders of whom we can all
be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy person is Nikki Pruitt, Chief Operating Officer of ALPS,
who recently received the East Tennessee Aging Innovation Award from the Tennessee
Federation for the Aging; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Pruitt played an instrumental role in the successful rollout of Caremobi,
an app to facilitate communication between patient caregivers and adult day care centers, for
which she oversaw the pilot program to test the app at ALPS Adult Day Services in Morristown;
and
WHEREAS, ALPS is a nonprofit adult day care facility that takes care of needs of adults
with cognitive and/or physical impairments by providing structure and meaningful activities that
improve or sustain various abilities that improve healthcare outcomes and quality of life
objectives; and
WHEREAS, Nikki Pruitt epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are characteristic of a
true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud Nikki Pruitt for her
dedication to the State of Tennessee and its citizens; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
SJR0340
006078
- 1 -
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Nikki Pruitt, Chief
Operating Officer of ALPS, upon her receipt of the East Tennessee Aging Innovation Award and
extend to her our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 006078
