SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 354
By Yager
A RESOLUTION to commend Reagan Hill for exemplary service
as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Reagan Hill is one such individual who has distinguished himself in his role
as a legislative intern for Senator Ken Yager; his work has resulted in countless benefits for the
legislators he assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Tennessee Technological University, where he is highly
regarded by his peers, Mr. Hill has achieved academic success while pursuing a Bachelor of
Science degree in Political Science; and
WHEREAS, he is an active member of Pi Sigma Alpha and the Baptist Collegiate
Ministry; additionally, he is a staunch Tennessee Vols fan; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize himself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee state government and further his studies, Reagan Hill brought his many abilities and
singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2025 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth General
Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2025 legislative session, Mr. Hill has surpassed all criteria
that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Reagan Hill has performed his many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Reagan Hill for outstanding

service as a legislative intern for Senator Ken Yager and extend to him our best wishes for
every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
