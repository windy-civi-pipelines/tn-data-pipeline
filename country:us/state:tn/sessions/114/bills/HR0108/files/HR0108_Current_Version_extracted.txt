HOUSE RESOLUTION 108
By Hill
A RESOLUTION to honor Katie Lipford on being elected FFA East
Tennessee state vice president.
WHEREAS, organized in 1928 to promote leadership, personal growth, and career
success among high school agriculture students, FFA continues to play an important role in
ensuring the growth and prosperity of agriculture in Tennessee and the nation by expertly
molding our future leaders; and
WHEREAS, we take great pleasure in recognizing those outstanding young citizens who
have distinguished themselves as emerging leaders in Tennessee's agriculture industry; and
WHEREAS, Katie Lipford of Johnson County High School was recently elected FFA
East Tennessee state vice president for 2025-2026 during the ninety-seventh annual
Tennessee FFA State Convention; and
WHEREAS, although she did not grow up on a farm, Ms. Lipford found a place to belong
in FFA, which has taught her the value of agriculture, the power of leadership, and the strength
of community; and
WHEREAS, her duties as FFA East Tennessee state vice president include leading
thousands of student members across the state by speaking with other FFA chapters, delivering
workshops, and representing Tennessee FFA at various events; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Lipford also will serve others through participation and leadership in
FFA conferences, camps, and contests; and
WHEREAS, her fellow FFA members, teachers, sponsors, and, indeed, all the good
people of Johnson County are very proud of Katie Lipford and her accomplishment; and
HR0108
007053
- 1 -
WHEREAS, an outstanding young woman, Katie Lipford has proven herself a devoted
and worthy leader in Tennessee FFA, and she is most deserving of our approbation; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
congratulate Katie Lipford on being elected FFA East Tennessee state vice president, salute her
many contributions and achievements in agriculture, and extend to her our best wishes for
success during her term as East Tennessee vice president.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
