Section 1:
By Bailey
HOUSE BILL 614
By Travis
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4 and
Title 56, relative to pharmacy services.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 56-7-3111, is amended by deleting
the last sentence of the section and substituting:
If a pharmacy shares this information with a pharmacy services administrative
organization or similar entity, then that organization or entity:
(1) Shall not disclose the information to a third party; and
(2) Shall comply, within five (5) business days of receipt, with a written
request from the pharmacy that shared the information to destroy the shared
information.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
