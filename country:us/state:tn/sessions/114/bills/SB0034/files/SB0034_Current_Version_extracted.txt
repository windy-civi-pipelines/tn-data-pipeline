Section 1:
By Freeman
SENATE BILL 34
By Campbell
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 39,
Chapter 17, Part 13, relative to firearms.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-17-1316, is amended by adding
the following as a new subsection:
(s)
(1) It is an offense to knowingly give, sell, lend, deliver, or otherwise
transfer a firearm to a person when the transferor knows or reasonably should
know that the person receiving the firearm is prohibited from purchasing or
possessing a firearm under state or federal law.
(2) A violation of this subsection (s) is a Class A misdemeanor.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it, and
applies to transfers of firearms made on or after such date.
