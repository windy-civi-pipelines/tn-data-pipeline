HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 202
By Farmer
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Dr. Amy Case on her
selection as the 2024–2025 Sevier County
School System Principal of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding professionals who have devoted their careers to providing
the highest quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Amy Case is one such estimable administrator, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Case has been selected as the 2024–2025 Sevier County School
System Principal of the Year, a most befitting honor that recognizes the positive influence she
has on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, a dedicated leader at Caton's Chapel Elementary School for nearly a
decade, Dr. Amy Case has served as principal for the past six years following three years as
assistant principal; and
WHEREAS, a lifelong resident of the Caton's Chapel community, Dr. Case has a deep
appreciation for the school, its families, and the traditions that make it a special place for
learning and growth; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Amy Case is passionate about creating meaningful learning experiences
that inspire curiosity, confidence, and success in all students; she believes in the power of
collaboration and works closely with teachers, parents, and community members to ensure that
every child has the resources and support they need to succeed; and
WHEREAS, under her leadership, Caton's Chapel Elementary School has continued to
thrive, emphasizing academic excellence, student well-being, and a strong sense of community;
and

WHEREAS, Dr. Case is an extremely talented and compassionate administrator who
works assiduously to motivate her students, teachers, and staff to reach their full potential as
scholars, professionals, and citizens; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Amy Case serves as an inspiration to the faculty and staff of Caton's
Chapel Elementary School, and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are
fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Case truly epitomizes the dedicated principal who is wholly committed to
the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Dr. Amy Case on being chosen as the
2024–2025 Sevier County School System Principal of the Year, commend her exceptional
service to the children of Tennessee, salute her professional expertise and personal excellence,
and wish her all the best in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
