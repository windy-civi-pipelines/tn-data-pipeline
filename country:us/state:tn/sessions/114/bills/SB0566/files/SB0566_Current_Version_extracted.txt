Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-105-105(a), is amended by

SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.

HOUSE BILL 1138
By Boyd
SENATE BILL 566
By Pody
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 68,
Chapter 105, relative to blasting.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-105-105(a), is amended by
deleting "shall provide documentation in a timely manner" and substituting "shall provide
documentation in a timely manner and at no cost to the owner or occupant".
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
