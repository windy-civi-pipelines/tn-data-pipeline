by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1.
(a) The desk of house chamber seat number 60 is hereby officially named the
Legislative Desk of Representative John J. Deberry, Jr.
(b) The chief clerk of the house of representatives, or the chief clerk's designee,
shall design and provide for the installation of a commemorative plaque on the side of
the desk of house chamber seat number 60 to recognize the naming of such desk as the
Legislative Desk of Representative John J. Deberry, Jr.
(c) The plaque must include the following language:
The Honorable John J. DeBerry, Jr., a gifted orator, peacemaker, and
statesman raised in the segregated South, who served the Tennessee
House of Representatives and the 90th House District in Shelby County
with honor and distinction for twenty-six years (1995-2020). For many of
those years, he used his abundant gifts from this very desk to give voice
to the voiceless, hope to the hopeless, and love to those in need.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
