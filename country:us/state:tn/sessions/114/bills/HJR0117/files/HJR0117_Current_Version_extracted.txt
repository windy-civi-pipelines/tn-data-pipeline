HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 117
By Hale
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate educator Bonnie
Patterson on the occasion of her retirement.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should specially recognize those outstanding teachers
who have devoted their careers to providing the highest-quality education for Tennessee's
students; and
WHEREAS, Bonnie Patterson is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, an outstanding educator, Bonnie Patterson served with distinction for forty-
three years with the Cannon County school system, recently bringing to a close an illustrious
career devoted to serving the youth of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Patterson is an extremely talented and compassionate educator who
works assiduously to motivate her students to reach their full potential, both as scholars and as
citizens; and
WHEREAS, Bonnie Patterson is an inspiration to her colleagues and students alike; her
friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Patterson truly epitomizes the dedicated teacher who is wholly
committed to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be
specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Bonnie Patterson on the occasion of her
retirement, commend her exceptional service to the children of Tennessee, salute her

professional expertise and personal excellence, and wish her all the best in her future
endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared

the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
