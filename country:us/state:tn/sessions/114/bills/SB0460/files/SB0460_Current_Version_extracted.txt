Section 1:
By Terry
SENATE BILL 460
By Briggs
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4;
Title 8; Title 14; Title 29; Title 33; Title 37; Title 39;
Title 53; Title 56; Title 63; Title 68 and Title 71,
relative to health care.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-1-136(f), is amended by deleting
the language "report to the members of the general assembly" and substituting "report to the
members of the general assembly and the legislative librarian".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
