Section 1:
Amendment No. 4 to HB1270
Pearson
Signature of Sponsor
by adding the following as a new subsection to the end of the amendatory language in Section
1:
( ) A public school or LEA shall not request an individual to produce evidence of
the individual's biological sex.
AND FURTHER AMEND by adding the following as a new subsection to the end of the
amendatory language in Section 2:
( ) A public institution of higher education shall not request an individual to
produce evidence of the individual's biological sex.
AND FURTHER AMEND by adding the following as a new subsection to the end of the
amendatory language in Section 3:
( ) The state shall not request an individual to produce evidence of the
individual's biological sex.
