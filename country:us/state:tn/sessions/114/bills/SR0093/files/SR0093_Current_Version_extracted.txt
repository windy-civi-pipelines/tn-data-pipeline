SENATE RESOLUTION 93
By Oliver
A RESOLUTION to recognize Rachel Miller on her selection as
the 2024–2025 Pearl-Cohn High School Teacher
of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest-quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Rachel Miller is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished herself
as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, a teacher at Pearl-Cohn High School, Ms. Miller was recently named the
school's 2024–2025 Teacher of the Year, a most befitting honor for her tenure as an educator,
as it recognizes the positive influence she has on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, Rachel Miller is an extremely talented and compassionate educator who
has worked assiduously throughout her career in education to motivate her students, fellow
teachers, and staff to reach their full potential as scholars, professionals, and citizens; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Miller truly serves as an inspiration to the faculty and staff at Pearl-
Cohn High School, and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate
to know her; and
WHEREAS, Rachel Miller epitomizes the dedicated educator who is wholly committed to
the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby honor and
congratulate Rachel Miller on being chosen as the 2024–2025 Pearl-Cohn High School Teacher

professional expertise and personal excellence, and wish her all the best in her future

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
