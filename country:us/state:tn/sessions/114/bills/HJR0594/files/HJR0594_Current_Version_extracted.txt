HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 594
By Faison
A RESOLUTION to commend Trinity Malca Vann for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Trinity Malca Vann is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in
her role as a legislative intern for House Republican Caucus Chairman, Representative Jeremy
Faison; Ms. Vann's work has resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted
and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a source of great pride for her parents, Charlcie Pettway Vann and
Romontey Vann, Trinity Vann is also the loving sister of Miniya-Grace Vann; and
WHEREAS, a student at Tennessee State University (TSU), where she is highly
regarded by her peers, Ms. Vann has achieved academic success while majoring in criminal
justice and participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, during her time as a student, she has been affiliated with the TSU Wesley
Foundation, TSU Pre-Alumni Council, TSU Honors College, Alpha Phi Sigma Criminal Justice
Honor Society, Phi Alpha Delta Law Fraternity International, and TSU Community Service
Scholars, and she has served as a TSU Police Department intern; and
WHEREAS, for having the highest GPA in her college, Ms. Vann has been selected to
serve as the Student Marshal and carry the banner at TSU's graduation ceremony; and
WHEREAS, having been accepted to several law schools, including Florida A&M
University, Loyola, and Faulkner University, Trinity Vann will attend North Carolina Central
University School of Law in Durham; and

WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Trinity Vann brought her many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2025 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2025 legislative session, Ms. Vann has surpassed all criteria
that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Trinity Vann has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Trinity Vann for outstanding service as a legislative
intern for Representative Jeremy Faison and extend to her our best wishes for every future
success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
