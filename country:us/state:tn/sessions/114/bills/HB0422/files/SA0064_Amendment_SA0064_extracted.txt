Senate Energy, Ag., and Nat. Resources 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB0323
Reeves
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting the amendatory language in SECTION 1 and substituting the following:
( ) Name real property, infrastructure, improvements to infrastructure, or features
under the agency's jurisdiction. If the commission enters into an agreement to name real
property, infrastructure, improvements to infrastructure, or features under the agency's
jurisdiction pursuant to this subdivision (a)( ) in exchange for compensation, then the
term of the agreement must not exceed ten (10) years.
