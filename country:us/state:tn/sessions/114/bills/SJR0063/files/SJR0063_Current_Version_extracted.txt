SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 63
By Campbell
A RESOLUTION to honor the life and legacy of Darek Tanner.
WHEREAS, we wish to honor the life of Darek Tanner, a beloved member of the
Nashville LGBTQ+ community and the longtime proprietor of Canvas Lounge, for his
extraordinary contributions to creating a safe, inclusive, and welcoming space for all; and
WHEREAS, Darek Tanner opened Canvas Lounge in 2010 with a vision to provide a
space where everyone could feel at home, regardless of their background, identity, or self-
expression. Through his dedication, Canvas Lounge became a sanctuary for many in the
LGBTQ+ community and beyond, hosting drag shows and dance parties and forming
partnerships with organizations such as Nashville CARES; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Tanner's legacy extends far beyond Canvas Lounge; his life was a
testament to kindness, love, and the power of community. He created a place where individuals
could find chosen family and express their authentic selves, embodying his philosophy that
everyone deserves to be celebrated and accepted; and
WHEREAS, his relocation of Canvas Lounge to East Nashville in 2022 further
demonstrated his commitment to fostering connections and being woven into the fabric of his
neighborhood; the establishment enriched the lives of all who came through its doors; and
WHEREAS, the Nashville community mourns the loss of a trailblazer who turned a
dream into a reality, leaving an indelible mark on countless lives through his compassion, vision,
and steadfast dedication to inclusivity; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor the memory of Darek Tanner for his

lifelong contributions to the LGBTQ+ community, his leadership in making Nashville a better and
more loving place, and his legacy of creating a space where all could feel safe, valued, and

family and friends and the entire Canvas Lounge community, and that a copy of this resolution

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
