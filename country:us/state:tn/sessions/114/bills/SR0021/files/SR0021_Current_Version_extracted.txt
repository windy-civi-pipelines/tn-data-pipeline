SENATE RESOLUTION 21
By Lamar
A RESOLUTION to honor Tish Towns in celebration of Women's
Day on the Hill.
WHEREAS, Women's Day on the Hill is a time to honor the achievements of those
Tennessee women who have paved the way for every person to have the opportunity to pursue
the American dream; and
WHEREAS, the Volunteer State is proud to be the home of the awe-inspiring Tish
Towns, a native Memphian who currently serves as Executive Vice President and Chief
Administrative Officer at Regional One Health, where she channels her passion for her
community into providing access to quality health services to all; and
WHEREAS, at Regional One Health, she serves as one of the senior leaders in the
development and execution of the organization's strategic plan and has direct responsibility for
ambulatory services, which includes primary care practices and multi-specialty locations; the
academic oncology program; post-acute services, including rehabilitation, skilled nursing, and
long-term acute care; marketing and communications; marketing development; community
engagement; pastoral care; volunteers; and guest services; and
WHEREAS, Tish Towns has further served her community by offering her time and
expertise to various organizations, including America's Essential Hospitals, the American
Hospital Association, the Tennessee Hospital Association, United Way of the Mid-South, the
Mid-South Minority Business Consortium, the Memphis Chapter of The Links, Inc., Memphis
Symphony Orchestra Circle of Friends, Delta Sigma Theta Sorority, Inc., Southern College of
Optometry, and the Memphis Theological Seminary; and
WHEREAS, in saluting Ms. Towns on this special occasion, let us remember that when
women succeed, America succeeds, as does Tennessee; now, therefore,
SR0021
003934
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby honor and commend
Tish Towns on her many contributions to the State of Tennessee as we celebrate Women's Day
on the Hill.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
