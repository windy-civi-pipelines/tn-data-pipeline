SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 357
By Briggs
A RESOLUTION to commend Janeyah Anderson, Majority Leader
of the 2025 Intern Mock Session.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, we are pleased to welcome a select group of
students who commit themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the
legislative process as legislative interns; and
WHEREAS, annually, during each session of the General Assembly, the intern class
holds a mock legislative session and elects from among their membership a governor, a
lieutenant governor, a speaker of the house, a majority leader, and a minority leader; and
WHEREAS, Janeyah Anderson, a student at Lipscomb University, was elected Majority
Leader of the 2025 Intern Mock Session for the first regular session of the 114th Tennessee
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, during this legislative session, Ms. Anderson has served with distinction in
her role as a legislative intern for Representative Torrey Harris; and
WHEREAS, Janeyah Anderson's commitment to excellence is clearly deserving of the
respect, admiration, and commendation of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor Janeyah Anderson on her election
as Majority Leader of the 2025 Intern Mock Session and extend to her our best wishes for every
future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
