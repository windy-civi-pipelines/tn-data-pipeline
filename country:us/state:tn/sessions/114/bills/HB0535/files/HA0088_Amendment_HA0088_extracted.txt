Section 1:
House State & Local Government 1
Amendment No. 1 to HB0535
Crawford
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting Section 3(a) and substituting:
(a) Any person who becomes a part-time state employee or part-time teacher on or after
July 1, 2025, shall become a member of the retirement system unless the person elects not to
participate in the retirement system by submitting a written, irrevocable election to the
Tennessee consolidated retirement system no later than thirty (30) calendar days after the
person's first day of employment. Any person who becomes a part-time employee on or after
July 1, 2025, for an employer participating under part 2 of this chapter that allows part-time
employee participation in the retirement system, shall become a member of the retirement
system unless the person elects not to participate in the retirement system by submitting a
written, irrevocable election to the Tennessee consolidated retirement system no later than thirty
(30) calendar days after the person's first day of employment.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting the last sentence in Section 19(c)(2) and substituting:
Any person who becomes a part-time state employee or part-time teacher on or after
July 1, 2025, shall become a member of the retirement system unless the person elects not to
participate in the retirement system by submitting a written, irrevocable election to the
Tennessee consolidated retirement system no later than thirty (30) calendar days after the
person's first day of employment.
