HOUSE RESOLUTION 102
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Ella Ryan on being
named the 2024 WILLCO Awards Female
Basketball Player of the Year.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Ella Ryan of Brentwood High School, an
athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven herself a formidable
competitor in basketball; and
WHEREAS, in recognition of her success on the hardwood throughout the school year,
Ella Ryan was named Female Basketball Player of the Year at the 2024 WILLCO Awards
celebration; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Ryan averaged 24.2 points, 6.4 rebounds, and 4 steals for the Bruins';
she was also the District 10-4A MVP; and
WHEREAS, Ella Ryan epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as she has
achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Ryan's notable achievements are a source of great pride for her family,
her community, and her school, and it is appropriate that we recognize this accomplished
athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
congratulate Ella Ryan on being named the 2024 WILLCO Awards Female Basketball Player of
the Year, wishing her much success and happiness in her future endeavors.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
