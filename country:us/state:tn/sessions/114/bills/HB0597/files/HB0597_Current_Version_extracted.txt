Section 1:
By Yarbro
HOUSE BILL 597
By Freeman
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 3,
Chapter 6, relative to lobbyists.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 3-6-302(b)(2), is amended by adding
the following new subdivision:
( ) Identification of each bill and legislative measure for which the lobbyist is
receiving compensation to oppose or support.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
