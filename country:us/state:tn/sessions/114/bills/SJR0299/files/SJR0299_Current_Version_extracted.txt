SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 299
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to honor J.T. King of the Sparta Police
Department on the occasion of his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that the members of this General Assembly should honor
those citizens who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their
careers in law enforcement; and
WHEREAS, J.T. King of the Sparta Police Department is one such estimable individual,
who has devoted his life to the preservation of law and order and the protection of his fellow
citizens; and
WHEREAS, after thirty years of distinguished service to the City of Sparta, Mr. King will
take his much-deserved retirement this year, having dedicated himself to the highest tenets of
public service and law enforcement throughout his career; and
WHEREAS, J.T. King has performed his duties with professionalism and integrity,
helping his fellow citizens in any way that he could and proving himself time and again to be a
tremendous asset to the Sparta Police Department; and
WHEREAS, Mr. King exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to community that are
characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, throughout his estimable career, J.T. King has demonstrated the utmost
ability and honor, winning the sincere respect and admiration of his colleagues and the citizens
of Sparta; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend J.T. King on his exemplary

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
