by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 41-8-103(11), is amended by
deleting the language "pending a parole revocation hearing" and substituting "pending a parole
or probation revocation hearing".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 41-8-106, is amended by adding the
following language as a new subsection (j):
(j)
(1) Except as otherwise provided in subdivision (j)(2), the subsidies paid
to counties pursuant to this chapter for housing state prisoners must be paid from
the date of conviction, regardless of whether the prisoner is housed under a
contract with the department of correction, and must include reimbursement for
such prisoners housed by counties while awaiting probation revocation hearings.
(2) For state prisoners housed under a contract, subdivision (j)(1) does
not affect any rights, obligations, or duties of any party to such a contract that
was executed prior to July 1, 2025, and that remains in effect on July 1, 2025,
and applies only to such a contract executed, renewed, or amended on or after
July 1, 2025, and to subsidies paid for housing state prisoners convicted on or
after July 1, 2025.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
