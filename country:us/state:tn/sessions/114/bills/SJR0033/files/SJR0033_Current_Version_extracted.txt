SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 33
By Yager
A RESOLUTION to honor Ted Clower for his years of service as
pastor of Salem Baptist Church.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should honor those individuals who have answered a
divine call to the ministry and who have served with dedication, vigor, and enthusiasm to
perform God's work for their congregations and their communities; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy person is Ted Clower, who has distinguished himself
as a citizen of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity and as a pastor of the highest order;
and
WHEREAS, Pastor Ted Clower was called to lead Salem Baptist Church in Roane
County in August 1980, and he continued as full-time pastor of the congregation until March
2024, amassing over forty-three years of devoted leadership and guidance; and
WHEREAS, during his tenure at Salem Baptist, Pastor Clower instituted many important,
successful initiatives, such as a bus ministry, a nursing home ministry, Vacation Bible School, a
jail mission ministry, a senior saints ministry, a youth ministry, and a children's church ministry;
and
WHEREAS, prior to becoming pastor, he was a bus driver and a deacon for the church;
his vocational record also includes work with County Farmers Co-Op and Oak Ridge National
Laboratory; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Clower was born on February 26, 1949, to Olaf and Rhea Clower; he
graduated from Midway High School in 1967 and attended Crown College, from which he
received an honorary doctorate in 2009. He also holds a degree from Bible Baptist Institute;
and

WHEREAS, throughout his many worthy endeavors, Pastor Clower has been blessed
with the love and support of his wife of fifty-six years, Euphemia Clower, who has faithfully
worked by his side; and
WHEREAS, Pastor Ted Clower epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are
characteristic of a true Tennessean, and he should be specially recognized for his many
accomplishments and devoted work; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor Pastor Ted Clower for his many years of
service as pastor of Salem Baptist Church and extend to him our best wishes for continued
success and happiness in all his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
