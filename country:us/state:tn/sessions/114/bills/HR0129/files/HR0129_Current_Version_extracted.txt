HOUSE RESOLUTION 129
By Sparks
A RESOLUTION to recognize and commend Paul F. Tuttobene.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should honor those
citizens who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm throughout their years of
service; and
WHEREAS, Paul F. Tuttobene is one such estimable person, who evinces the greatest
integrity and probity in all his chosen endeavors; and
WHEREAS, born on December 3, 1930, Paul Tuttobene was the first American citizen
born in his family after they emigrated from Sicily, Italy, to the United States; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Tuttobene was raised during the Great Depression, and his American-
Sicilian roots in Rochester, New York, were distinctly characterized by a strong emphasis on
family values, faith and religion, and assimilation to American culture, which ultimately helped
shape and prepare him for future challenges; and
WHEREAS, a first-generation college graduate, Paul Tuttobene earned his degree from
Niagara University; soon after, he enlisted in the United States Army and proudly served his
nation from 1953 to 1957; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Tuttobene began employment at Manufacturers Hanover Trust in the
early 1970s and rose through the ranks to senior vice president; and
WHEREAS, Paul Tuttobene took his much-deserved retirement in 1990 following a
lifetime of hard work, and for the next two decades, he traveled across the United States with
the love of his life and best friend, Connie; and
WHEREAS, wholly devoted to his family and family values, Mr. Tuttobene has been
grateful for the love and support of his cherished wife of more than sixty-five years, the former

Constance Gloria "Connie" Schimenti; the couple was blessed with two sons, in addition to a
host of grandchildren and great-grandchildren; and
WHEREAS, Paul Tuttobene is wholly committed to the noble precepts of public service
that have earned Tennessee recognition as the Volunteer State, and he should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby
recognize and commend Paul F. Tuttobene for his honorable and astute service and extend to
him our best wishes for much continued success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
