HOUSE RESOLUTION 1
By Martin G
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Maxine Fields
McKenzie on the celebration of her ninetieth
birthday.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pay tribute to
those citizens who are celebrating special occasions in their estimable lives; and
WHEREAS, Maxine Fields McKenzie celebrated her ninetieth birthday on January 9,
2025, a milestone that was commemorated as yet another precious souvenir of life's rich
pageant; and
WHEREAS, born in Flat Rock, Alabama, Maxine McKenzie has lived an amazing life
and has experienced firsthand the breadth and span of American history; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. McKenzie graduated from Pisgah High School, where she met her
husband, Lewis McKenzie, and then attended the University of Alabama and received a
Bachelor of Science degree in Secondary Education; she has since served as president of the
local chapter of the University of Alabama Alumni Association; and
WHEREAS, in 1959, Mrs. McKenzie moved to Chattanooga, and she first taught in
Rossville, Georgia, before accepting a sixth grade teaching position, specializing in science, at
Alpine Crest Elementary School, where she served for twenty-five years; and
WHEREAS, her fellow teachers knew Mrs. McKenzie to be a strong but fair
disciplinarian as a teacher; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. McKenzie is a devoted member of Red Bank United Methodist Church,
and her capacity for volunteering earned her the nickname "The Energizer Bunny" from her
friends; and

WHEREAS, Maxine McKenzie is the loving mother of her daughter, Marcia, and son,
John, who know her as a selfless and giving caretaker; she is also the grandmother to four
grandchildren and great-grandmother to seven great-grandchildren; and
WHEREAS, as Mrs. McKenzie enters her ninetieth year, she is still an active and
productive member of society who is admired for her wisdom, spirit of generosity, and
enthusiasm for life; and
WHEREAS, we wish to grasp this golden opportunity to specially recognize one of
Tennessee's most outstanding citizens on this very special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby
honor and congratulate Maxine Fields McKenzie on the celebration of her ninetieth birthday and
extend to her our best wishes for many happy returns of the day.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
