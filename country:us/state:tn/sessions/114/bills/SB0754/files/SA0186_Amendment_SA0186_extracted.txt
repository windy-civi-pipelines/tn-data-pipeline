Section 1:
Senate Commerce and Labor 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB0754
Bailey
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting the language "thirty thousand (30,000)" in subdivision (2) of § 6-59-102 in the
amendatory language of Section 1 and substituting instead the language "ten thousand
(10,000)".
AND FURTHER AMEND by adding the following language as a new § 6-59-107 in the
amendatory language of Section 1:
6-59-107. Repeal of chapter.
This chapter is repealed on June 30, 2028.
AND FURTHER AMEND by adding the following language at the end of subdivision (1) in the
amendatory language of Section 2:
Notwithstanding a repeal of the Courthouse Square Revitalization Act, compiled in title 6,
chapter 59, any municipality receiving an allocation of state sales tax revenue on June 1,
2028, pursuant to the Courthouse Square Revitalization Act, must continue to receive
the allocation of revenue. The allocation must equal the amount of revenue derived from
the state tax imposed by this chapter on the sale or use of goods, products, and services
within the courthouse square revitalization zone. For purposes of this subdivision (h)(1),
"courthouse square revitalization zone" has the same meaning provided in § 6-59-102
and consists of the area that is included within the revitalization zone on June 1, 2028.
