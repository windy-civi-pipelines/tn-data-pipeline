SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 341
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to recognize Reverend Richard M. Long on the
occasion of his retirement.
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should honor those individuals who have answered
a divine call to the ministry and who have served with dedication, vigor, and enthusiasm to
perform God's work for their congregations and their communities; and
WHEREAS, Reverend Richard M. Long of Brown Springs Baptist Church and Big
Springs Baptist Church is one such venerable individual, who has inspired the faithful as a
pastor for thirty-seven years; and
WHEREAS, Reverend Long will soon retire from the pulpit as an active pastor, having
evinced an unwavering commitment of service to the people whose spiritual lives he has guided
throughout his vibrant ministry; and
WHEREAS, a reception to honor Reverend Long's many years of devoted service will be
held at Brown Springs Baptist Church in Mosheim on March 30, 2025, from 2:00 pm to 5:00 pm;
and
WHEREAS, Reverend Richard M. Long has demonstrated excellence in his calling as a
man of God, and the members of his congregations are proud to call him their pastor, leader,
and friend; and
WHEREAS, a true shepherd of the flock, Reverend Long has an abiding love for the
ministry; his many positive contributions to his church, his community, and this State are deeply
appreciated, and it is wholly fitting that he be recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Reverend Richard M.
Long on the occasion of his retirement, thank him for his faithful service as pastor of Brown

for much continued success and happiness.
