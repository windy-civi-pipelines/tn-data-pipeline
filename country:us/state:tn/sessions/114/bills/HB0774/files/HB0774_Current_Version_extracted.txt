Section 1:
By Yager
HOUSE BILL 774
By Powers
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 8,
Chapter 36, Part 8, relative to campus police
officers.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 8-36-809(a), is amended by deleting
the language "or sheriff's deputy" and substituting instead the language "sheriff's deputy, or
campus police officer".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 8-36-809(c), is amended by deleting
the subsection and substituting instead:
(c) This section is repealed on June 30, 2027.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
