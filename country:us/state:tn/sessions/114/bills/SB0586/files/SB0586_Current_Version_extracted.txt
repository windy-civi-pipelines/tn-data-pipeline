SENATE BILL 586
By Akbari
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 39,
Chapter 17, relative to unlawful exposure.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-17-318(a)(1), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting:
(1) The image was photographed or recorded under circumstances where the
parties agreed or understood that the image would remain private, regardless of whether
the person who distributes the image was a party to the agreement or understanding;
and

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
