Section 1:
House Commerce 2
Amendment No. 2 to HB0743
Vaughan
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting § 47-18-5803(f) in SECTION 1.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting from § 47-18-5807(a)(1) in SECTION 1 the word
"materially".
AND FURTHER AMEND by inserting the following into § 47-18-5808 in SECTION 1 as a new
subsection (a) and redesignating the existing subsection (a) and remaining subsections
accordingly:
(a) The commissioner may promulgate rules to administer this part, including
rules to establish reasonable fees to be paid by the applicants and licensees for the
expenses of administering this part. The rules must be promulgated in accordance with
the Uniform Administrative Procedures Act, compiled in title 4, chapter 5.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting from § 47-18-5808(c) in SECTION 1 the language
"subsection (a)" and substituting "subsection (b)".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting from § 47-18-5809 in SECTION 1 the language "(b) A
licensee shall maintain a toll-free telecommunications system" and substituting "(d) A licensee
shall maintain a toll-free telecommunications system".
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting from § 47-18-5809 in SECTION 1 the language "(c) A
licensee may extend credit to a consumer" and substituting "(e) A licensee may extend credit to
a consumer".
