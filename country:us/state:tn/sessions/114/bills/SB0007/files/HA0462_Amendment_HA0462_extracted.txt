Section 1:
Amendment No. 2 to HB0073
Howell
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting Section 2 and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 2. The Tennessee historical commission shall complete its review of any and
all relevant information relating to a community development block grant proposed to be issued
to a property meeting the description under Section 1 no later than fifteen (15) business days
after the effective date of this act. If the commission fails to complete its review as required
under this Section 2, the joint government operations committee of the house of representatives
and the senate shall require the commission to appear before the committee to justify its
noncompliance with this Section 2.

Section 3:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
