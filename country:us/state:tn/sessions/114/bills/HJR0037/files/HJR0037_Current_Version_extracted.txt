HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 37
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to provide for the adjournment of the One
Hundred Fourteenth General Assembly
organizational session and to provide for the
convening of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly 2025 regular session.
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that the One Hundred Fourteenth General Assembly organizational session
stand in adjournment at the close of business in both houses on Thursday, January 16, 2025.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that pursuant to Article II, Section 8, of the Constitution of
Tennessee, the One Hundred Fourteenth General Assembly convene in regular session on
Thursday, January 16, 2025, immediately following adjournment of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly organizational session.
