SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 527
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Cosby King, 2025
Valedictorian of Upperman High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pause to pay tribute to those students who,
through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to academic excellence, have
distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Cosby King is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
Valedictorian of the 2025 graduating class at Upperman High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. King has achieved outstanding academic success
and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country; and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Cosby King has earned an exemplary
grade point average and participated in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. King's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for
his future success; and
WHEREAS, his noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for his family,
his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Cosby King's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor the 2025 Upperman
High School Valedictorian, Cosby King, on his impressive academic achievement and extend to
him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.

008039
- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
