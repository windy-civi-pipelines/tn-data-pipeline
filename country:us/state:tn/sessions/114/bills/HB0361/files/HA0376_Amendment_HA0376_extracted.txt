SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 36-3-601(3)(A), is amended by

matters, or a circuit court exercising criminal jurisdiction when the court is hearing a
criminal matter involving a domestic abuse victim, sexual assault victim, or stalking

to child custody, visitation, or support;

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 36-3-601(3)(B), is amended by
deleting "and the general sessions court" and substituting ", a circuit court exercising criminal
jurisdiction when the court is hearing a criminal matter involving a domestic abuse victim, sexual
assault victim, or stalking victim, or the general sessions court"; and by deleting "to the general
sessions court" and substituting "to the criminal court or the general sessions court".

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 36-3-601, is amended by deleting
subdivision (3)(C) and substituting:
(C) "Court," in all other counties, means a court of record with jurisdiction over
domestic relations matters, a circuit court exercising criminal jurisdiction when the court
is hearing a criminal matter involving a domestic abuse victim, sexual assault victim, or
stalking victim, or the general sessions court. This definition does not grant jurisdiction
to the criminal court for matters relating to child custody, visitation, or support;

SECTION 4. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 36-3-601(3)(E), is amended by
