Section 2:
SECTION __. The following appropriations in this act to the specified entities or for the
described purposed are hereby reduced as follows:
(a) $113,458,200 from the Charter School Commission from payroll and
operational budget;
(b) $220,800 from the Charter School Commission for 2 new positions;
(c) $20,000,000 from the increase in the Charter School Facilities Fund;
(d) $12,000,000 from the Sumner County FastTrack project;
(e) $77,200,000 allocated for voucher administration costs;
(f) $500,000 for Teach for America; and
(g) $14,492,000 for EFA.

Section 3:
SECTION __. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $237,871,000 (nonrecurring) to the Highway Fund.
