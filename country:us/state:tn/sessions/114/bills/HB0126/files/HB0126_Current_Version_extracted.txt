Section 1:
By Johnson
HOUSE BILL 126
By Lamberth
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 11,
Chapter 14, Part 1, relative to state natural areas.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-14-108(b)(1)(O), is amended by
deleting "sixteen and five-tenths (16.5) acres" and substituting "forty-one (41) acres".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-14-108(b)(2), is amended by
adding the following as new subdivisions:
( ) Clifty Creek Gorge. A forested area of approximately eighty-nine (89) acres
in Morgan County containing a scenic gorge of Clifty Creek that flows into the Emory
River and protects the federally threatened Virginia spirea (Spiraea virginiana);
( ) Rocky Hill. An approximately one (1) acre site in Rutherford County that
protects the type locality of the state endangered running glade clover (Trifolium
calcaricum) and limestone cedar glade habitat;
( ) Union Grove. An area of approximately fifty-nine (59) acres in Hamblen
County that protects high-quality oak and mixed forest, headwater streams, and the
state endangered Tennessee trillium (Trillium tennesseense), a species discovered new
to science in 2013;
( ) Versailles Knob. A forest of approximately forty (40) acres in Rutherford
County that protects a population of the federally endangered Braun's Rockcress
(Boechera perstellata) with large limestone outcrops that provide habitat for the
rockcress;

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-14-108(b)(2)(B), is amended by
deleting "Barnett’s Woods" and substituting "Barnett's Woods and Prairie" and by deleting "forty
(40) acre site" and substituting " site of one hundred fifty-six (156) acres".

Section 5:
SECTION 4. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-14-108(b)(2)(G), is amended by
deleting "two thousand six hundred ninety (2,690) acres" and substituting "two thousand seven
hundred fifty-four (2,754) acres".

Section 6:
SECTION 5. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-14-108(b)(2)(I), is amended by
deleting "one hundred forty (140) acre site" and substituting "site of six hundred twenty-seven
(627) acres".

Section 7:
SECTION 6. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-14-108(b)(2)(P), is amended by
deleting "eight hundred forty-six (846) acres" and substituting "nine hundred twenty-eight (928)
acres".

Section 8:
SECTION 7. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-14-108(b)(2)(S), is amended by
deleting "two hundred forty-nine (249) acre site" and substituting "site of two hundred seventy-
three (273) acres".

Section 9:
SECTION 8. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-14-108(b)(2)(NN), is amended by
deleting "three thousand (3,000) acres" and substituting "three thousand six hundred (3,600)
acres".

Section 10:
SECTION 9. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-14-108(b)(2)(PP), is amended by
deleting "one thousand three hundred sixty-seven (1,367) acre area" and substituting "area of
one thousand three hundred eighty-eight (1,388) acres".

Section 11:
SECTION 10. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 11-14-108(b)(2)(LLL), is amended
by deleting "four hundred twenty-eight (428) acres" and substituting "four hundred seventy-six
(476) acres".

Section 12:
SECTION 11. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
