HOUSE RESOLUTION 87
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Katherine Hunt, the
2024-2025 Lipscomb Elementary School and
Williamson County Schools Elementary School
Teacher of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should specially recognize those outstanding teachers
who have devoted their careers to providing the highest-quality education for Tennessee's
students; and
WHEREAS, Katherine Hunt is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Hunt was recently selected as the 2024-2025 Lipscomb Elementary
School Teacher of the Year and Williamson County Schools Elementary School Teacher of the
Year, honors most befitting her outstanding tenure as a teacher, as they recognize the positive
influence she has on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Hunt is an extremely talented and compassionate educator who works
assiduously to motivate her students to reach their full potential, both as scholars and as
citizens; and
WHEREAS, she is truly an inspiration to her colleagues and students alike, and her
friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Katherine Hunt epitomizes the ideal of the dedicated teacher who is wholly
committed to the highest values of public service and quality education for all, and she should
be specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
congratulate Katherine Hunt on being named the 2024-2025 Lipscomb Elementary School and

service to the children of Tennessee, salute her professional expertise and personal excellence,
and wish her all the best in her future endeavors.

for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
