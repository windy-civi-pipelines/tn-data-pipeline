Amendment No. 1 to HB0731
Crawford
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 8-44-102(b)(1), is amended by
adding the following as a new subdivision:
(F) The governing body of Tennessee One Health, that seeks to promote and
support the health of humans, animals, and the environment through cooperation and
collaboration across disciplines, including local, state, and federal agencies, academic
institutions, and outside partners.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 8-44-102(b)(2), is amended by
designating the existing language as subdivision (b)(2)(A) and adding the following as a new
subdivision (b)(2)(B):
(B) "Meeting" also means the convening of a governing body described under
subdivision (b)(1)(F).

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
