HOUSE RESOLUTION 139
By Parkinson
A RESOLUTION to recognize and congratulate Khadajah
Kimbrough, 2025-2026 Miss LeMoyne-Owen
College.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should specially recognize those emerging community
leaders who strive to improve the quality of life for their fellow citizens while bringing beauty,
grace, and purpose to all their endeavors; and
WHEREAS, one such superlative individual is Khadajah Kimbrough, who was recently
named Miss LeMoyne-Owen College for the 2025-2026 academic year, a most befitting honor,
as it recognizes her dedication to excellence; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Kimbrough, a senior biology major at LeMoyne-Owen College, is the
seventy-seventh Miss LeMoyne-Owen College; and
WHEREAS, her platform initiative for her reign, "The R.O.S.E. That Grew From
Concrete," seeks to ensure that every student on campus has a voice and is permitted to
express ideas for the greater good of LeMoyne-Owen College; and
WHEREAS, Khadajah Kimbrough also aims to bridge the gap of student and staff
connection on campus, while emphasizing mental health awareness; her plan is to enhance
academic enrichment while also cultivating a more hands-on environment in every aspect of
campus life; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Kimbrough is an exceptional role model and campus leader, and it is
fitting that we recognize her worthy reign; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we commend
and congratulate Khadajah Kimbrough, the seventy-seventh Miss LeMoyne-Owen College,

endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
