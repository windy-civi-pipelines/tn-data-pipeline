Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 67-5-1005(a)(1), is amended by
adding the following language immediately after the third sentence of the subdivision:
Property that is qualified as agricultural land, owned by two (2) individuals, and held
under the title, tenancy by the entireties, or the title, joint tenancy, is deemed to have
unchanged ownership upon the death or divorce of an owner if the property is retained
by the other owner through a right of survivorship and the property continues to meet
minimum size requirements and other criteria necessary to qualify as agricultural land
under this part.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 67-5-1006(a)(1), is amended by
adding the following language immediately after the third sentence of the subdivision:
Property that is qualified as forest land, owned by two (2) individuals, and held under the
title, tenancy by the entireties, or the title, joint tenancy, is deemed to have unchanged
ownership upon the death or divorce of an owner if the property is retained by the other
owner through a right of survivorship and the property continues to meet minimum size
requirements and other criteria necessary to qualify as forest land under this part.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 67-5-1007(b)(1), is amended by
adding the following language immediately after the third sentence of the subdivision:
Property that is qualified as open space land, owned by two (2) individuals, and held
under the title, tenancy by the entireties, or the title, joint tenancy, is deemed to have
unchanged ownership upon the death or divorce of an owner if the property is retained
