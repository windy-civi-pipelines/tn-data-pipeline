HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 605
By Boyd
A RESOLUTION to recognize and commend Chris Jones for his
service on the Tennessee Nuclear Energy
Advisory Council and his contributions to the
advancement of energy in Tennessee.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should recognize those individuals who have
demonstrated outstanding leadership and dedication to the advancement of energy and
economic development in Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Chris Jones, president and chief executive officer of Middle Tennessee
Electric (MTE), is one such individual who has made significant contributions to the energy
sector through his leadership and expertise; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Jones has served as the fifth CEO in MTE's eighty-five-year history,
leading the cooperative in providing reliable electric service to more than 325,000 members
across eleven counties in Middle Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, a strong advocate for innovation in the energy sector, Chris Jones has been
a key leader in advancing Tennessee's energy infrastructure, working closely with government
and regulatory agencies, including the Tennessee Valley Authority, to ensure sustainable and
resilient energy solutions for Tennesseans; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Jones has demonstrated exceptional service to the State of Tennessee
as a valued member of the Tennessee Nuclear Energy Advisory Council, providing critical
insight into the role of nuclear energy in Tennessee's future energy portfolio and helping to
position the state as a leader in nuclear innovation and development; and
WHEREAS, in addition to his leadership in the energy sector, Chris Jones has devoted
significant time and effort to serving the broader community, holding leadership roles with the
United Way of Rutherford and Cannon Counties, the Tennessee Chamber of Commerce, the

Rutherford County Chamber of Commerce, and the Williamson County Chamber of Commerce,
among others; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Jones is a graduate of the University of Tennessee with a degree in
communications and has continually demonstrated his commitment to collaboration and
strategic leadership throughout his career; and
WHEREAS, he resides in Murfreesboro with his wife, Bonita, a retired teacher, and they
are the proud parents of two daughters, Cayln and Chrisln; and
WHEREAS, Chris Jones is a devoted member of Forest Mill Baptist Church in
Manchester, where he serves as a deacon, further exemplifying his commitment to faith,
service, and community; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should recognize and honor Chris Jones for his
exemplary leadership, public service, and dedication to advancing Tennessee's energy future;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we recognize and commend Chris Jones for his outstanding service on the
Tennessee Nuclear Energy Advisory Council, his distinguished leadership in the energy sector,
and his unwavering commitment to the people of Tennessee.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
