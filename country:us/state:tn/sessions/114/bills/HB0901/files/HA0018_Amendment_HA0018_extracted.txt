Section 2:
SECTION __. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 43, Chapter 25, is amended by adding
the following new section:
The department of agriculture, in conjunction with the Center for Historic
Preservation at Middle Tennessee State University, is directed to create, and make
available, signage designating century farms owned by African Americans.
AND FURTHER AMEND by deleting "and" at the end of subdivision (e)(3) in SECTION 1,
deleting "." and substituting "; and" at the end of subdivision (e)(4), and by adding the following
as a new subdivision to subsection (e) in SECTION 1:
(5) In conjunction with the Center for Historic Preservation at Middle Tennessee
State University, create, and make available, signage designating century farms owned
by African Americans.
