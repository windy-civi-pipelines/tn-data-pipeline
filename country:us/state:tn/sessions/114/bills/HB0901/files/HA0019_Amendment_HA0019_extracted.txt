Section 3:
SECTION __. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 43, Chapter 25, is amended by adding
the following new section:
(a) The department of agriculture is directed to perform a study on the status of
century farms in this state, their continued viability, and their impact on farming
generally. The study must include, but not be limited to, examinations of:
(1) The financial impact on century farm families resulting from their
operation of such farms;
(2) The short-term and long-term projections on the continued viability of
century farms given:
(A) The effect of current economic conditions on such farms;
(B) The demand for agricultural products grown and produced by
century farms in this state; and
(C) Long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns on
century farms; and
(3) Any other barriers encountered by century farm families from all
economic strata, and proposed solutions to such barriers.

(b) It is the legislative intent that this study be conducted within the department

(c) On or before January 31, 2026, the department shall report its findings and
