Section 1:
By Bailey
HOUSE BILL 1078
By Kumar
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 8;
Title 56; Title 63; Title 68 and Title 71, relative to
insurance.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 56-2-125(f)(1), is amended by
deleting subdivision (A) and substituting:
(A) Each month, a group health plan or health insurance issuer shall provide
electronic health insurance claims data for state residents to the commissioner or a
designated entity authorized by the commissioner, in accordance with standards and
procedures recommended by the Tennessee health information committee pursuant to
subdivision (c)(2) and adopted by the commissioner by rule.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
