Section 1:
By Lowe
HOUSE BILL 23
By Davis
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 2 and
Title 6, Chapter 53, relative to election districts.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 6-53-110, is amended by adding the
following new subsection:
( ) As used in this section, "district" means a district, region, precinct, sector,
territory, or other geographical area prescribed for the purposes of nominating and
electing candidates in accordance with this section.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
