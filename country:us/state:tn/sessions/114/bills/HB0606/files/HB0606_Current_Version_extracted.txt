HOUSE BILL 606
By McCalmon
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 57,
relative to alcoholic beverages.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 57-1-206(a), is amended by deleting
"the members of the general assembly" and substituting "the governor and the members of the
general assembly".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.

SENATE BILL 726

HOUSE BILL 606
By McCalmon
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 57,
relative to alcoholic beverages.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 57-1-206(a), is amended by deleting
"the members of the general assembly" and substituting "the governor and the members of the
general assembly".
