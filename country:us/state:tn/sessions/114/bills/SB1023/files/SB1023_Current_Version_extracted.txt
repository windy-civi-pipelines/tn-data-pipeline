AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49,
relative to education.

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-3024(d), is amended by
deleting the subsection.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-3024(e), is amended by
deleting "After submission of the report required in subsection (d), the department" and
substituting "The department".

SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
