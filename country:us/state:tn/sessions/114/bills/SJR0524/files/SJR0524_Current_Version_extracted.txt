SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 524
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Logan Montgomery,
2025 Salutatorian of Monterey High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Logan Montgomery is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named Salutatorian of the 2025 graduating class at Monterey High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Montgomery has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Logan Montgomery has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Montgomery's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes
well for his future success; and
WHEREAS, Logan Montgomery's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of
pride for his family, his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Montgomery's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the
respect, admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor the 2025 Monterey
High School Salutatorian, Logan Montgomery, on his impressive academic achievement and
extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
SJR0524
008036
- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
