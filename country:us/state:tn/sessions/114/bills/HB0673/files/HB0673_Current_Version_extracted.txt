Section 1:
By Akbari
HOUSE BILL 673
By Love
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 9 and
Title 49, relative to land grant institutions.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1.
The Tennessee higher education commission, in consultation with the
department of finance and administration, shall create and submit a report to the
members of the general assembly detailing the state appropriations made to each land
grant institution in this state from 1987 to 2020, on a per pupil basis. The report must be
submitted to the members of the general assembly by December 31, 2025.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
