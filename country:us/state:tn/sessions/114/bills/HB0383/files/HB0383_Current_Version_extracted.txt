Section 1:
By Yager
HOUSE BILL 383
By Butler
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4;
Title 63 and Title 68, relative to reportable
diseases.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. On or before July 1, 2025, the commissioner of health shall add alpha-gal
syndrome to the department of health's published list of reportable diseases and conditions, the
occurrence of which must be reported to the department by a healthcare provider pursuant to
administrative rule.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
