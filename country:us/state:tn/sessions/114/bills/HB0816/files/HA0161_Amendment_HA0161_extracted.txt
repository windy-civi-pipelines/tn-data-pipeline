Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-50-504, is amended by adding
the following as a new subsection:
(k) Not more than four (4) points shall be assessed to a person's driver record
for the offense of operating a motor vehicle while the person's driving privilege is
cancelled, suspended, or revoked.

Section 3:
