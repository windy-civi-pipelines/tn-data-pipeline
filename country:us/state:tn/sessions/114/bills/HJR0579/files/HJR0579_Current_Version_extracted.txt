HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 579
By Farmer
A RESOLUTION to commend Alexis Bartlett for exemplary service
as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Alexis Bartlett is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in her
role as a legislative intern for Representative Andrew Farmer and the House Judiciary
Committee; her work has resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and
the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Bartlett also lent her time and talents to the House Criminal Justice
Subcommittee, the House Children and Family Affairs Subcommittee, and the House Civil
Justice Subcommittee; and
WHEREAS, a student at Tennessee State University (TSU), where she is highly
regarded by her peers, Ms. Bartlett has achieved academic success while working toward a
Bachelor of Science degree in Political Science and Government, with a minor in history and
concentration in law, and volunteering with Fish & Loaves Ministry and Girl Scouts of America;
and
WHEREAS, she is a member of the TSU Honors College, an event coordinator for the
TSU chapter of Delight Ministries, and a representative in the Tennessee Intercollegiate Student
Legislature (TISL), receiving the Carlisle Award during the 54th TISL General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Alexis Bartlett brought her many

abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2025 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2025 legislative session, Ms. Bartlett has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Alexis Bartlett has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Alexis Bartlett for outstanding service as a legislative
intern for Representative Andrew Farmer and the House Judiciary Committee and extend to her
our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
