SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 507
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Charisse Carter, 2024–
2025 Springdale Elementary School Teacher of
the Year and Wilson County Schools Teacher of
the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should specially recognize those outstanding teachers
who have devoted their careers to providing the highest-quality education for Tennessee's
students; and
WHEREAS, Charisse Carter is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Carter has been named the 2024–2025 Teacher of the Year at
Springdale Elementary School, as well as the Wilson County Schools Teacher of the Year, most
befitting honors that recognize the positive influence she has on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, an educator for ten years, Charisse Carter serves as a K-5th grade ESL
(English as a second language) instructor at Springdale Elementary; she holds a bachelor's
degree in accounting, a master's degree in curriculum and instruction, and a master's degree in
accounting with an emphasis in information systems; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Carter serves as an inspiration to her colleagues and students alike,
and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Charisse Carter truly epitomizes the dedicated teacher who is wholly
committed to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be
specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Charisse Carter on
SJR0507
007980
- 1 -
being chosen as the 2024–2025 Springdale Elementary School Teacher of the Year and Wilson
County Schools Teacher of the Year, commend her exceptional service to the children of
Tennessee, salute her professional expertise and personal excellence, and wish her all the best
in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
