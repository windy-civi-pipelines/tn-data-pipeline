HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 316
By Stevens
A RESOLUTION to recognize and congratulate Murfreesboro
Anesthesia Group upon its receipt of the
Physician Champion Award from the Ascension
Saint Thomas Rutherford Foundation.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should acknowledge
and commend those estimable Tennessee businesses that are dedicated to serving the needs
of their communities; and
WHEREAS, one such business is Murfreesboro Anesthesia Group, which was recently
named a 2025 Rutherford Society Gala honoree and the recipient of the Physician Champion
Award from the Ascension Saint Thomas Rutherford Foundation, an honor most befitting the
group's many years of service to the Rutherford County community; and
WHEREAS, the Ascension Saint Thomas Rutherford Foundation will host its eighteenth
annual Rutherford Society Gala on March 29, 2025, at the Stones River Country Club; the event
will feature keynote speaker Inky Johnson, a former University of Tennessee football player
turned motivational speaker, entrepreneur, and author; and
WHEREAS, the Rutherford Society Gala celebrates philanthropic excellence and
recognizes donors who have contributed $1,000 or more to the foundation; and
WHEREAS, founded in 1971, Murfreesboro Anesthesia Group (MAG) provides top-tier
anesthesia services to hospitals and medical centers throughout Rutherford County; and
WHEREAS, established by Dr. Fred Lovelace and Dr. Kenneth Hunt at Rutherford
Hospital, MAG has since grown to include thirteen anesthesiologists, forty-six certified
registered nurse anesthetists, and nine office staff; the group now supports more than 20,000
cases annually, including 4,000 labor and delivery epidurals and 7,000 regional nerve blocks;
and

WHEREAS, MAG has been a key partner in advancing Ascension Saint Thomas's
cardiac surgery program, enhancing patient care and heart health outcomes in the community;
and
WHEREAS, known for pioneering ultrasound-guided regional nerve blocks in Middle
Tennessee, MAG earned the Ascension Saint Thomas Rutherford Foundation Physician
Champion Award thanks to its commitment to innovation and patient safety; and
WHEREAS, MAG's ongoing efforts to raise the standard of care in Rutherford County
highlight the significant role it plays in the healthcare community; and
WHEREAS, the anesthesiologists, anesthetists, and staff members of MAG have
worked diligently to better the lives of their fellow citizens, and they are most worthy of our
approbation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we recognize and congratulate Murfreesboro Anesthesia Group upon its
receipt of the Physician Champion Award from the Ascension Saint Thomas Rutherford
Foundation, extending our best wishes for the group's continued success in its future
endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
