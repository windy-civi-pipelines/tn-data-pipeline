HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 424
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Joah Wilhite, 2025 Top
Ten Senior of Mt. Pleasant High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Joah Wilhite is one such exceptional student, who was recently named a
Top Ten Senior of the 2025 graduating class at Mt. Pleasant High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Wilhite has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Joah Wilhite has earned an exemplary
grade point average of 96.45 while participating in numerous extracurricular activities, including
playing percussion with the high school band and being a member of a NASA project team; and
WHEREAS, apart from his scholastic endeavors, Mr. Wilhite enjoys writing and
engineering, interests that will serve him well as he moves forward; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Wilhite's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for his future success; and
WHEREAS, his noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for his family,
his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Joah Wilhite's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
HJR0424
004858
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Joah Wilhite on being named a 2025 Mt.
Pleasant High School Top Ten Senior, salute his impressive academic achievement, and
extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 004858
