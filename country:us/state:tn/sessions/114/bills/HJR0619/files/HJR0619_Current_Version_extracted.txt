HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 619
By Grills
A RESOLUTION to recognize The I AM Fitness Training and its
founder, Bradley Vaughn.
WHEREAS, born and raised in Dyersburg, Bradley Vaughn has devoted himself to the
service of others, drawing on his deep faith and compassion to improve the lives of those in his
hometown; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Vaughn transformed his personal belief in helping others into action by
founding The I AM Fitness Training in Dyersburg, establishing a faith-based training facility that
serves as both a gym and a ministry to uplift members of the community; and
WHEREAS, a certified personal trainer accredited by the American Council on Exercise
(ACE), Bradley Vaughn has demonstrated a steadfast commitment to both physical fitness and
spiritual wellness in his community, reflecting his belief that nurturing the body and the spirit
together can transform lives; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Vaughn built The I AM Fitness Training on the principles of acceptance
and love, creating a judgment-free environment that respects every individual's journey; people
from all walks of life are welcomed through its doors with understanding and encouragement;
and
WHEREAS, through this ministry of fitness, Bradley Vaughn and his team offer a broad
range of programs such as boxing, kickboxing, strength and circuit training, speed and agility
workouts, dance classes, and youth fitness classes, ensuring that individuals of all ages,
backgrounds, and ability levels can work toward better health and self-confidence in a
supportive atmosphere; and
HJR0619
007110
- 1 -
WHEREAS, through its inclusive programs and faith-based approach, The I AM Fitness
Training has encouraged countless individuals to improve their health, gain confidence, and find
hope; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we recognize The I AM Fitness Training and its founder, Bradley Vaughn,
for their exemplary contributions to the Dyersburg community and this State, commend their
commitment to promoting inclusive fitness and spiritual well-being, and extend to them our best
wishes for much continued success in their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
