Section 1:
By Lamar
HOUSE BILL 1165
By Parkinson
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 39
and Title 40, relative to criminal sentencing.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 40-35-114, is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision:
( ) The defendant was an adult at the time of the offense and committed the
offense with a minor, regardless of whether the minor was convicted of the offense;

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it, and
applies to offenses committed on or after that date.
