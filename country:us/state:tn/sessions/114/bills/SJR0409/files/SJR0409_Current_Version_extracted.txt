SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 409
By Massey
A RESOLUTION to congratulate South Knox Elementary School
upon being named a 2024 National ESEA
Distinguished School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pay tribute to
those educational institutions that, through the natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence evidenced by their administration, faculty, staff, and students, have earned
national accolades; and
WHEREAS, South Knox Elementary School in Knox County is one such exemplary
institution, which was recently recognized by the United States Department of Education as a
2024 National ESEA Distinguished School; and
WHEREAS, since 1996, the National ESEA Distinguished School program has honored
those public schools that demonstrate outstanding academic achievement by their students and
highlight the efforts of those schools that make significant improvements in one of three
categories: exceptional student performance, closing the achievement gap between student
groups, and excellence in serving special populations of students; and
WHEREAS, South Knox Elementary School was one of just two schools in Tennessee
and among only 100 in the nation to receive this illustrious award, which places it among the top
schools in the United States; and
WHEREAS, the administration, faculty, and staff of South Knox Elementary School have
built an environment at their school that ensures students have the very best atmosphere for
learning; and

WHEREAS, their school's selection as a National ESEA Distinguished School is a
tremendous source of pride for the administration, teachers, students, and parents of the South
Knox Elementary School community; and
WHEREAS, the dedication to academic excellence demonstrated by all the fine
individuals affiliated with South Knox Elementary School is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we congratulate South Knox Elementary School on
being named a 2024 National ESEA Distinguished School by the U.S. Department of Education;
salute the school's administration, faculty, staff, and students on this prestigious award; and
extend to them our best wishes for much continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
