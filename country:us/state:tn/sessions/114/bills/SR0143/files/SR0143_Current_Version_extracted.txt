SENATE RESOLUTION 143
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to honor the Arc of Cumberland County on its
fiftieth anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pay tribute to
those exemplary organizations and their remarkable personnel who work with purpose and
conviction to improve the quality of life in their respective communities; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable organization is the Arc of Cumberland County, which is
commemorating its fiftieth anniversary this year, marking five decades of providing the highest
quality community and support to those with mental disabilities; and
WHEREAS, founded in 1975, the Arc of Cumberland County currently has about 200
members and is a local organ of the nation's largest volunteer organization solely devoted to
improving the quality of life for children and adults experiencing some form of intellectual
disability and their families; and
WHEREAS, the members of the group dedicate themselves to providing education,
training, employment and housing assistance, and other opportunities to more than 7.2 million
Americans with intellectual disabilities, in addition to advocating for the consistent, ubiquitous
recognition and application of their inalienable rights; and
WHEREAS, Arc also helps to educate its community on how to prevent disabilities in
infants and young children, providing a bulwark of knowledge against threats of preventable
damage to the most vulnerable; and
WHEREAS, the Arc of Cumberland County, first organized in Crossville, is almost
entirely operated by its base of members and volunteers, each of whom is a shining example of
community involvement, enrichment, and altruism; and

WHEREAS, the devoted members of the Arc of Cumberland County have served our
State for five decades, and we are privileged to recognize this esteemed organization for its
countless contributions to the well-being of the citizens of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we commemorate the fiftieth
anniversary of the Arc of Cumberland County and salute its members and personnel for their
ongoing commitment to ensuring a high quality of life for those with intellectual disabilities in
Cumberland County.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
