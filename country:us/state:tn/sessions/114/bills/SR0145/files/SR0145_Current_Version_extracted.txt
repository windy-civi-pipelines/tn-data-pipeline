SENATE RESOLUTION 145
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to commend Wayne Elam for his honorable and
astute service as a member of the McNairy
County Election Commission.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to
specially recognize those estimable public servants who have dedicated themselves to
improving the quality of life for their fellow citizens; and
WHEREAS, Wayne Elam is one such estimable person who evinces the greatest
integrity and probity in all his chosen endeavors; and
WHEREAS, having served with distinction for fifty years as a member of the McNairy
County Election Commission, Mr. Elam has taken his well-deserved retirement from that
esteemed body; and
WHEREAS, throughout his five decades of service, Wayne Elam witnessed and
contributed to the modernization of the voting process in McNairy County, transitioning from
paper ballots to computerized systems that ensure accuracy and integrity in each election; and
WHEREAS, known for his steadfast adherence to procedure and policy, Mr. Elam
advised new members to read and follow the municipal election manual diligently, a practice he
credited with guiding his successful service; and
WHEREAS, his leadership and dedication have been recognized and appreciated by his
colleagues, including McNairy County Election Administrator Joanie Collins, who described him
as a strong and steadfast leader and a pleasure to work alongside; and
WHEREAS, Wayne Elam has further distinguished himself by helping to appoint honest
and capable individuals to ensure the smooth conduct of elections; and
WHEREAS, throughout his service on the McNairy County Election Commission, Mr.
Elam has shown unwavering dedication as a public-spirited citizen of the highest order and as
an exceptional asset to his community; and

WHEREAS, Wayne Elam is wholly committed to the noble precepts of public service that
have earned Tennessee recognition as the Volunteer State, and he should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby commend Wayne
Elam for his honorable and astute service to the good people of Tennessee as a member of the
McNairy County Election Commission and salute his unswerving commitment to living the
examined life with courage and conviction.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy
