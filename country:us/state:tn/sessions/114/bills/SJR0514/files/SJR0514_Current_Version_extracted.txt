SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 514
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Chloey Marshall for her
outstanding achievement in the Baileyton
Veterans Memorial design contest.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly should recognize and celebrate the
accomplishments of young Tennesseans who demonstrate exceptional creativity, civic spirit,
and artistic talent; and
WHEREAS, Chloey Marshall, a student at North Greene Middle School, distinguished
herself as the second-place winner in the Baileyton Veterans Memorial sign design contest, a
competition that drew an impressive 147 entries from her peers; and
WHEREAS, the contest invited students to design a new road sign for the Baileyton
Veterans Memorial, which honors the service and sacrifice of our nation's veterans; and
WHEREAS, Miss Marshall's artistic contribution placed her design among the top three
chosen by the Town of Baileyton and reflected her talent, thoughtfulness, and respect for those
who have served; and
WHEREAS, Chloey Marshall's recognition came during a meeting of the Baileyton Board
of Mayor and Aldermen at Town Hall, where she stood proudly alongside her fellow honorees,
Lily Brake and Ellieonna Stetson, who received first and third place respectively; and
WHEREAS, Miss Marshall's achievement is a great source of pride for her family, her
school, and her community, and it is most appropriate that she be specially honored by the
members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Chloey Marshall for

creativity and civic engagement, and extend to her our best wishes for every success in all
