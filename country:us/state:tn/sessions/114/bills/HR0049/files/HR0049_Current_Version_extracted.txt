HOUSE RESOLUTION 49
By Hill
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Faith Roberts upon
her receipt of the 2025 Good Neighbor Award
from the Gamma Mu chapter of Delta Kappa
Gamma.
WHEREAS, we take great pleasure in recognizing those young Tennesseans who have
demonstrated outstanding qualities that are not measured by standardized tests or athletic
ability but instead by the kindness they exhibit to others; and
WHEREAS, these fine young people are notable for their compassion, their
selflessness, their solid work ethic, their willingness to help others, and their generosity of spirit;
and
WHEREAS, Faith Roberts, a student at Mountain City Elementary School, is always
willing to help and lift others up; and
WHEREAS, Faith works with younger students and, in turn, is adored by them; and
WHEREAS, Faith Roberts is being honored with the Good Neighbor Award by the
Gamma Mu chapter of Delta Kappa Gamma, an organization of women educators who strive to
promote excellence in education around the world; and
WHEREAS, the Gamma Mu chapter in Johnson City created the Good Neighbor Award
to recognize and affirm students such as Faith who have attained maturity beyond their years in
following the path of good citizenship; and
WHEREAS, Faith Roberts is, without a doubt, a role model and future leader of her
community, this State, and our nation, and she is most worthy of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and

chapter of Delta Kappa Gamma, salute her ongoing commitment to civility, compassion, and

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
