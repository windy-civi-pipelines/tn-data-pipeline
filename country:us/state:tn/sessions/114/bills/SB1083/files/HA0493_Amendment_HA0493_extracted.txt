Section 1:
Amendment No. 10 to HB0622
Powell
Signature of Sponsor
by adding the following as a new subsection (e) in SECTION 2:
(e) On January 1, 2026, and each year thereafter, each county government shall
provide a report to the general assembly detailing the demographic characteristics of the county
government’s employees. The report may be provided electronically.
AND FURTHER AMEND by adding the following as a new subsection (e) in SECTION 3:
(e) On January 1, 2026, and each year thereafter, each municipal government shall
provide a report to the general assembly detailing the demographic characteristics of the
municipal government’s employees. The report may be provided electronically.
AND FURTHER AMEND by adding the following as a new subsection (e) in SECTION 4:
(e) On January 1, 2026, and each year thereafter, each metropolitan government shall
provide a report to the general assembly detailing the demographic characteristics of the
metropolitan government’s employees. The report may be provided electronically.
AND FURTHER AMEND by adding the following as a new subsection (e) in SECTION 5:
(e) On January 1, 2026, and each year thereafter, each LEA shall provide a report to
the general assembly detailing the demographic characteristics of the LEA’s employees. The
report may be provided electronically.
AND FURTHER AMEND by adding the following as a new subsection (e) in SECTION 6:
(e) On January 1, 2026, and each year thereafter, each public institution of higher
education shall provide a report to the general assembly detailing the demographic
characteristics of the public institution of higher education’s employees. The report may be
provided electronically.
