HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 624
By Terry
A RESOLUTION to commend Maverick Emberton for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Maverick Emberton is one such individual who has distinguished himself in
his role as a legislative intern for Representative Bryan Terry; Mr. Emberton's work has resulted
in innumerable benefits for the legislators he assisted and the General Assembly as a whole;
and
WHEREAS, a student at Tennessee Technological University, where he is highly
regarded by his peers, Maverick Emberton has achieved academic success while majoring in
political science and participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Emberton is a member of Pi Sigma Alpha, Pre-Law Club, History Club,
and the Overton County Republican Party; he serves as the president of the College
Republicans at Tennessee Technological University, and he has also served as a campaign
worker for Senator Ken Yager; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize himself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further his studies, Maverick Emberton brought his many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2025 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2025 legislative session, Mr. Emberton has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Maverick Emberton has performed his many duties with extraordinary
insight and rare decorum; now, therefore,
HJR0624
007267
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Maverick Emberton for outstanding service as a
legislative intern for Representative Bryan Terry and extend to him our best wishes for every
future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 007267
