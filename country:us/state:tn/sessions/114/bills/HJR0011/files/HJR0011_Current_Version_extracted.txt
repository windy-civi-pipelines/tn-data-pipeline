HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 11
By Moon
A RESOLUTION to recognize Lisa Nagy on the occasion of her
retirement.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should specially
recognize those citizens who have performed with uncommon devotion and enthusiasm
throughout their years of public service; and
WHEREAS, Lisa Nagy is one such estimable person, who has demonstrated the
greatest dedication, ability, and integrity in all her chosen endeavors; and
WHEREAS, having distinguished herself as a public-spirited citizen of the highest order
throughout her exemplary tenure as administrative assistant of the Alcoa Police Department,
Ms. Nagy is taking her much-deserved retirement; and
WHEREAS, Lisa Nagy began her career with the Alcoa Police Department as a records
clerk/secretary in 1997 and rose through the ranks to administrative assistant in 2000,
approaching her duties in a professional manner and using her innate ability to work with
individuals from all walks of life to help others; and
WHEREAS, in her position, Ms. Nagy was an instrumental part of achieving the
department's first accreditation award from the Commission on Accreditation for Law
Enforcement Agencies in 2002; and
WHEREAS, in 2005, Ms. Nagy achieved designation as a Certified Administrative
Professional from the International Association of Administrative Professionals (IAAP), and her
involvement with the organization led to her nomination and selection as Administrative
Professional of the Year in 2007, a most befitting honor that recognized her dedication to her
profession; and

WHEREAS, additionally, Lisa Nagy was chosen as the 2009 Administrative Professional
of the Year and the 2011 Member of the Year for the local IAAP; and
WHEREAS, a highly respected individual in her profession, Ms. Nagy has attended
numerous trainings and achieved her Certified Administrative Professional rating in 2005,
adding a specialty in organizational management credential in 2007; and
WHEREAS, prior to her position with the APD, Ms. Nagy devoted her time and talents to
several other governmental agencies within the State of Tennessee; she served the Tennessee
Bureau of Investigation's Information Enforcement System as an assistant terminal agency
coordinator before being promoted to a terminal agency coordinator and the Law Enforcement
Accreditation Coalition of Tennessee as a secretary/treasurer; and
WHEREAS, Lisa Nagy exemplifies the spirit and allegiance to family and community that
are characteristic of a true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, throughout her years of public service, Ms. Nagy demonstrated the utmost
professionalism, ability, and integrity, winning the sincere respect and admiration of her
colleagues and a grateful public; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend Lisa Nagy upon the occasion of her
retirement, extending our gratitude for her many years of dedicated service to the Alcoa Police
Department and our best wishes for success in all her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
