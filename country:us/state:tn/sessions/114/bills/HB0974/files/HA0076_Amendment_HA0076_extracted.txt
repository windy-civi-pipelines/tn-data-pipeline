Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-17-120, is amended by adding
the following as a new subsection:
( )
(1) Notwithstanding this part, a motor vehicle dealer license is not
required to sell or purchase a previously titled antique vehicle that is at least
twenty-five (25) years of age or a unique motor vehicle through an auction format
if:
(A) The auction is being held at a venue with a permanent seating
capacity of at least twenty thousand (20,000), located in a county with a
population of not less than one hundred forty-seven thousand seven
hundred (147,700) and not more than one hundred forty-seven thousand
eight hundred (147,800), according to the 2020 federal census or any
subsequent census;
(B) The auction operator conducts no more than one (1) auction
in this state per year;
(C) The auction offers no less than four hundred (400) vehicles
and no more than two thousand (2,000) vehicles for any one (1) auction;
and
(D) The auction operator has a minimum net worth of twenty-five
million dollars ($25,000,000).
