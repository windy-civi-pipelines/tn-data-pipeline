SENATE RESOLUTION 94
By Oliver
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Edward Knauss on his
selection as the 2024–2025 The Academy at
Hickory Hollow Teacher of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest-quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Edward Knauss is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished
himself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Edward Knauss has recently been named the 2024–2025 The Academy at
Hickory Hollow Teacher of the Year, a most befitting honor that recognizes the positive
influence he has on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Knauss is an extremely talented and compassionate educator who
works assiduously to motivate his students to reach their full potential, both as scholars and as
citizens; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Knauss serves as an inspiration to his colleagues and students alike,
and his friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to know him; and
WHEREAS, Edward Knauss truly epitomizes the dedicated teacher who is wholly
committed to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and he should be
specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby honor and
congratulate Edward Knauss on being chosen as the 2024–2025 The Academy at Hickory
Hollow Teacher of the Year, commend his exceptional service to the children of Tennessee,
