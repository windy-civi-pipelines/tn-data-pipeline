HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 385
By Grills
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the Dyer County Rescue
Squad on their fiftieth anniversary.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should salute the
men and women of rescue squads who, in the course of discharging their vital public duties,
provide services that dramatically improve outcomes for individuals who are seriously injured or
in peril; and
WHEREAS, one such valorous group is the Dyer County Rescue Squad, which has a
staff of the highest quality, skill, and dedication, and is celebrating its fiftieth anniversary of
public service this year; and
WHEREAS, equipped with a wide spectrum of skills and knowledge for manifold
emergencies, the Dyer County Rescue Squad is trained in vehicle extrication, land search and
rescue, water search and recovery, weather operations, traffic control, community outreach, and
other technical rescue services; in a secondary capacity, they assist with disaster relief and
support operations for the Dyer County Emergency Management Agency, Emergency Medical
Services, Tennessee Highway Patrol, Dyer County Sheriff's Office, local fire departments, and
Tennessee Association of Rescue Squads; and
WHEREAS, the Dyer County Rescue Squad is an all-volunteer, nonprofit organization
and member of the Tennessee Association of Rescue Squads that is staffed entirely by Dyer
County residents, and their team represents a wide cross section of careers and backgrounds,
including factory and construction workers, emergency personnel, and office workers; and
WHEREAS, in addition to their primary purpose of emergency aid, they also dedicate
time to assist in vehicle maintenance, education, finance, fundraising, community outreach, and

grant writing, and the team is always seeking further ways to foster their abilities and resources
to better serve the community of Dyer County; and
WHEREAS, liberal with the many gifts that their team possesses, the Dyer County
Rescue Squad renders multiagency teamwork across county lines when possible and
necessary, and all their endeavors are focused on supplying the most effective emergency
services wherever they are called upon; and
WHEREAS, the exemplary members of the Dyer County Rescue Squad are truly
dedicated to the health and well-being of their fellow Tennesseans, and for their prodigious
efforts on behalf of the people of this State, they should be recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the members of the Dyer County Rescue Squad
on the celebration of the squad's fiftieth anniversary, salute their distinguished service to the
good people of Dyer County, and extend our best wishes for much continued success in their
future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
