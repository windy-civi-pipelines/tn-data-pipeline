Section 1:
By Crowe
HOUSE BILL 727
By Hicks G
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 53;
Title 68 and Title 71, relative to temporary
assistance for needy families.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 71-3-1203, is amended by adding
the following to the end of the section:
The department shall report on the effectiveness of the drug testing program to such
committees of the senate and the house of representatives by December 1 of each year.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
