SENATE RESOLUTION 33
By Roberts
A RESOLUTION approving the amendments and revisions to the
Tennessee Rules of Civil Procedure,
promulgated by the Supreme Court of
Tennessee pursuant to the provisions of
Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 16, Chapter 3,
Part 4.
WHEREAS, on December 16, 2024, the Supreme Court of Tennessee, acting pursuant
to Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 16, Chapter 3, Part 4, filed an order promulgating certain
amendments and revisions to the Tennessee Rules of Civil Procedure, previously promulgated
and adopted by the Court; and
WHEREAS, on January 23, 2025, a certified copy of such order, together with a certified
copy of the proposed amendments and revisions to the Tennessee Rules of Civil Procedure,
was reported and filed with the General Assembly by the Chief Justice for approval by a
resolution of both houses; and
WHEREAS, such amendments and revisions to the Tennessee Rules of Civil Procedure
have been duly considered by the Senate of the One Hundred Fourteenth General Assembly of
the State of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that the amendments and revisions
to the Tennessee Rules of Civil Procedure, as promulgated and adopted by the Supreme Court
in its order dated December 16, 2024, a copy of which was filed with the Clerks of the House of
Representatives and Senate on January 23, 2025, as aforesaid, are hereby ratified and
approved.
