Section 1:
By Haston
SENATE BILL 1340
By Powers
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 9;
Title 49 and Title 62, relative to higher education.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-7-202(f), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision:
(4) The commission shall review and update the outcomes-based funding
formula model at least once every five (5) years to provide incentives for institutions that
offer programs leading to job placement in high-demand fields in this state.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
