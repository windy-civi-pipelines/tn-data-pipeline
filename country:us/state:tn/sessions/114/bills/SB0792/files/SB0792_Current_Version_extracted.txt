Section 1:
By Williams
SENATE BILL 792
By Bailey
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4;
Title 10; Title 37; Title 38; Title 39; Title 40; Title 55
and Title 58, relative to terrorism.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 39, Chapter 13, Part 8, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
The district attorneys general conference shall compile a yearly report detailing
convictions made pursuant to this part during the previous year. The report must be
provided to the general assembly no later than January 15, 2026, and each January 15
thereafter.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
