Section 1:
By Bailey
HOUSE BILL 165
By Marsh
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4;
Title 47; Title 54; Title 55; Title 56; Title 65; Title 66
and Title 67, relative to transportation.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-7-103(a), is amended by deleting
the language "From January 15 to April 15 of each year" and substituting instead the language
"From January 1 to April 15 of each year".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
