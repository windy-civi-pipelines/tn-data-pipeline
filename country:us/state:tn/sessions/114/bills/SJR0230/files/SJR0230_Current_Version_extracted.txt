SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 230
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor David Painter on setting the new record
for career pinfalls in bowling at Union
Commonwealth University.
WHEREAS, we take great pleasure in recognizing those dedicated student athletes who
serve as the heart and soul of their schools and communities while setting major records in their
respective sports; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary young man is David Painter, who enjoyed an
outstanding bowling career at Greeneville High School before continuing his career on the lanes
at Union Commonwealth University in 2020; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Painter competed with the Union bowling team for five years (2020-
2025) and recently etched his name into the annals of the school's bowling history by breaking
the previous record for career pinfalls, establishing himself as the new all-time leader with
32,231; and
WHEREAS, David Painter set the new school record for pinfalls at the Hoosier Classic, a
prestigious tournament featuring more than seventy teams from across the nation, eclipsing the
previous mark in his third game; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this young man
surely bode well for his future success; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Painter has distinguished himself as an extraordinary bowler of whom
we can all be proud, and he is most worthy of our approbation on this special occasion; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
SJR0230
004690
- 1 -
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate David Painter on setting
the new record for career pinfalls in bowling at Union Commonwealth University and wish him
all the best in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 004690
