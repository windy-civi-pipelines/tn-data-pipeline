Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 62-5-305(b), is amended by deleting
subdivision (7) and substituting the following:
(7) Has completed two (2) years of apprenticeship in the presence of and under
the direction and supervision of a licensed funeral director. An associate degree from a
college accredited by the American Board of Funeral Service Education or completion of
a program of study evidenced by an official transcript as provided in subdivision (b)(6),
may be substituted for one (1) year of apprenticeship.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
