SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 140
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to provide for a recess in the proceedings of the
One Hundred Fourteenth General Assembly.
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that the One Hundred Fourteenth General Assembly
stand in recess at the close of business in both houses on Thursday, February 13, 2025, and
the House of Representatives reconvene on Thursday, February 20, 2025, at 9:00 a.m. (CST)
and the Senate reconvene on Thursday, February 20, 2025, at 8:30 a.m. (CST).
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that pursuant to Article II, Section 16, of the Constitution
of Tennessee, the House of Representatives, by its concurrence in this resolution, consents to
this recess.
SJR0140
