HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 239
By Darby
A RESOLUTION to congratulate Anne Claire Collins on being
named the 2025 Hostess Princess of the 72nd
Annual World's Biggest Fish Fry and Jordyn
Ingram, Lainey Martin, McKenna Thrower, Mary
Claire Palmer, and Halle Ann Casey on being
named to the 2025 Hostess Princess Court.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to specially recognize
those outstanding young women of this State whose poise, admirable personality traits, and
beauty, both inner and outer, make them role models among their peers; and
WHEREAS, five such outstanding young women from Henry County include Anne Claire
Collins, who was named 2025 Hostess Princess, and Jordyn Ingram, Lainey Martin, McKenna
Thrower, Mary Claire Palmer, and Halle Ann Casey who were selected as members of the 2025
Hostess Princess Court in honor of the festivities of the 72nd Annual World's Biggest Fish Fry
sponsored by the Paris-Henry County Jaycees in Paris, Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, for more than seven decades, the World's Biggest Fish Fry has brought
acclaim and recognition to Paris; this renowned festival features a full week of activities for all
ages and is conducted for members of the community and visitors alike to enjoy; and
WHEREAS, the 2025 Hostess Princess is Anne Claire Collins, a senior at Henry County
High School and the daughter of Andy and Susanne Collins; and
WHEREAS, the 2025 Hostess Princess Court is composed of first maid Jordyn Ingram,
second maid Lainey Martin, third maid McKenna Thrower, fourth maid Mary Claire Palmer, and
fifth maid Halle Ann Casey; and
WHEREAS, these young ladies' beauty, grace, drive, and determination are sources of
pride for their parents, their families, and their communities and bode well for their future
successes; and
HJR0239
003680
- 1 -
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we specially recognize and honor the Hostess Princess and
her Court, five of Tennessee's most promising young women; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Anne Claire Collins on being named 2025
Hostess Princess of the 72nd Annual World's Biggest Fish Fry in Paris, Tennessee; likewise, we
honor Jordyn Ingram, Lainey Martin, McKenna Thrower, Mary Claire Palmer, and Halle Ann
Casey on being named to the 2025 Hostess Princess Court and extend to these exceptional
young ladies our best wishes for every success in all their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
