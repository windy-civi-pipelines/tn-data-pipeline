Section 1:
Senate State and Local Government 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB0039
Briggs
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting the first sentence of the amendatory language in Section 1 and substituting:
Notwithstanding a charter, resolution, or another law to the contrary, a county
legislative body may, by a two-thirds (2/3) vote of the legislative body, remove any
member of an appointed board or commission, if such removal is recommended by the
appointing authority and the member of the board or commission is given at least five (5)
business days' notice of the meeting when the vote on removal will occur. This section
does not apply in a county that has a charter provision or resolution providing for the
removal of members of boards and commissions prior to the effective date of this act.
