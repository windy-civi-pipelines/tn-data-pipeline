HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 89
By Freeman
A RESOLUTION to celebrate March 15, 2025, as "Tennessee
Tree Day."
WHEREAS, Tennessee is the most biologically diverse inland state in these United
States; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee is a state of robust forest cover and tree canopy, which provide
myriad benefits to Tennessee's economy, communities, and quality of life; and
WHEREAS, in certain areas, ongoing human development, natural disasters, and other
causes are responsible for the loss of trees and forest cover every year; and
WHEREAS, there is a perpetual need to encourage Tennesseans within their
communities to grow our State's tree canopy; and
WHEREAS, the Tennessee Environmental Council (TEC), a nonprofit organization
founded in 1970, has been organizing large-scale tree planting events annually since 2007,
resulting in more than one million native trees being planted in all ninety-five counties; and
WHEREAS, over 100,000 Tennessee volunteers have participated in these events since
2007, and the public demand for planting trees is strong year after year; and
WHEREAS, partnerships and cooperation with the Tennessee Department of
Agriculture-Division of Forestry, UT Extension Institute of Agriculture, numerous Tennessee-
based corporations, local storm water agencies, rural electric cooperatives, public works
departments, and dozens of local community organizations and agencies are integral to the
success of TEC's statewide tree-planting efforts; and
WHEREAS, the majority of the native trees planted in these events are grown at the
Tennessee Division of Forestry's East Tennessee Nursery in Delano; and
HJR0089
001829
- 1 -
WHEREAS, in 2025, the Tennessee Environmental Council will engage thousands of
volunteer participants in planting more than 100,000 native trees across all ninety-five counties
on March 15th; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we join with the Tennessee Environmental Council in its celebration of
Saturday, March 15, 2025, as "Tennessee Tree Day," during which day thousands of
Tennesseans will participate in tree-planting events in their communities.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we honor and commend the Tennessee
Environmental Council's service as the lead organization orchestrating these events and its
pledge to continue offering tree seedlings, educational information, and coordination as
community support, resources, and capacity allow.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
