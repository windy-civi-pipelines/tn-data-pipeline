Section 1:
By Bricken
SENATE BILL 13
By Walley
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4 and
Title 7, Chapter 86, Part 3, relative to the
emergency communications board.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-86-302(b)(1), is amended by
deleting "The board shall be composed of nine (9) members" and substituting "The board shall
be composed of eleven (11) members".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-86-302(b)(1), is amended by
deleting subdivisions (C) and (D) and substituting:
(C) Two (2) representatives of county government, appointed by the speaker of
the senate;
(D) Two (2) representatives of city government, appointed by the speaker of the
house of representatives;

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-86-302(h), is amended by deleting
the subsection and substituting:
A quorum shall consist of six (6) or more members, and all official action of the
board requires a quorum.

Section 5:
SECTION 4. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.

SENATE BILL 13
By Walley
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4 and
Title 7, Chapter 86, Part 3, relative to the
emergency communications board.

deleting "The board shall be composed of nine (9) members" and substituting "The board shall

deleting subdivisions (C) and (D) and substituting:
(C) Two (2) representatives of county government, appointed by the speaker of
the senate;
(D) Two (2) representatives of city government, appointed by the speaker of the
house of representatives;
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-86-302(h), is amended by deleting
the subsection and substituting:
A quorum shall consist of six (6) or more members, and all official action of the
board requires a quorum.
SECTION 4. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
