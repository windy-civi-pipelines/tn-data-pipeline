HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 353
By Warner
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Garrett Webster on
earning the distinguished rank of Eagle Scout
and being named a National Merit Scholar.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
an outstanding young man who, through diligence and dexterity, has earned the rank of Eagle
Scout and been named a National Merit Scholar; and
WHEREAS, the highest rank a Scout can attain in the Boy Scouts of America Scouts
BSA program is that of Eagle Scout, and Garrett Webster, after considerable effort and diligent
preparation, has joined the prestigious Eagle Court of Honor; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Webster began his Scouting journey as a Cub Scout in the Wolf den,
and his notable record of achievement includes numerous merit badges and distinguished
service as a leader among his peers; and
WHEREAS, Garrett Webster is a member of the BSA honor society, the Order of the
Arrow, and he has served his troop as a senior patrol leader, chaplain's aide, and junior
assistant Scoutmaster; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Webster's Eagle project greatly contributed to the betterment of his
community and reflected the true spirit of volunteerism; for his project, he devoted eighteen
service hours to replacing benches for the City of Lewisburg and Daughters of the American
Revolution; and
WHEREAS, a senior at Cornersville High School, Mr. Webster has proven himself a
respected scholar and honor student of the highest caliber, and his accomplishments most
certainly bode well for his future success; and

WHEREAS, in addition to his participation in dual enrollment courses at the University of
Tennessee Southern, the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, and Columbia State Community
College, Mr. Webster has been a valuable member of the Marshall County Teen Court, student
council, the First National Bank Student Advisory Board, Future Business Leaders of America,
and Beta Club; and
WHEREAS, a dynamic leader in his community, he attended Hugh O'Brian Youth
Leadership in 2023 as an ambassador and in 2024 as a junior staff member and served as a
delegate to the Tennessee American Legion Boys State at Tennessee Technological University,
where he served as Minority Party Leader of the Senate and won Best in Parade; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Webster's noteworthy achievements are a tremendous source of pride
for his family, his school, his community, and this State; and
WHEREAS, it is appropriate that we should pause to recognize Mr. Webster as one of
the most hardworking and outstanding young people in our State; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and commend Garrett Webster on earning the
distinguished rank of Eagle Scout and being named a National Merit Scholar, extending to him
our best wishes for much continued success in the years to come.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
