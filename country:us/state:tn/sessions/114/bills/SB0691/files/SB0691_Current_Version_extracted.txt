Section 1:
By Burkhart
SENATE BILL 691
By Johnson
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 2-
16-103; Section 3-1-102 and Section 3-1-103,
relative to redistricting.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 3-1-102(g), is amended by adding
the following language at the end of the subsection:
The comptroller of the treasury shall report to the general assembly which counties, if
any, redrew precinct lines pursuant to this subsection (g).

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
