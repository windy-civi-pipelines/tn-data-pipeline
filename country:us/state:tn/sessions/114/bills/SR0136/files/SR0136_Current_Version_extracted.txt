SENATE RESOLUTION 136
By Oliver
A RESOLUTION to commend Destini Rainer for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Destini Rainer is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in her
role as a legislative intern for Senator Charlane Oliver; her work has resulted in innumerable
benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Tennessee State University, where she is highly regarded by
her peers, Destini Rainer has achieved academic success while double majoring in political
science and English; and
WHEREAS, in addition to her legislative and academic responsibilities, she has also
participated in a number of extracurricular activities and organizations, including the National
Association of Colored Women's Clubs Incorporated, Women of Empowerment; the Tennessee
Intercollegiate State Legislature; Pi Sigma Alpha; SoulFire Poetry and Spoken Word Club; the
Shelby County School Legal Department and the City of Memphis Law Division as an intern; the
Resident Assistant program; the Memphis Ambassador program; and the Humanities
Tennessee Literature and Language programs; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Ms. Rainer brought her many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2025 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
SR0136
007122
- 1 -
WHEREAS, throughout the 2025 legislative session, Destini Rainer has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Destini Rainer has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight
and rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby commend Destini
Rainer for outstanding service as a legislative intern for Senator Charlane Oliver and extend to
her our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
