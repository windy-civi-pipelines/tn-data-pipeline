Section 1:
By Massey
HOUSE BILL 212
By Howell
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 55,
Chapter 4, relative to license plates.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-4-225(a), is amended by adding
the following at the end of the subsection:
The application and proof may be submitted electronically, by mail, or in person.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
