SENATE RESOLUTION 187
By Oliver
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Lena Zewdu Feleke,
2025 Valedictorian of Martin Luther King, Jr.,
Magnet High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pause to pay tribute to those students who,
through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to academic excellence, have
distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Lena Zewdu Feleke is one such exceptional student who was recently
named Valedictorian of the 2025 graduating class at Martin Luther King, Jr., Magnet High
School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Lena Zewdu Feleke has achieved outstanding
academic success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our
country; and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Lena Zewdu Feleke has earned an
exemplary grade point average; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Feleke's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for her future success; and
WHEREAS, Lena Zewdu Feleke's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of
pride for her family, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Lena Zewdu Feleke's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the
respect, admiration, and commendation of the members of this legislative body; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby commend and honor
the 2025 Martin Luther King, Jr., Magnet High School Valedictorian, Lena Zewdu Feleke, on her

future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
