HOUSE RESOLUTION 77
By Clemmons
A RESOLUTION to commend Spencer Webb for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Spencer Webb is one such individual, who has distinguished himself in his
role as a legislative intern for House Democratic Caucus Chairman, Representative John Ray
Clemmons; Spencer's work has resulted in innumerable benefits for the legislators he assisted,
Chairman Clemmons, and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Austin Peay State University, where he is highly regarded by
his peers, Spencer Webb has achieved academic success while pursuing a bachelor's degree
in history with a minor in pre-law studies; and
WHEREAS, Spencer thrives in the classroom and has twice been named to the Austin
Peay Dean's List for his academic achievements; and
WHEREAS, after graduation, he plans to pursue a Juris Doctor degree and hopes to
someday practice law in the field of civil litigation; and
WHEREAS, a native of Bradley County and a cum laude graduate of Cleveland High
School, Spencer Webb is the eldest of three brothers; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize himself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further his studies, Spencer Webb brought his many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2025 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and
HR0077
006924
- 1 -
WHEREAS, throughout the 2025 legislative session, Spencer Webb has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, he has performed his many duties with extraordinary insight and rare
decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we commend
Spencer Webb for outstanding service as a legislative intern for Chairman John Ray Clemmons
and extend to him our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
