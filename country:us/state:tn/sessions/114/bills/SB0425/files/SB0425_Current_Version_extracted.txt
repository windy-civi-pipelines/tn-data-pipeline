Section 1:
By Sparks
SENATE BILL 425
By Reeves
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Section
10-7-504, relative to public employees.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 10-7-504(f)(1)(D), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting:
(D) Residential information, including the street address, city, state, and zip
code, for any state, county, municipal, or other public employee;

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
