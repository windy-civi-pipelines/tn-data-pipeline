HOUSE RESOLUTION 10
By Maberry
A RESOLUTION to honor Araceli White upon receiving the Navy
Club of the United States Military Excellence
Award.
WHEREAS, we take great pleasure in recognizing a passionate and resilient young
Tennessean who has begun her career in the U.S. Navy by receiving top marks and a
prestigious award; and
WHEREAS, Airman Apprentice Araceli White of Clarksville recently graduated as the top
Sailor from U.S. Navy Recruit Training Command (RTC), earning the Military Excellence Award;
and
WHEREAS, the Navy Club of the United States Military Excellence Award is the highest
award presented to the recruit who best exemplifies the qualities of enthusiasm, devotion to
duty, military bearing, and teamwork; and
WHEREAS, Araceli White's receipt of this award places her at the pinnacle of today's
newest Sailors as she embarks upon her promising career in the U.S. Navy; and
WHEREAS, Ms. White enlisted in the Navy to help make her country a better place and
to progress and develop as a person; and
WHEREAS, at RTC, she acquired the leadership traits necessary to transform a group
of individuals into a team, a key aspect of all military training; and
WHEREAS, Airman Apprentice White will now attend Air Traffic Controller "A" School for
training in the fundamentals of air traffic control, radar and radio communications group
instruction, and simulators; and
WHEREAS, a young lady of diverse talents, Araceli White is a graduate of West Creek
High School, where she was a member of the National Honor Society, the honor choir, student

Gladiolus Days Scholarship Pageant; and
WHEREAS, we wish Araceli White all the best as she continues to serve her country
with pride and proficiency; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
congratulate Airman Apprentice Araceli White of Clarksville upon graduating as the top Sailor

thank her for her ongoing commitment to the United States of America.
