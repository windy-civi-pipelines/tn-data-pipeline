HOUSE BILL 176

AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 8,
relative to retirement for law enforcement officers.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 8-36-809(b)(7)(A), is amended by
inserting the language "in writing" immediately after the language "Notify".

SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
