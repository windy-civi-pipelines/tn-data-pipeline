Section 2:
SECTION ___. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 37-1-153, is amended by
adding the following as a new subsection:
(g)
(1) Notwithstanding this section or another law to the contrary, all files
and juvenile court records in a delinquency proceeding under this part, including
medical reports, psychological evaluations, and any other document, relating to a
person must be opened to public inspection and their content subject to
disclosure to the public if:
(A) The person has committed a homicide on the grounds of a
school, as defined in ยง 39-13-114(a); provided, however, that this
subdivision (g)(1) does not require the person to have been adjudicated
delinquent for or convicted of a homicide offense in order for the person's
records to be open to public inspection; and
(B) The person is deceased.
(2) Any information in a file or juvenile court record that directly or
indirectly identifies another juvenile must be redacted.
