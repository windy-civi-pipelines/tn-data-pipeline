HOUSE RESOLUTION 62
By Howell
A RESOLUTION to commend Lauren Cabrera for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this body is pleased to recognize certain persons
who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment of the legislative
process; and
WHEREAS, Lauren Cabrera is one such individual who has distinguished herself in her
role as a legislative intern for Representative Dan Howell; Ms. Cabrera's work has resulted in
innumerable benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Freed-Hardeman University, where she is highly regarded by
her peers, Ms. Cabrera has achieved academic success while majoring in political science and
participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Lauren Cabrera brought her many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2025 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2025 legislative session, Ms. Cabrera has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Lauren Cabrera has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight
and rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby
commend Lauren Cabrera for outstanding service as a legislative intern for Representative Dan
Howell and extend to her our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
