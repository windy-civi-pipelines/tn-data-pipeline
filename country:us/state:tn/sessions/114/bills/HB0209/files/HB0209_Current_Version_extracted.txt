Section 1:
By Massey
HOUSE BILL 209
By Howell
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4;
Title 5; Title 6; Title 7; Title 8; Title 54; Title 55;
Title 65 and Title 67, relative to transportation.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-21-304, is amended by adding
the following as a new subsection:
(c) The commissioner may take reasonable measures to publicize the availability
of the option described in this part to potential participants.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-50-307, is amended by adding
the following as a new subsection (d) and redesignating existing subsection (d) accordingly:
(d) The department may take reasonable measures to publicize the availability
of the option described in this section to potential participants.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
