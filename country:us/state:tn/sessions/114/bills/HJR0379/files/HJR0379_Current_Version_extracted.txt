HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 379
By Cepicky
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate the 2025 Mule Day
Court Third Runner-Up, Maya Keeton.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should salute those
young citizens who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as
outstanding Tennesseans of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy person is Maya Keeton, who was recently selected as
the 2025 Mule Day Court Third Runner-Up; and
WHEREAS, the daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Mark Keeton, Maya Keeton is currently a
junior at Columbia Academy, where she excels in dual enrollment classes and as a member of
Beta Club, Science Club, and Best Buddies, in addition to playing on the varsity basketball and
B. Wright basketball teams; and
WHEREAS, additionally, Ms. Keeton was selected to participate in the REACH
academic excellence club and the 2025 Youth Leadership Maury Class; and
WHEREAS, dedicated to her community, Ms. Keeton is a member of St. Peter's Church
and enjoys devoting her time and talents as a volunteer for Crossroads and the People's Table;
she has accumulated more than 100 volunteer hours and has helped to collect more than 100
items to assist Columbia's homeless population; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Keeton's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
her family, her community, and the citizens of Maury County; and
WHEREAS, the Mule Day Queen and her court play a prominent role in the promotion of
Mule Day, participating in media interviews and serving as hostesses during the festivities; and

WHEREAS, 2025 Mule Day Court Third Runner-Up Maya Keeton epitomizes the spirit of
distinction and the commitment to greatness that are characteristic of true Tennesseans; her
dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect and admiration of this body; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to pause in their
deliberations to acknowledge and applaud this young woman for her remarkable achievement;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Maya Keeton upon being selected as the 2025
Mule Day Court Third Runner-Up, extending to her our best wishes for every future success and
happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
