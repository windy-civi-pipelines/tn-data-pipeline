SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 161
By Oliver
A RESOLUTION to honor Mary's Old Fashioned Pit Bar-B-Que on
more than sixty years of service to North
Nashville.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize an
outstanding restaurant that earned loyal customers and received critical accolades for its mouth-
watering food; and
WHEREAS, for more than six decades, Mary's Old Fashioned Pit Bar-B-Que served the
North Nashville community with some of the most delicious down-home food available in Music
City; and
WHEREAS, after opening in 1962, Mary's Old Fashioned Pit Bar-B-Que quickly became
a neighborhood fixture by offering a taste of traditional barbeque that attracted numerous repeat
customers over the years; and
WHEREAS, after surviving tornadoes and a global pandemic, Mary's Old Fashioned Pit
Bar-B-Que closed its doors for the final time on February 1, 2025; however, its legacy will
continue to inspire the merchants located on the historic Jefferson Street corridor and their
clientele, the good people of North Nashville; and
WHEREAS, owner Katresa Fizer, granddaughter of the business's founder, Mary, is
stepping away from the business to enjoy life during retirement; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Fizer fondly remembers her grandmother Mary as a savvy
businesswoman who was also a generous benefactor of the community; and
WHEREAS, for sixty-three years, Mary's Old Fashioned Pit Bar-B-Que was an icon in
Nashville, and the spirit of giving and sense of community commenced by its founder and
embraced by Ms. Fizer are deserving of our recognition; now, therefore,
SJR0161
003542
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor Mary's Old Fashioned Pit Bar-B-Que on
more than sixty years of service to North Nashville, thank its owners and employees for
providing delicious food to people from all walks of life, and wish Katresa Fizer all the best in her
future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
