SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 445
By Harshbarger
A RESOLUTION to honor the Colonial Heights Athletic
Commission.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly take great pleasure in recognizing
organizations that are deeply committed to helping the youth of Tennessee mature into
responsible citizens of robust character who are committed to giving back to their community;
and
WHEREAS, one such worthy organization is the Colonial Heights Athletic Commission
(CHAC); based in Kingsport, CHAC is a youth sports organization dedicated to creating a fun,
inclusive, and competitive environment in which young people can grow through participation in
sports such as baseball, basketball, flag football, and volleyball; and
WHEREAS, CHAC's programs focus on building skills, confidence, and character while
also emphasizing teamwork, sportsmanship, and a sense of community among its young
athletes; and
WHEREAS, CHAC is not only interested in developing young athletes but is also
committed to building lasting connections, both on and off the field; and
WHEREAS, CHAC is fortunate to have the leadership and acumen of President Lucas
LeBlanc, Vice President Jacob Britt, Treasurer Alex Rogers, Secretary Audra Sanders, and
Communications Director Dustin Nelson; and
WHEREAS, the youth of Tennessee are deserving of our best efforts in preparing them
for adulthood, and CHAC and its leadership are answering this call to duty by teaching
Kingsport's youth the importance of hard work, cooperation, character, and confidence in
building a good life; now, therefore,
SJR0445
007496
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor the officers, volunteers, and parents of
Colonial Heights Athletic Commission of Kingsport for developing tomorrow's leaders today
through athletic competition, teamwork, and sportsmanship and extend to them our best wishes
for much continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 007496
