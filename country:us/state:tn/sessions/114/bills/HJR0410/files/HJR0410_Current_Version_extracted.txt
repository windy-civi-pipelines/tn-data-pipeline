HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 410
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Christopher Campbell,
2025 Top Ten Senior of Hampshire Unit School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Christopher Campbell is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named a Top Ten Senior of the 2025 graduating class at Hampshire Unit School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Campbell has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Christopher Campbell has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities,
including Beta Club and FBLA; and
WHEREAS, his time at Hampshire Unit School saw him volunteer generously to
fundraise for FBLA; and
WHEREAS, Christopher Campbell's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely
bodes well for his future success at Columbia State Community College, where he will study
accounting; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Campbell's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
his parents, Suzanne Kathy Campbell and Byron Lyon Campbell, his school, and his
community; and
HJR0410
005282
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Christopher Campbell's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the
respect, admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Christopher Campbell on being named a
2025 Hampshire Unit School Top Ten Senior, salute his impressive academic achievement, and
extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
