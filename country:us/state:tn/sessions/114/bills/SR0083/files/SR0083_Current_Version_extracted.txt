SENATE RESOLUTION 83
By Oliver
A RESOLUTION to recognize Nikki Boleyjack on her selection as
the 2024–2025 Martin Luther King, Jr., School
Middle School Teacher of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest-quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Nikki Boleyjack is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, a teacher at Martin Luther King, Jr., School, Ms. Boleyjack was recently
named the school's 2024–2025 Middle School Teacher of the Year, a most befitting honor for
her tenure as an educator, as it recognizes the positive influence she has on so many young
lives; and
WHEREAS, Nikki Boleyjack is an extremely talented and compassionate educator who
has worked assiduously throughout her career in education to motivate her students, fellow
teachers, and staff to reach their full potential as scholars, professionals, and citizens; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Boleyjack truly serves as an inspiration to the faculty and staff at Martin
Luther King, Jr., School, and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are
fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Nikki Boleyjack epitomizes the dedicated educator who is wholly committed
to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby honor and

congratulate Nikki Boleyjack on being chosen as the 2024–2025 Martin Luther King, Jr., School
Middle School Teacher of the Year, commend her exceptional service to the children of
Tennessee, salute her professional expertise and personal excellence, and wish her all the best
in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
