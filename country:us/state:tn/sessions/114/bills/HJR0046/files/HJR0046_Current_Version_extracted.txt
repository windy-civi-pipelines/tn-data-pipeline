HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 46
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor Kyle Shoulders upon his selection as
the 2024 Tennessee Titans Coach of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should pay tribute to those excellent coaches who have
led their players to success in athletic competition and in the game of life; and
WHEREAS, for the past seven years, Coach Kyle Shoulders has established himself as
the heart and soul of the Macon County football program and guided the Tigers to
unprecedented heights of achievement; and
WHEREAS, Kyle Shoulders was named the 2024 Coach of the Year by the Tennessee
Titans in appreciation of his transformation of the Macon County High School football program,
an endeavor that resulted in a historic state championship win for the school; and
WHEREAS, Coach Kyle Shoulders began his coaching career at Macon County High
School as an assistant on staff; he went on to serve Red Boiling Springs High School as the
head coach, leading the team to its first postseason appearance in the program's history, and
then Oakland High School; and
WHEREAS, Kyle Shoulders joined Macon County High School as the head coach in
2018, and in 2024, he led the Macon County Tigers to a school record of 13-2 and their first
TSSAA football championship; and
WHEREAS, Coach Kyle Shoulders has distinguished himself as a great coach, tactician,
and motivator of young people, and he should be appropriately recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Kyle Shoulders upon his selection as the 2024

success both on and off the field.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared

the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
