HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 520
By Marsh
A RESOLUTION to honor Nick Davis upon being inducted as the
2025 president of the Tennessee Road Builders
Association.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should salute those
citizens who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as business
leaders of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such noteworthy person is Nick Davis, who is the 2025 president of the
Tennessee Road Builders Association; and
WHEREAS, on February 12, 2025, Mr. Davis was installed as the seventy-ninth
president of the Tennessee Road Builders Association (TRBA), an honor most befitting his
commitment to and leadership in his profession; and
WHEREAS, born and raised in Murfreesboro, Nick Davis currently resides in Cookeville
with his wife, Susy Davis, and their children, Avery and Hudson; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Davis is a graduate of Middle Tennessee State University, where he
received a Bachelor of Science degree in Concrete Industry Management and was a member of
the second Concrete Management graduating class; and
WHEREAS, in 2001, he began his career at Harper Construction; in 2004, he gained
employment with Mid-State Construction Company in Livingston, and he currently serves as the
vice president of the Bridge Division; and
WHEREAS, Nick Davis is the second person from Mid-State Construction to serve as
TRBA president, following Johnny Fred Coleman in 2006; and
HJR0520
006539
- 1 -
WHEREAS, a noted and trusted leader in his profession, Mr. Davis was elected to the
TRBA Board in 2017 as chairman of the Bridge & Concrete Division, in which capacity he
served for six years; and
WHEREAS, he has also served as the TRBA's first vice president and treasurer, second
vice president, and a member of the organization's Investment Committee; additionally, he is a
graduate of TRBA's Emerging Leaders Development Program; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Davis is a member of the Tennessee Highway Contractors PAC Board
and the TRBA/TDOT Work Zone Safety Committee, and he is a graduate of the American Road
& Transportation Builders Association Transportation Builder Institute Project Management
Academy; and
WHEREAS, Nick Davis epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are characteristic of a
true Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly find it appropriate to acknowledge
and applaud Mr. Davis for his dedication to the highway and bridge construction industry; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor Nick Davis upon being inducted as the 2025 president of the
Tennessee Road Builders Association, commend him for his meritorious service to the State of
Tennessee, and extend to him our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
