SENATE RESOLUTION 173
By Rose
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Justice Cook, 2025
Valedictorian of Halls High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Justice Cook is one such exceptional student who was recently named
Valedictorian of the 2025 graduating class at Halls High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Cook has achieved outstanding academic success
and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country; and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Justice Cook has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in a number of extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, his display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for his
future success; and
WHEREAS, Justice Cook's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
his family, his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Cook's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of our respect,
admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby commend and honor
the 2025 Halls High School Valedictorian, Justice Cook, on his impressive academic
achievement and extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
SR0173

- 1 -
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
