SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 197
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to recognize Tamara Yelton of Unicoi County
High School on her selection as a 2024–2025
Kiwanis Club Teacher of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Tamara Yelton is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, an educator at Unicoi County High School, Tamara Yelton was recently
named the 2024–2025 Teacher of the Year by the Kiwanis Club of Erwin; and
WHEREAS, an educator for nine years, Ms. Yelton has taught U.S. and world history at
Unicoi County High School for three years and is passionate about supporting the school's
students by attending student-led events, including school sports, dances, plays, and youth
group church productions; and
WHEREAS, Tamara Yelton is an extremely talented and compassionate educator who
has worked assiduously throughout her career in education to motivate her students, fellow
teachers, and staff to reach their full potential as scholars, professionals, and citizens; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Yelton truly serves as an inspiration to the faculty and staff at Unicoi
County High School, and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are
fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Tamara Yelton epitomizes the dedicated educator who is wholly committed
to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
SJR0197
004201
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Tamara Yelton
on being chosen as a 2024–2025 Teacher of the Year by the Kiwanis Club of Erwin, commend
her exceptional service to the children of Tennessee, salute her professional expertise and
personal excellence, and wish her much continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
