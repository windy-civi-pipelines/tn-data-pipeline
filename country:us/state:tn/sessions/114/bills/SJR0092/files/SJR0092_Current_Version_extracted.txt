Section 1:
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 92
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of John Compton to
the board of trustees of the University of
Tennessee.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-201, provides for the governance
of the University of Tennessee by the board of trustees; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-202, provides that the governor
shall appoint ten members to the board of trustees of the University of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed John Compton to the board of trustees of
the University of Tennessee for a term that expires June 30, 2030; and
WHEREAS, a graduate of the University of Tennessee, Mr. Compton is the former chief
executive officer of PepsiCo Americas Foods; he also served as the chief executive officer of
Pilot Flying J and has served as an advisor to Pilot Flying J, the Haslam family, and the
Cleveland Browns; and
WHEREAS, in 2013, he joined a New York private equity firm, Clayton, Dubilier & Rice
as an operating advisor; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Compton brings valuable experience and knowledge to the board of
trustees; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-202, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the board of trustees of the University of Tennessee shall be
confirmed by joint resolution of the General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
SJR0092
001533
- 1 -
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that this body does hereby confirm the appointment of
John Compton to the board of trustees of the University of Tennessee for a term that expires
June 30, 2030.
