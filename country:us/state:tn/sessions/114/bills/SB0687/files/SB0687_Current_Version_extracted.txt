Section 1:
By Hicks T
SENATE BILL 687
By White
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4 and
Title 49, relative to education.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-1-104(i), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision:
(3) By June 1, 2025, and June 1 each year thereafter, the commissioner shall
publish on the department's website the number of waivers granted to LEAs pursuant to
this subsection (i) during the previous school year.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
