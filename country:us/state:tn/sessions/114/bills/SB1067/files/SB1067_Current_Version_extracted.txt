Section 1:
By Cepicky
SENATE BILL 1067
By White
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49,
relative to education curricula.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-6-1007(c), is amended by adding
the following at the end of the subsection:
Each LEA shall publish the character education curriculum on the LEA's website.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
