Section 1:
Senate Education 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB1014
White
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting subsection (a) in Section 2 and substituting instead the following:
(a) As used in this section:
(1) "Child care facility" means a child care agency, a child care center, or
a drop-in center, as those terms are defined in § 71-3-501, that is owned and
operated by one (1) or more individuals who do not own or operate the center or
agency as a corporation domiciled in this or another state;
(2) "Underutilized property" has the same meaning as defined in § 49-13-
104; and
(3) "Vacant property" has the same meaning as defined in § 49-13-104.
