by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 5-1-202, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(d) The offices of sheriff, register, county clerk, assessor of property, and trustee
in a county that has adopted a charter form of government in a county having a
population greater than nine hundred thousand (900,000), according to the 2020 or a
subsequent federal census, shall independently develop the budget for such office and
submit the budget directly to the county legislative body. These offices shall adhere to
the budget as finally approved by the county legislative body, both in total and in line
item detail, except that the offices may approve transfers between line items within the
total amount of each category of personnel and operational-maintenance as set forth in
the approved budget for any such office. These offices shall submit budget
amendments from line items in personnel categories to operational-maintenance
categories, and vice versa, to the county legislative body. The county legislative body
shall not implement any kind of personnel restrictions or operational-maintenance
restrictions on these offices.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
