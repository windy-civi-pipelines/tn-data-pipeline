Section 1:
By Warner
SENATE BILL 159
By Hensley
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 2,
Chapter 6, Part 5 and Title 2, Chapter 9, relative to
online voting.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 2-9-110, is amended by adding the
following new subsection (d):
(d) Notwithstanding another law to the contrary, voting equipment and machines
must not transmit or receive a ballot, or otherwise be used to cast a ballot, using the
internet, except as authorized for members of the military and overseas citizens in
accordance with chapter 6, part 5 of this title.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
