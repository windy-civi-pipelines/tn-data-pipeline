SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 361
By Hatcher
A RESOLUTION to commend Darryan Walker for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize
certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment
of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Darryan Walker is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in her
role as a legislative intern for Senator Tom Hatcher; Darryan's work has resulted in innumerable
benefits for the legislators she assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Tennessee State University (TSU), where she is highly
regarded by her peers, Ms. Walker has achieved academic success while majoring in public
health; and
WHEREAS, during her collegiate career, Ms. Walker, always seeking greater ways to
develop her already impressive character, has been a member of the Student Government
Association at TSU, the Alpha Psi Chapter of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Inc., the Seventh
Cohort of Leadership at TSU in 2022, and the Eighth Cohort of Leadership at TSU in 2023; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize herself with the characteristics and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further her studies, Darryan Walker brought her many
abilities and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2025 session of the One Hundred
Fourteenth General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2025 legislative session, Ms. Walker has surpassed all
criteria that define a devoted and conscientious legislative intern; and
WHEREAS, Darryan Walker has performed her many duties with extraordinary insight
and rare decorum; now, therefore,
SJR0361
006617
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Darryan Walker for
outstanding service as a legislative intern for Senator Tom Hatcher and extend to her our best
wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
