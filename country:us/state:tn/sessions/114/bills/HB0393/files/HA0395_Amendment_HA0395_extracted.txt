Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4, Chapter 6, Part 1, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
(a) When a canine that was utilized in the service of the department of correction
is retired due to age, service-related injury, or any other reason, and the canine is retired
to the care and custody of a department of correction employee, whether active or
retired, the department, from funds budgeted to the department through the general
appropriations act, shall pay, on a monthly basis, the employee caring for the retired
canine the sum of eighty-five dollars ($85.00) to be used exclusively for the medical care
of the retired canine.
(b) The employee with custody of the retired canine shall maintain records of all
medical treatment provided to the retired canine in the employee's care and custody
and, by June 1 of each year, submit a copy of these medical records to the department
of correction for the fiscal year ending at the end of that month.
(c) Within thirty (30) days of the death of a retired canine in the care of a
department of correction employee, the employee shall notify the department of
correction of the canine's death. The department shall terminate the monthly medical
treatment payments to the employee provided by this section upon receiving notification
pursuant to this subsection (c).

(d) The monthly amount set out in subsection (a) is in addition to any amount
that is otherwise due and paid to the employee by virtue of salary, retirement, disability,
