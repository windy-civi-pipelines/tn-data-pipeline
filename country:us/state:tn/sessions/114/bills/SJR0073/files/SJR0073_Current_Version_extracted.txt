SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 73
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Amanda Bean on her
receipt of the Student Achievement Award from
the Tennessee School Board Association.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their remarkable efforts, have distinguished themselves
as emerging community leaders of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such exceptional individual is Amanda Bean, a senior at Morristown-
Hamblen High School East, who recently received the Student Achievement Award from the
Tennessee School Board Association, a most befitting honor that recognizes her academic
achievements, astute leadership, and community service; and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Ms. Bean has earned an exemplary
grade point average and participated in numerous extracurricular activities, including cross
country, FCCLA, and Interact Club, which she was a key part of founding; and
WHEREAS, an incredibly talented musician, Amanda Bean is an East Advanced Chorus
