Section 1:
By Littleton
SENATE BILL 678
By White
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 39,
Chapter 13, Part 5, relative to sexual offenses.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-13-504(b), is amended by
deleting the subsection and substituting:
(b)
(1) Except as provided by subdivision (b)(2), aggravated sexual battery is
a Class B felony.
(2) Aggravated sexual battery is a Class A felony if the victim was less
than eighteen (18) years of age or was mentally defective, mentally
incapacitated, or physically helpless.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
