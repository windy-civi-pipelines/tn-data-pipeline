SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 355
By Yager
A RESOLUTION to honor Anna Collins upon her outstanding
service to the Senate Republican Caucus Press
Secretary's office.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize
certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment
of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Anna Collins is one such individual, who has distinguished herself in her
role as bill clerk for the Senate Republican Caucus Press Secretary's office; and
WHEREAS, during her tenure on Capitol Hill, Ms. Collins has evidenced great
enthusiasm for state government in general and the legislative process in particular; and
WHEREAS, Anna has earned the respect and admiration of her Senate colleagues and
supervisors for her attention to detail and her dedication to performing quality work; and
WHEREAS, she has become an expert at taking flattering photographs of Senators
during the 2025 session, always making sure to snap only photos of Senator Yager's good side;
and
WHEREAS, Anna is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Economics at the University of
Mississippi; when, and if, session finally concludes, she will refocus on her graduate research
on political economy; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Collins earned her Bachelor of Science degree in Agribusiness from
Middle Tennessee State University (MTSU), graduating summa cum laude with a minor in
economics as the Outstanding Agribusiness Senior and Chair of Excellence Urban and
Regional Planning Scholar; and

WHEREAS, during her matriculation at MTSU, she received the Buchanan Fellowship,
Phi Kappa Phi Pioneer Award, and 2024 Outstanding Honors Student recognition; and
WHEREAS, Anna Collins also served as the coordinator of the SOAR program at
MTSU, developing the research skills of college and high school students, and as an
ambassador for the School of Agriculture; and
WHEREAS, she has presented her undergraduate research at numerous conferences
and studied agriculture abroad in Argentina; and
WHEREAS, active in the Tennessee FFA, Anna previously served as the organization's
State Reporter and earned the American FFA Degree, which is awarded to only one percent of
FFA members; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2025 legislative session, Anna Collins has demonstrated
professionalism, organization, courtesy, and kindness to all, and she will be greatly missed by
the Senate Republican Caucus and its staff; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend Anna Collins upon her
outstanding service to the Senate Republican Caucus Press Secretary's office, and although we
congratulate her upon pursuing studies in a selective graduate program at Ole Miss and wish
her great success, we remind her that we will not be cheering on the Rebels. Go Vols!
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
