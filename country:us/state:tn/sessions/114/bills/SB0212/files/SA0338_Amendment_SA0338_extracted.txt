Section 1:
Senate State and Local Government 3
Amendment No. 3 to SB0212
Briggs
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 8-44-110, is amended by deleting the
language "state governing body or a local government legislative body" wherever it may appear
and substituting "state governing body or a local governmental body", and is further amended
by deleting the language "may" in subsection (d) and substituting "shall".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 8-44-110, is amended by deleting
subsection (e) and substituting the following language:
(e) As used in this section, "local governmental body" means:
(1) The legislative body of an incorporated city or town, a metropolitan
government, or a county;
(2) A regional or municipal planning commission;
(3) A board of zoning appeals;
(4) A public utility board;
(5) An industrial development corporation board of directors;
(6) A housing authority;
(7) A regional or municipal airport authority;
(8) A county election commission; and
(9) The budget committee of a legislative body of an incorporated city or
town, a metropolitan government, or a county.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
