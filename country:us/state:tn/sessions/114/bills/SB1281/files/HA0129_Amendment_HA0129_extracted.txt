Section 1:
House Commerce 1
Amendment No. 1 to HB1309
Vaughan
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting the amendatory language of SECTION 1 and substituting:
(e)
(1) Interest income accruing on investments and deposits as a result of
subsection (a) must be credited to a separate account for the purpose of carrying out
this section, must not revert to the general fund, and must be carried forward into each
subsequent fiscal year.
(2) This subsection (e) is deleted on June 30, 2029.
