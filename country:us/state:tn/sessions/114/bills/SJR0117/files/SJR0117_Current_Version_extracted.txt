Section 1:
SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 117
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Stefan Maupin to
the advisory board for the University of
Tennessee at Martin.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, established an advisory
board for the University of Tennessee at Martin; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that the governor
shall appoint five members to such advisory board; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Stefan Maupin to the advisory board for
the University of Tennessee at Martin for a term that expires on June 30, 2028; and
WHEREAS, Stefan Maupin serves as the executive director of the Tennessee Soybean
Promotion Council, where he is responsible for assisting the council in managing the investment
of soy checkoff dollars that are used to fund soybean research, market development, and
education; and
WHEREAS, a graduate of the University of Tennessee at Martin, Mr. Maupin worked for
the Tennessee Farm Bureau Federation for twenty-seven years, serving as the director of the
public policy division for the last eleven of those years; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-501, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the advisory board for the University of Tennessee at Martin shall
be confirmed by the general assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby confirm the appointment of Stefan
