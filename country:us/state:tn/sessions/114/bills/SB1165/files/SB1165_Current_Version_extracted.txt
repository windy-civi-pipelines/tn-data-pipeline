Section 1:
SENATE BILL 1165
By Akbari
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 2,
relative to a ballot question.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Each county election commission shall include on the November 2026
election ballot the following question:
Should the State of Tennessee legalize the sale, possession, and use of medical
marijuana? YES or NO

Section 3:
SECTION 2. The secretary of state shall compile the results of the public policy opinion
poll in Section 1 and forward the results to each member of the general assembly.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. The outcome of the public policy opinion poll is not binding on the general
assembly.

Section 5:
SECTION 4. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
