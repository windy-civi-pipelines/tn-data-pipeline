HOUSE RESOLUTION 97
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Raya Whitlock on
being named the 2024 WILLCO Awards Dance
Athlete of the Year.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable individual is Raya Whitlock of Centennial High School,
an athlete of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who has proven herself a formidable
competitor in dance; and
WHEREAS, in recognition of her success in athletic competition throughout the school
year, Raya Whitlock was recognized as the Dance Athlete of the Year at the 2024 WILLCO
Awards celebration; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Whitlock led the Cougars as team captain; and
WHEREAS, Raya Whitlock epitomizes all that is good in today's student athlete, as she
has achieved a high level of success in sports while evincing great character; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Whitlock's notable achievements are a source of great pride for her
family, her community, and her school, and it is appropriate that we recognize this accomplished
athlete; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
congratulate Raya Whitlock on being named the 2024 WILLCO Awards Dance Athlete of the
Year, wishing her much success and happiness in her future endeavors.
HR0097

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
