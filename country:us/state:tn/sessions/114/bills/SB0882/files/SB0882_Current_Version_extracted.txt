Section 1:
By Boyd
SENATE BILL 882
By Reeves
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 68,
relative to the health facilities commission.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-11-203(b)(F)(iii), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting:
(iii) One (1) representative of the assisted-care living facility industry who may be
appointed from lists of qualified persons submitted by interested groups, including the
Tennessee Center for Assisted Living; and

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
