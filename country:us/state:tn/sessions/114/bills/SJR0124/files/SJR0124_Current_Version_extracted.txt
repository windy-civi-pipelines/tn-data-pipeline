SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 124
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to honor Aaron Loy on winning the Tennessee
Farm Bureau Young Farmers and Ranchers
State Discussion Meet contest.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should recognize
those citizens who are wholly devoted to preserving this State's proud traditions in agriculture
while working to improve and enrich their local communities; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Aaron Loy of Jefferson County, who recently won the
Tennessee Farm Bureau Young Farmers and Ranchers State Discussion Meet contest at the
organization's 103rd annual meeting, held in Franklin; and
WHEREAS, the Discussion Meet showcases competitors' knowledge of pertinent issues
that face farmers and the agriculture industry and suggested solutions; and
WHEREAS, as a State winner, Mr. Loy advanced to the national competition in San
Antonio, Texas, at the American Farm Bureau Convention; and
WHEREAS, Aaron Loy is proudly continuing and advancing Tennessee's agricultural
heritage while working to improve conditions and production for the farmers of Jefferson County,
and it is most fitting that he be specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Aaron Loy on winning
the Tennessee Farm Bureau Young Farmers and Ranchers State Discussion Meet contest,
extending our gratitude for his ongoing commitment to farming in his community and in his
family and our best wishes for much continued success.

BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
