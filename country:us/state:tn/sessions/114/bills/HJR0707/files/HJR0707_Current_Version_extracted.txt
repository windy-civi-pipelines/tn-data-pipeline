HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 707
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to provide for the adjournment of the 2025
session of the One Hundred Fourteenth General
Assembly.
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that the 2025 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth General Assembly be
adjourned at the close of business in both houses on Tuesday, April 22, 2025, and that the 2026
session of the One Hundred Fourteenth General Assembly be convened at 12:00 noon (CST)
on Tuesday, January 13, 2026.
