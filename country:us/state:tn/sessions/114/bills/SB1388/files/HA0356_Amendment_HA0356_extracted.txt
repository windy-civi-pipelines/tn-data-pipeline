Amendment No. 2 to HB1066

by adding the following as a new section in the amendatory language of SECTION 1:
71-4-512.
(a) Notwithstanding this part to the contrary, priority must not be given to the
department, an eligible individual with a disability, or a blind vendor unless the proposal
of the department, individual, or vendor to operate a vending facility or vending machine,
when considered with all other proposals after a bidding process, is found to be
competitive in terms of quality of service, pricing of merchandise, and the rate of
commission or the rent to be paid.
(b) A proposal described in subsection (a) is not competitive if the proposed
payment of annual commissions, rental fees, or a combination of annual commission
and rental fees, is not within two percent (2%) of that submitted by an organization that
would otherwise be able to operate a vending facility or vending machine on the public
property.
