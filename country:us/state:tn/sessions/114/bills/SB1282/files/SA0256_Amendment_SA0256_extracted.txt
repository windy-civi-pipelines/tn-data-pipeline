Section 1:
Senate Health and Welfare 1
Amendment No. 1 to SB1282
Crowe
Signature of Sponsor
by adding the following as a new subsection (d) in SECTION 5 and redesignating the existing
subsection (d) and the remaining subsection accordingly:
(d) The permit fee, as established by rule under this section, may be increased
up to three percent (3%) during a calendar year and up to five percent (5%) during a
five-year period beginning January 1, 2026, and during each subsequent five-year
period. For purposes of this section, the permit fee is established on January 1, 2026,
and any increase is assessed upon renewal of the food service establishment permit or
upon application for a new food service establishment permit.
