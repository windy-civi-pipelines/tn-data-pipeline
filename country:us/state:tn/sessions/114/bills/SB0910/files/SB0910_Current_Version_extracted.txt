Section 1:
By Cepicky
SENATE BILL 910
By Lowe
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49,
relative to interscholastic athletics.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-2-136(b), is amended by deleting
subdivision (2) and substituting:
Upon request of the education committee of the senate or the committee of
house of representatives having jurisdiction over education, the department shall submit
a report to the requesting committee documenting each LEA's and public charter
school's compliance with this section.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
