SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-4-701(b), is amended by deleting
subdivisions (2) and (3) and substituting:
(2) "Eligible candidate" means a student who is:
(A) Enrolled in an approved EPP:
(i) In the student's junior or senior year as determined by the
institution in which the student is enrolled as an undergraduate student; or
(ii) As a post-baccalaureate student, if the student is seeking an
initial educator license; and
(B) A citizen of the United States who has resided in this state for at least
one (1) year prior to the student's admission to the eligible postsecondary
institution at which the student is enrolled;
(3) "Eligible postsecondary institution" has the same meaning as defined in ยง 49-
4-902;

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-4-701(b), is amended by deleting
subdivision (4) and redesignating subsequent subdivisions accordingly.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-4-701, is amended by deleting
subsections (d)-(i) and substituting:
(d) To be eligible for a Tennessee Future Teacher scholarship, an eligible
candidate must:
(1) Be admitted to and enrolled in an approved EPP;

(2) Complete the scholarship application, as required by TSAC; and
(3) Sign a promissory note agreeing to teach in an LEA or public charter
school in this state for at least two (2) consecutive years in a targeted setting.
(e) If an eligible candidate ceases to be eligible for the Tennessee Future
Teacher scholarship at any time for any reason, then the eligible candidate is not eligible
to regain the scholarship.
(f) If an eligible candidate is awarded a Tennessee Future Teacher scholarship,
then the eligible candidate may continue to receive a Tennessee Future Teacher
scholarship under this section until the occurrence of the first of the following events:
(1) The eligible candidate has attained a degree through an approved
EPP;
(2) The eligible candidate no longer meets the standards established by
the state board of education for admission to the eligible candidate's approved
EPP or the standards of the approved EPP in which the eligible candidate is
enrolled; or
(3) Three (3) years have elapsed since the date of the eligible student's
enrollment in a term for which the eligible student received the eligible
candidate's first Tennessee Future Teacher scholarship.
(g) An eligible candidate who receives a Tennessee Future Teacher scholarship
may transfer from one (1) eligible postsecondary institution to another eligible
postsecondary institution without loss of the scholarship so long as the eligible candidate
continues to meet all eligibility requirements for the scholarship
(h) Subject to the amounts appropriated by the general assembly, the amount of
the Tennessee Future Teacher scholarship is three thousand five hundred dollars
($3,500). A Tennessee Future Teacher scholarship may be awarded to an eligible
candidate each academic year the eligible candidate is enrolled in an approved EPP.
- 2 - 005301
(i) If an eligible candidate receives a Tennessee Future Teacher scholarship, but
does not teach in a targeted setting for two (2) consecutive years, then the eligible
candidate must reimburse TSAC for each year the eligible candidate did not teach in a
targeted setting in an amount equal to the total scholarship amount the eligible candidate
received pursuant to this section divided by two (2), without interest.

Section 5:
SECTION 4. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
