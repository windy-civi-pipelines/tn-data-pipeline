SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 196
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to recognize Elena Pate of Unicoi County Middle
School upon her selection as a 2024–2025
Kiwanis Club Teacher of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest-quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Elena Pate is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished herself
as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, an educator at Unicoi County Middle School (UCMS), Elena Pate was
recently named a 2024–2025 Teacher of the Year by the Kiwanis Club of Erwin; and
WHEREAS, for the past two years, Ms. Pate has played a key role in the school's 4-H
programs, as well as serving as the main instructor for the BB shooting program and co-
instructor for the archery program; she leads the STARS after-school program and supports
Unicoi County Middle School sports, band, gymnastics, and drama activities; and
WHEREAS, Elena Pate is also a member of the school-wide leadership team and the
UCMS social committee, and she oversees the annual Veterans Day program; in addition, she
helps lead the Student Life Committee, a student-driven group focused on improving UCMS;
and
WHEREAS, Ms. Pate is an extremely talented and compassionate educator who has
worked assiduously throughout her career in education to motivate her students, fellow
teachers, and staff to reach their full potential as scholars, professionals, and citizens; and

WHEREAS, Elena Pate truly serves as an inspiration to the faculty and staff at Unicoi
County Middle School, and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are
fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Pate epitomizes the dedicated educator who is wholly committed to the
highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Elena Pate on
being chosen as a 2024–2025 Teacher of the Year by the Kiwanis Club of Erwin, commend her
exceptional service to the children of Tennessee, salute her professional expertise and personal
excellence, and wish her much continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
