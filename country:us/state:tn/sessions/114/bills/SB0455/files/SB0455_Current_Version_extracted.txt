Section 1:
By Scarbrough
SENATE BILL 455
By Rose
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 40,
Chapter 35, Part 5, relative to parole.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 40-35-503(b)(2)(A), is amended by
deleting the subdivision and substituting the following:
(2)
(A) The release from custody at the time would depreciate the
seriousness of the crime of which the incarcerated individual stands convicted or
promote disrespect for the law;

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
