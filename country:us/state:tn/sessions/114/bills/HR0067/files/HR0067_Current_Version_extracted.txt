HOUSE RESOLUTION 67
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to recognize and congratulate George
Dagermangy, winner of the 2024 Secretary of
State's civics essay contest.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we recognize those industrious, talented students of
Tennessee who bring pride and honor to our State; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is George Dagermangy, who recently won first place
among all students in grades K-2 statewide in the 2024 Secretary of State's civics essay
contest; and
WHEREAS, a resident of Williamson County and a student at St. Henry School, George
was one of twelve overall winners across all grade levels; a total of 4,356 students participated
in the contest, with the theme "Why Trusted Sources Matter"; and
WHEREAS, in winning first-place in the essay contest, George Dagermangy will receive
a $500 TNStars 529 College Savings Program scholarship and a trip to the State Capitol; and
WHEREAS, additionally, George will serve as a House Page on the morning of April 3,
2025, in recognition of his notable achievement; and
WHEREAS, George's recent victory is a great source of pride for his family, his school,
and his community; and
WHEREAS, George Dagermangy's display of aptitude and ambition surely bodes well
for his future success, and his dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of our respect,
admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we commend
and congratulate George Dagermangy on his statewide victory in the Secretary of State's 2024
