Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 29-20-403(b), is amended by adding
the following as new subdivisions:
(5) Minimum limits of not less than four hundred thousand dollars ($400,000) for
bodily injury or death of any one (1) person in any one (1) accident, occurrence, or act,
and not less than eight hundred fifty thousand dollars ($850,000) for bodily injury or
death of all persons in any one (1) accident, occurrence, or act, and one hundred fifty
thousand dollars ($150,000) for injury or destruction of property of others in any one (1)
accident, occurrence, or act. This subdivision (b)(5) shall apply to any action arising on
or after July 1, 2025, but before July 1, 2027; and
(6) Minimum limits of not less than five hundred thousand dollars ($500,000) for
bodily injury or death of any one (1) person in any one (1) accident, occurrence, or act,
and not less than one million dollars ($1,000,000) for bodily injury or death of all persons
in any one (1) accident, occurrence, or act, and two hundred thousand dollars
($200,000) for injury or destruction of property of others in any one (1) accident,
occurrence, or act. This subdivision (b)(6) shall apply to any action arising on or after
July 1, 2027.
