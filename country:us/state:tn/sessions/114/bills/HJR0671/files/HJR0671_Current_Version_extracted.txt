HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 671
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Blair Baugus of
Wayne County High School upon being named
TSSAA Class 1A Miss Basketball.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should recognize
those incredibly successful student athletes who have distinguished themselves as inspirational
leaders among their peers; and
WHEREAS, a statewide committee of sportswriters recently selected Blair Baugus of
Wayne County High School (WCHS) as TSSAA Class 1A Miss Basketball based upon her
outstanding performance on the hardwood during the 2024-2025 season; and
WHEREAS, her superior academic record and upstanding character were also taken
into consideration when the committee selected Ms. Baugus to receive this prestigious award,
which honors the best Class 1A female basketball player in the entire State; and
WHEREAS, the daughter of Jerrod and Ginger Baugus, Blair Baugus will graduate with
honors from WCHS on May 16, 2025; she is ranked ninth in her class with an overall grade
point average of 97.7; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Baugus is currently the leading scorer in WCHS history, with 2,539
points scored during her four-year career; a four-year starter for the Lady Cats, she helped lead
her teams to a record of 132 wins against only 13 losses; and
WHEREAS, she also amassed over 1,000 rebounds and 50 double-doubles in her
career at WCHS; and
WHEREAS, Blair Baugus was recently named the 2025 BCAT Player of the Year and
the Times Daily Player of the Year; she is a two-time Division I Miss Basketball recipient, with
the first coming during her sophomore season in 2023; and

WHEREAS, her talent and dedication certainly bode well for her collegiate basketball
career, which she will begin this fall as a student at Middle Tennessee State University; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Baugus exemplifies the spirit, determination, and perseverance for
which Tennesseans are notable, and she is richly deserving of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Blair Baugus of Wayne County High School
upon being named TSSAA Class 1A Miss Basketball and wish her much continued success
both on and off the court.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
