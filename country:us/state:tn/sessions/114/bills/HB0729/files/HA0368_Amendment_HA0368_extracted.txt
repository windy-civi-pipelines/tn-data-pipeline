SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 39-17-307(c), is amended by adding
the following new subdivisions:
(4) Notwithstanding subdivision (c)(1), a violation of subdivision (a)(1) is a Class
D felony if the defendant intentionally obstructs a highway, street, or other place used for
the passage of vehicles or conveyances.
(5) If a violation of subdivision (a)(1) meets the requirements of subdivisions
(c)(3) and (c)(4), then the offense may be prosecuted under either subdivision.
