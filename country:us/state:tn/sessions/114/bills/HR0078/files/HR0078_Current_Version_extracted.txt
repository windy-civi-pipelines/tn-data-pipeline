HOUSE RESOLUTION 78
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Rachel Lytle upon her
receipt of the Outstanding Biology Teacher
award for the State of Tennessee.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Rachel Lytle is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished herself
as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Lytle recently received the Outstanding Biology Teacher award for the
State of Tennessee, a most befitting honor that recognizes the positive influence she has had
on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, an educator at Brentwood High School, Ms. Lytle is an extremely talented
and compassionate biology teacher who works assiduously to motivate her students to reach
their full potential, both as scholars and as citizens; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Lytle serves as an inspiration to her colleagues and students alike, and
her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Rachel Lytle truly epitomizes the dedicated teacher who is wholly
committed to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be
specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby
honor and congratulate Rachel Lytle upon her receipt of the Outstanding Biology Teacher award
for the State of Tennessee, commend her exceptional service to the children of Tennessee,

endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
