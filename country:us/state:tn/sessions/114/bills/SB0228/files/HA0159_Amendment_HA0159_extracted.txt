by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. This act is known and may be cited as the "Law Enforcement's Safer
Tennessee Electronic Registration (LESTER) Act."

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-4-138, is amended by designating
the existing language as subsection (a), deleting the last sentence in such new subsection (a),
and adding the following new subsections:
(b) If a person displays the evidence in an electronic format pursuant to
subsection (a), then the evidence must be accepted by law enforcement as proof of
registration, unless:
(1) The image is not clear enough for the officer to determine the
information; or
(2) The officer believes the image or the information contained in the
image is fraudulent or inaccurate.
(c) An officer may require additional information to verify the validity of the
electronic registration.
(d) If an officer cannot verify the validity of the electronic registration, then the
officer is not required to accept the electronic registration.

(e) If a person displays the evidence in an electronic format pursuant to
subsection (a), then the display is not consent for law enforcement to access any other

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 55-10-312(a), is amended by deleting
the language "motor-propelled vehicle" and "motor propelled vehicle" wherever it appears and
substituting instead the language "motor vehicle".

Section 5:
SECTION 4. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
