by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 8-44-112, is amended by designating
subsection (a) as subdivision (a)(1) and by adding the following as subdivision (a)(2):
(2)
(A) A local governing body shall, for each public meeting, reserve a
period for public comment to provide the public with the opportunity to comment:
(i) On matters that are germane to the items on the agenda for
the meeting; and
(ii) On any matter that is germane to the jurisdiction of the local
governing body, regardless of whether such matter is an item on the
agenda for the meeting.
(B) As used in this subdivision (a)(2), "local governing body" means the
governing body of an incorporated city or town, county, metropolitan government,
school district, regional authority, or other political subdivision of this state other
than a state governmental agency or entity.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
