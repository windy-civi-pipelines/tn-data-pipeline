Section 1:
By Hawk
SENATE BILL 913
By Lowe
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 4,
Chapter 29; Title 49, Chapter 7; Title 49, Chapter
8 and Title 49, Chapter 9, relative to the
composition of governing boards of institutions of
higher education.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201(f)(6), is amended by
deleting subdivision (D) and substituting:
(D) Members of a governing body for another institution of higher education.

Section 3:
SECTION 2.
If, on the effective date of this act, a state university board member is serving on
the governing board for another institution of higher education, then the member's seat
on the state university board is vacated on the effective date of this act. The appropriate
appointing authority shall fill the vacancy for the remainder of the term.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
