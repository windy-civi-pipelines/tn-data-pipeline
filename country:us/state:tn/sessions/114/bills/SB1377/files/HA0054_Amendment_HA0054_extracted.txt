SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 50, Chapter 1, is amended by adding
the following as a new part:
50-1-1101.
This part is known and may be cited as the "Voluntary Portable Benefit Act."
50-1-1102.
As used in this part:
(1) "Benefit plan" includes:
(A) Health insurance;
(B) Income replacement insurance;
(C) Life insurance;
(D) Retirement benefits; and
(E) Other benefits that may customarily be provided as part of a
benefit plan;
(2) "Employee" means an individual who performs services for an
employer for wages, and the services performed by the individual qualify as an
employer-employee relationship with the employer based upon consideration of
the twenty-factor test described in § 50-2-111;
(3) "Hiring party" means a person who hires or enters into a contract with
an independent contractor;

(5) "Portable benefit account" means an account opened by an

50-1-1103.
(a) An individual or entity, including a hiring party, whether public or private, an
