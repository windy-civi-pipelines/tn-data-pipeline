SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 529
By Bailey
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Camdyn Holbrook, 2025
Valedictorian of Stone Memorial High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Camdyn Holbrook is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named Valedictorian of the 2025 graduating class at Stone Memorial High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Holbrook has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Camdyn Holbrook has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Holbrook's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes
well for her future success; and
WHEREAS, Camdyn Holbrook's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of
pride for her family, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Holbrook's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor the 2025 Stone
Memorial High School Valedictorian, Camdyn Holbrook, on her impressive academic
achievement and extend to her our best wishes for a happy, successful future.

the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
