SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 396
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to recognize and commend the members of the
Collinwood High School Winter Guard team
upon their success at the Southeastern Color
Guard Circuit.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to specially recognize
those young people who, through their natural talents and extraordinary efforts, bring the joys of
artistic expression and culture to the citizens of this State while promoting the importance of the
performing arts; and
WHEREAS, one such exemplary team of individuals is the Collinwood High School
Winter Guard, whose members have proven themselves to be the fiercest of competitors and
truly worthy of admiration in their success at the Southeastern Color Guard Circuit; and
WHEREAS, the Collinwood High School Winter Guard placed first in Tennessee and
finished as the overall runner-up among all competitors at the Southeastern Color Guard Circuit;
and
WHEREAS, the members of the Collinwood High School Winter Guard include Jacie
Brewer, Lily Darnell, Jaylee Moore, Savannah Mickey, DJ Risner, Gabriella Stults, Ryan Tucker,
Brianna Weeks, Emma Weeks, and Clara West; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young performers not only made possible a successful season but also surely bode well for their
future success; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud the members of the
Collinwood High School Color Guard for their outstanding performance; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
SJR0396
006938
- 1 -
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we recognize and commend the members of the
Collinwood High School Color Guard upon their success at the Southeastern Color Guard
Circuit, applaud their dedication to excellence, and extend to them our best wishes for every
continued success in the future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 006938
