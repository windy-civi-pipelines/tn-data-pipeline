Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-1-228, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
The department of education shall not exclude from the calculation of a school
letter grade:
(1) The data of any group of students within a school if the department
collects, aggregates, and reports the data, and if the group of students has at
least twenty (20) valid test scores in math or English language arts for at least
one (1) grade band; and
(2) Data for the college and career readiness indicator if the school has
at least twenty (20) students in a graduating cohort.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
