Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 41-2-152, is amended by deleting
subsection (b) and substituting instead:
(b) If the prisoner is released for paid employment, then the cost of the electronic
monitoring device must be deducted from the prisoner's wages by the sheriff and paid
directly to the electronic monitoring device provider by the county or state, as applicable.
If a prisoner is released for unpaid work, then the entity utilizing the prisoner for work
shall pay the costs of the electronic monitoring device.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 41-2-152(a), is amended by deleting
the language "beginning January 1, 2024,".

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect July 1, 2025, the public welfare requiring it.
