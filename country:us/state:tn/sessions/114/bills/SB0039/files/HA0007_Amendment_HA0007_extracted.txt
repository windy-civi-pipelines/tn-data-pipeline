House State & Local Government 1
Amendment No. 1 to HB0056
Crawford
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting the first sentence of the amendatory language in Section 1 and substituting:
Notwithstanding a charter, resolution, or another law to the contrary, a county
legislative body may, by a two-thirds (2/3) vote of the legislative body, remove any
member of an appointed board or commission, if such removal is recommended by the
appointing authority.
