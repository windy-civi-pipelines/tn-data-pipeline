HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 432
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Zareena Jones, 2025
Valedictorian of Mt. Pleasant High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Zareena Jones is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
Valedictorian of the 2025 graduating class at Mt. Pleasant High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Jones has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Zareena Jones has earned an
exemplary grade point average of 100.12962 while participating in numerous extracurricular
activities, including Key Club, HOSA, student government, Best Buddies, and Beta Club, as well
as captaining the volleyball and basketball teams and acting as a Girls State delegate; and
WHEREAS, Zareena Jones's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes
well for her future success in college, where she plans to pursue a double degree in criminology
and legal studies; and
WHEREAS, her noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for her family,
her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Jones's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
HJR0432
004850
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor the 2025 Mt. Pleasant High School
Valedictorian, Zareena Jones, on her impressive academic achievement and extend to her our
best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
