HOUSE RESOLUTION 91
By Bulso
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Rob Pautienus on
being named the 2024 WILLCO Awards Female
Sports Coach of the Year.
WHEREAS, we take great pleasure in paying tribute to those outstanding coaches who,
through hard work and commitment, have distinguished themselves among the storied coaches
and high school programs of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Rob Pautienus, coach of the Brentwood High School girls' cross country
team, was recently named Female Sports Coach of the Year at the 2024 WILLCO Awards; and
WHEREAS, his selection is an honor that truly reflects the commitment to athletic
excellence displayed by Coach Pautienus and his outstanding team; and
WHEREAS, under his astute leadership, the Brentwood Bruins brought home their fourth
consecutive Class AAA cross country championship title, bringing to a close another unbeaten
year for the team; and
WHEREAS, Rob Pautienus epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are characteristic
of a true Tennessean; he has served the Brentwood High School girls' cross country program
with great diligence and dedication, positively influencing the lives of countless young people
with his display of character and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Coach Pautienus has distinguished himself as a great coach, tactician, and
motivator of young athletes; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud Rob Pautienus for his
outstanding leadership as coach of the Brentwood High School girls' cross country team; now,
therefore,
HR0091
006639
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and
congratulate Rob Pautienus on being named the 2024 WILLCO Awards Female Sports Coach
of the Year and extend to him our best wishes for much continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
