Section 1:
By Taylor
HOUSE BILL 1226
By Lafferty
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 7 and
Title 68, relative to the world health organization.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-51-209(a)(4)(C)(i), is amended by
deleting "the World Health Organization or".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-2-611(a)(1), is amended by
deleting "world health organization" and substituting "federal centers for disease control and
prevention".

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.

SENATE BILL 669
By Taylor
HOUSE BILL 1226
By Lafferty
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 7 and
Title 68, relative to the world health organization.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-51-209(a)(4)(C)(i), is amended by
deleting "the World Health Organization or".
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 68-2-611(a)(1), is amended by
deleting "world health organization" and substituting "federal centers for disease control and
prevention".
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
