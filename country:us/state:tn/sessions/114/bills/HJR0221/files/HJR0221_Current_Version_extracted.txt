HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 221
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Cassandra Cooper
Smith upon her induction into the Clay County
Sports Hall of Fame.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly pay tribute to those gifted athletes,
coaches, and administrators who have attained the very pinnacle of sporting achievement; and
WHEREAS, Cassandra Cooper Smith is one such legendary athlete, who was recently
inducted into the Clay County Sports Hall of Fame; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Smith, a standout athlete at Clay County High School from 2009 to
2012, excelled in both volleyball and basketball, and she played a key role in the Lady Bulldogs'
2012 state championship in basketball; and
WHEREAS, as a two-year starter on the hardwood for the Lady Bulldogs, she scored
778 points and pulled down 467 rebounds in her career; and
WHEREAS, Cassandra Smith started all four years in volleyball and helped her team
reach the region tournament each year; and
WHEREAS, she received numerous all-conference honors in both sports, highlighted by
a volleyball District Tournament MVP award and 1st Team selection in basketball and volleyball;
and
WHEREAS, as a senior in basketball, Mrs. Smith was named to the All-State and All-
Upper Cumberland teams, made the State All-Tournament team, and received offers to play
both sports at the collegiate level; and
WHEREAS, Cassandra Smith demonstrated leadership, skill, and athleticism throughout
her athletic career, and it is fitting that we should honor her on this special occasion; now,
therefore,
HJR0221
003507
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Cassandra Cooper Smith upon her induction
into the Clay County Sports Hall of Fame and extend to her our best wishes for much continued
success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
- 2 - 003507
