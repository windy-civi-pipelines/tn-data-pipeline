HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 220
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Rob Edwards upon
his induction into the Clay County Sports Hall of
Fame.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly pay tribute to those gifted athletes,
coaches, and administrators who have attained the very pinnacle of sporting achievement; and
WHEREAS, Rob Edwards is one such legendary coach, who was recently inducted into
the Clay County Sports Hall of Fame; and
WHEREAS, an iconic figure within the annals of the Clay County High School boys'
basketball program, Coach Edwards has guided the Bulldogs to the highest levels of success
over the past twenty-five years; and
WHEREAS, the longest-tenured and winningest coach in program history, Rob Edwards
began the 2024-2025 season with 538 wins, having captured fourteen district crowns and nine
region titles, appeared in fourteen sectionals, qualified for nine state tournaments, claimed one
state runner-up finish, and won the 2021 state championship; and
WHEREAS, no stranger to awards and accolades, Coach Edwards has received
numerous coach of the year accolades, including statewide recognitions, and the basketball
court at Clay County High School is named in his honor; and
WHEREAS, Rob Edwards has positively influenced the lives of countless young people
with his display of character and integrity throughout his coaching career, and it is fitting that we
should honor him on this special occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Rob Edwards upon his induction into the Clay

happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
