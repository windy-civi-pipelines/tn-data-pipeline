SENATE RESOLUTION 131
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend Courtney Bingham on
being named a 2025 Sterling Award winner by
the Jackson Area Business Professional
Women.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should salute those
citizens who, through their extraordinary efforts, have distinguished themselves as community
leaders of whom we can all be proud; and
WHEREAS, one such estimable person is Courtney Bingham, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Bingham was recently named a 2025 Sterling Award winner by the
Jackson Area Business and Professional Women and duly recognized as one of twenty Most
Influential Women in West Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Bingham currently serves as a systems operations analyst at Jackson
Energy Authority (JEA), in which position she has earned the respect and admiration of her
colleagues and community alike for the superior quality of her work; and
WHEREAS, in addition to her current position at JEA, Ms. Bingham excels as an
entrepreneur and the owner of Mimi's Boutique; and
WHEREAS, Courtney Bingham is a well-respected member of her community and her
profession, and she epitomizes the spirit and commitment that are characteristic of a true
Tennessean; and
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud Ms. Bingham for her
many contributions to the good people of Tennessee, her unwavering commitment to others,
and her astute leadership in Chester County; now, therefore,
SR0131
007048
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and commend
Courtney Bingham on being named a 2025 Sterling Award winner by the Jackson Area
Business and Professional Women and extend to her our best wishes for every future success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
- 2 - 007048
