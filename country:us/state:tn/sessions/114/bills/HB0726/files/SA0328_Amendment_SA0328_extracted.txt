SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 67, Chapter 5, Part 6, is amended by
deleting subdivision (b)(1)(A) from the section added by Section 1 of Chapter 6 of the Public
Acts of the First Extraordinary Session of 2025, and substituting instead the following:
(A) Was destroyed or damaged by a qualified disaster, as determined by the
assessor of property by April 15, 2025; and

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 67-5-1601(a)(3), is amended by
deleting the language "in lieu of" and substituting instead the language "with or without".

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 67-5-1601(b)(3), is amended by
deleting the last sentence of the subdivision.

Section 5:
SECTION 4. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
