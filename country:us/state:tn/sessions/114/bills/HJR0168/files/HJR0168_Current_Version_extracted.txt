Section 1:
HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 168
By Lamberth
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Donnie Smith to
the board of trustees of the University of
Tennessee.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-201, provides for the governance
of the University of Tennessee by the board of trustees; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-202, provides that the governor
shall appoint ten members to the board of trustees of the University of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Donnie Smith to the board of trustees of
the University of Tennessee to a term that expires June 30, 2030; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Smith is the former chief executive officer and chairman of Foster
Farms and the former president and chief executive officer of Tyson Foods, Inc., one of the
world's largest food companies; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Smith earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Animal Science from the
University of Tennessee, Knoxville; and
WHEREAS, in 2014, he and his wife, Terry, pledged $3.2 million to the University of
Tennessee (UT) to establish an endowment for international sustainable agriculture, and in
2018, the Smiths funded the Smith Center for International Sustainable Agriculture at UT's
Institute of Agriculture; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Smith brings valuable experience and knowledge to the board of
trustees; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-9-202, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the board of trustees of the University of Tennessee shall be
confirmed by joint resolution of the General Assembly; now, therefore,
HJR0168
001534
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that this body does hereby confirm the appointment of Donnie Smith to the
board of trustees of the University of Tennessee for a term that expires June 30, 2030.
