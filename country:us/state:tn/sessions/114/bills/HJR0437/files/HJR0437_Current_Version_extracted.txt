HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 437
By Capley
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Carstan Drew Bevis,
2025 Top Ten Senior at Wayne County High
School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Carstan Drew Bevis is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named a Top Ten Senior of the 2025 graduating class at Wayne County High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Bevis has achieved outstanding academic success
and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country; and
WHEREAS, in his remarkable high school career, Carstan Bevis has earned an
exemplary grade point average of 98.405 while participating in numerous extracurricular
activities, including the Wildcat football and baseball teams, the Beta Club, Math Club, FCA, and
Interact; and
WHEREAS, admired as a leader among his peers, Carstan Bevis served as president of
his senior class, and he was voted "Most Likely to Succeed" and Mr. WCHS; and
WHEREAS, his display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for his
future success as a student at the University of Tennessee at Martin; and
WHEREAS, Carstan Bevis's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride
for his parents, Stan and Winnette Bevis, his school, and his community; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Bevis's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of this General Assembly; now, therefore,
HJR0437
004826
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Carstan Drew Bevis of Wayne County
High School on being named a 2025 Top Ten Senior, salute his impressive academic
achievement, and extend to him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
