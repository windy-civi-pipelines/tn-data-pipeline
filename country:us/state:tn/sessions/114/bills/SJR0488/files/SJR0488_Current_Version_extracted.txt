SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 488
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to recognize Amy Richards on her selection as
the 2024–2025 Wilson County Adult High School
Teacher of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest-quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Amy Richards is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, an adult learning teacher at Wilson County Adult High School, Ms. Richards
was recently named the school's 2024–2025 Teacher of the Year, a most befitting honor for her
tenure as an educator, as it recognizes the positive influence she has on so many lives; and
WHEREAS, Amy Richards is an extremely talented and compassionate educator who
has worked assiduously throughout her career in education to motivate her students to reach
their full potential as scholars and citizens; and
WHEREAS, a teacher with sixteen years of classroom experience, Ms. Richards holds a
Bachelor of Arts degree; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Richards truly serves as an inspiration to the faculty and staff at Wilson
County Adult High School, and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are
fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Amy Richards epitomizes the dedicated educator who is wholly committed
to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
SJR0488
007864
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Amy Richards on
being chosen as the 2024–2025 Wilson County Adult High School Teacher of the Year,
commend her exceptional service to the students of Tennessee, salute her professional
expertise and personal excellence, and wish her all the best in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
