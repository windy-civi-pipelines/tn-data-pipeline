SENATE RESOLUTION 25
By Lamar
A RESOLUTION to honor Cathy Carrillo in celebration of
Women's Day on the Hill.
WHEREAS, Women's Day on the Hill is a time to honor the achievements of those
Tennessee women who have paved the way for every person to have the opportunity to pursue
the American dream; and
WHEREAS, the Volunteer State is proud to be the home of the awe-inspiring Cathy
Carrillo, a Nashville native who has a decade of experience in nonprofit and grassroots
organizations and who now serves as the Metro Human Relations Commission's Director of
Community Engagement; and
WHEREAS, her work, both past and present, has been instrumental in preserving the
dignity, diversity, and common welfare of Nashville's citizens; and
WHEREAS, in saluting Ms. Carrillo on this special occasion, let us remember that when
women succeed, America succeeds, as does Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby honor and commend
Cathy Carrillo on her many contributions to the State of Tennessee as we celebrate Women's
Day on the Hill.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
