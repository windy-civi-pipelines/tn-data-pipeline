Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 67-4-2006(c), is amended by adding
the following as a new subdivision:
(10)
(A) Subject to subdivision (c)(10)(B), a taxpayer may, at the taxpayer's
discretion, add back to net earnings:
(i) Any amounts taken as deductions from the taxpayer's federal
taxable income that are also allowed to be taken as deductions in
determining the taxpayer's net earnings under subsection (a); and
(ii) Any amounts subtracted from net earnings under subdivision
(b)(2).
(B) Discretionary additions to net earnings made under subdivision
(c)(10)(A) may be made, adjusted, or removed at the taxpayer's discretion for
any tax year on any timely filed original or amended return; provided, however,
that adjustments made pursuant to this subdivision (c)(10) must not reduce a
taxpayer's net earnings below the amount that would have been computed for
the tax year without regard to this subdivision (c)(10).

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
