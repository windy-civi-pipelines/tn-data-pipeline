HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 679
By McCalmon
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend William Zabka.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to recognize those
individuals whose talents and dedication to the performing arts have left a lasting cultural
legacy; and
WHEREAS, William Zabka, an esteemed actor and producer, has earned acclaim for his
portrayal of Johnny Lawrence in Cobra Kai, the critically celebrated continuation of the iconic
Karate Kid franchise; and
WHEREAS, reprising a role he first made famous in 1984, Mr. Zabka brought
remarkable depth, humor, and humanity to the character of Johnny Lawrence, portraying him as
a fully realized figure whose personal growth, struggles, and redemption resonated deeply with
viewers of all ages; and
WHEREAS, throughout its run, Cobra Kai garnered numerous accolades, most notably
the 2025 Saturn Award for Best Action/Thriller Television Series, a testament to the high quality
of its production and the powerful performances of its cast, including that of William Zabka; and
WHEREAS, beyond its achievements in entertainment, Cobra Kai inspired renewed
interest in the martial arts, encouraging a new generation to explore the disciplines of karate
and self-defense, and instilling in audiences the timeless values of perseverance, discipline, and
personal growth; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Zabka's contributions to the series, both as a leading actor and creative
force, have helped reinvigorate a beloved story for contemporary audiences, offering themes of
resilience, mentorship, and second chances; and

WHEREAS, it is wholly fitting that we should honor an artist whose work continues to
resonate deeply across Tennessee and beyond; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor William Zabka for his outstanding contributions to television and
the martial arts community, commend him for his pivotal role in the success of Cobra Kai, and
extend to him our best wishes for much continued success in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
