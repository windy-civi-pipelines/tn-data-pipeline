SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 38-8-107, is amended by adding the
following as a new subsection:
(g) The commission shall also issue a certificate of compliance to a person
appointed as a private special deputy on or after January 1, 2026, if the person meets
the qualifications for employment as a police officer and satisfactorily completes an
approved recruit training program as required by this part. To retain this certification, the
private special deputy shall also successfully complete an annual in-service training
session appropriate for the private special deputy's responsibilities. As used in this
subsection (g), "private special deputy" means the same as defined in ยง 38-8-101.
