Section 1:
Senate Education 2
Amendment No. 2 to SB0817
White
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting subsection (e) in Section 2 and substituting instead the following:
(e)
(1) A school nurse or other trained school personnel authorized to
administer a bronchodilator rescue inhaler pursuant to this section is not liable for
any civil damages for ordinary negligence in acts or omissions resulting from the
administration of a bronchodilator rescue inhaler to a student believed in good
faith to be having life-threatening asthma symptoms or respiratory distress.
(2) An LEA or public school, including a public charter school, that
contracts for or employs a school nurse or other trained school personnel who
administers a bronchodilator rescue inhaler pursuant to this section is not liable
for any civil damages for ordinary negligence in acts or omissions resulting from
the administration of a bronchodilator rescue inhaler to a student believed in
good faith to be having life-threatening asthma symptoms or respiratory distress.
