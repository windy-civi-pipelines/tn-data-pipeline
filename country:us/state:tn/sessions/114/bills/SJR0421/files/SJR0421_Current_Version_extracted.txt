SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 421
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor Weston Brown on being elected
Tennessee FFA state president.
WHEREAS, organized in 1928 to promote leadership, personal growth, and career
success among high school agriculture students, FFA continues to play an important role in
ensuring the growth and prosperity of agriculture in Tennessee and the nation by expertly
molding our future leaders; and
WHEREAS, we take great pleasure in recognizing those outstanding young citizens who
have distinguished themselves as emerging leaders in Tennessee's agricultural industry; and
WHEREAS, Weston Brown of West Greene High School has been elected Tennessee
FFA state president for 2025-2026 after undergoing rigorous interviews and testing to earn this
important position; and
WHEREAS, Weston Brown is an active fifth-generation worker on his family's beef cattle
farm, so his passion for agriculture started at an early age; he has been involved in FFA since
he was fourteen years old; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Brown worked hard to prepare himself for his presidency, studying
agricultural issues, traveling all across Tennessee for courses, and meeting with industry
professionals; and
WHEREAS, his duties as Tennessee FFA state president include hosting workshops,
facilitating and working with members, attending FFA camp and conferences, and speaking and
serving as an ambassador on behalf of Tennessee FFA; and
WHEREAS, his fellow FFA members, teachers, sponsors, and, indeed, all the good
people of Greene County are very proud of Weston Brown and his accomplishment; and
SJR0421
006859
- 1 -
WHEREAS, an outstanding young man, Weston Brown has reached the very acme of
Tennessee FFA, and he is most deserving of our approbation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Weston Brown on being
elected Tennessee FFA state president, salute his many contributions and achievements in
agriculture, and extend to him our best wishes for success during his presidency.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
