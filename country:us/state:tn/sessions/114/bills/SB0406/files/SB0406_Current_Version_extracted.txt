Section 1:
By Parkinson
SENATE BILL 406
By Gardenhire
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 16;
Title 18 and Title 36, relative to orders of
protection.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 36-3-601(3)(E), is amended by
deleting "or the general sessions criminal court" and substituting ", the general sessions criminal
court, or a criminal court when the criminal court is hearing a criminal matter involving a
domestic abuse victim, sexual assault victim, or stalking victim"; and by deleting "the general
sessions court" and substituting "the criminal court or the general sessions court".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.

HOUSE BILL 361
By Parkinson
SENATE BILL 406
By Gardenhire
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 16;
Title 18 and Title 36, relative to orders of
protection.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 36-3-601(3)(E), is amended by
deleting "or the general sessions criminal court" and substituting ", the general sessions criminal
court, or a criminal court when the criminal court is hearing a criminal matter involving a
domestic abuse victim, sexual assault victim, or stalking victim"; and by deleting "the general
sessions court" and substituting "the criminal court or the general sessions court".
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
