Section 1:
By Yager
HOUSE BILL 672
By Scarbrough
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 7,
Chapter 53, Part 3, relative to industrial
development corporations.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 7-53-317(a)(2), is amended by
deleting the period (.) at the end of the subdivision and adding the following language:
; however, upon dissolution of the nonprofit entity, the acreage shall be transferred to a
501(c)(3) nonprofit entity whose main purpose is to promote industrial development and
new nuclear development.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
