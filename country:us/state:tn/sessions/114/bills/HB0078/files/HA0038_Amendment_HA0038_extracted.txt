Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 40-11-154, is amended by adding
the following as a new subsection:
(e) Venue for prosecution under this section is the county in which the conduct
constituting the violation of a condition of release occurred; however, if one (1) or more
elements of the offense are committed in one (1) county and one (1) or more elements in
another county, then the offense may be prosecuted in either county.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming law, the public welfare requiring it.
