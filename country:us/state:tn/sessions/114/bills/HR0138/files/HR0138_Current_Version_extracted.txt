HOUSE RESOLUTION 138
By Grills
A RESOLUTION to honor Dyersburg State Community College on
being named the College of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we pay tribute to those educational institutions that, through
the natural talents and unflagging commitment to academic excellence evidenced by their
administration, faculty, staff, and students, have brought honor to the State of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Dyersburg State Community College is one such exemplary institution,
which was recently named the College of the Year as part of the Tennessee Board of Regents'
seventh annual SOAR Awards; and
WHEREAS, the SOAR Awards annually recognize one community college based on a
number of criteria, including the institution's major accomplishments for students and the
communities they serve; and
WHEREAS, under the leadership of Dr. Scott Cook, president of Dyersburg State
Community College, the institution has focused on fostering student success and creating an
empowering educational environment; and
WHEREAS, this prestigious honor is a tremendous source of pride for the
administration, faculty, staff, and students of Dyersburg State Community College and the
residents of the State of Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, the dedication to academic excellence demonstrated by all the fine
individuals affiliated with Dyersburg State Community College is clearly deserving of our
respect, admiration, and commendation; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we
congratulate Dyersburg State Community College on being named the College of the Year,

salute the school's administration, faculty, staff, and students on this prestigious honor, and
extend to them our best wishes for much continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
