HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 668
By Camper
A RESOLUTION to commend Derryus Shaw for exemplary
service as a legislative intern.
WHEREAS, each legislative session, this General Assembly is pleased to recognize
certain persons who have committed themselves, through their adroit service, to the betterment
of the legislative process; and
WHEREAS, Derryus Shaw is one such individual, who has distinguished himself in his
role as a legislative intern for Representative Karen Camper; Mr. Shaw's work has resulted in
innumerable benefits for the legislators he assisted and the General Assembly as a whole; and
WHEREAS, a student at Austin Peay State University, where he is highly regarded by
his peers, Derryus Shaw has achieved remarkable academic success while majoring in political
science and minoring in history; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Shaw is a member of Phi Alpha Theta national honor society for history,
Chi Alpha Christian University Ministry, Govs Programming Council, and History Club, which he
served as vice president; he has received both the O.S. Uffelman Exemplary History Student
Award Endowment and the Fred Seip, Jr. Memorial Scholarship; and
WHEREAS, eager to familiarize himself with the myriad aspects and complexities of
Tennessee State government and to further his studies, Mr. Shaw brought his many abilities
and singular expertise to Capitol Hill for the 2025 session of the One Hundred Fourteenth
General Assembly; and
WHEREAS, throughout the 2025 legislative session, Mr. Shaw has been punctual,
professional, and a joy to have in the office; he is a bright-shining star and a great sign of hope
for future generations; and
HJR0668
007707
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Derryus Shaw has performed his many duties with extraordinary insight and
rare decorum; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby commend Derryus Shaw for outstanding service as a legislative
intern for Representative Karen Camper and extend to him our best wishes for every future
success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
