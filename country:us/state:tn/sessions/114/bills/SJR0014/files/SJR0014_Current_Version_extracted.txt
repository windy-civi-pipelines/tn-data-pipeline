SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 14
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to call a joint convention of the Senate and the
House of Representatives for the purpose of
electing a Secretary of State, a Comptroller of
the Treasury, and a State Treasurer.
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that the Senate and the House of Representatives meet
in joint convention in the chamber of the House of Representatives on Wednesday, January 15,
2025, at 9:30 a.m. (CST) for the purpose of electing a Secretary of State, a Comptroller of the
Treasury, and a State Treasurer, and for the purpose of administering the oath of office to such
officials.
