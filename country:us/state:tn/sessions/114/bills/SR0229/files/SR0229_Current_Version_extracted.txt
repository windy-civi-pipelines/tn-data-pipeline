SENATE RESOLUTION 229
By Yarbro
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Lillian Kate Padgett,
2025 Valedictorian of Valor Flagship Academy.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Lillian Kate Padgett is one such exceptional student, who was recently
named Valedictorian of the 2025 graduating class at Valor Flagship Academy; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Ms. Padgett has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Lillian Kate Padgett has earned an
exemplary grade point average while participating in a number of extracurricular activities; and
WHEREAS, her display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes well for her
future success; and
WHEREAS, Lillian Kate Padgett's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of
pride for her family, her school, and her community; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Padgett's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the respect,
admiration, and commendation of the members of this legislative body; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we commend and honor the
2025 Valor Flagship Academy Valedictorian, Lillian Kate Padgett, on her impressive academic
achievement and extend to her our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
