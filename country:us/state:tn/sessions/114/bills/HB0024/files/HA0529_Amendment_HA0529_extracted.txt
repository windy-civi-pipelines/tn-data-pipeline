by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 70-4-106(a), is amended by deleting
subdivision (2) and substituting:
(2) A violation of this subsection (a) is a Class B misdemeanor, punishable by a
fine only, not to exceed five hundred dollars ($500) for each violation. Upon conviction
for any violation of this subsection (a), the court may revoke the license of the person so
convicted. Any license so revoked shall be surrendered to the court and transmitted to
the arresting officer, to be made a part of the prosecution record.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 70-4-106(b)(2), is amended by
deleting subdivision (A) and substituting:
(A) A violation of this subsection (b) is a Class B misdemeanor, punishable by a
fine only, not to exceed five hundred dollars ($500) for each violation. Upon conviction
for any violation of this subsection (b), the court may revoke the license of the person
convicted. Any license so revoked shall be surrendered to the court.
