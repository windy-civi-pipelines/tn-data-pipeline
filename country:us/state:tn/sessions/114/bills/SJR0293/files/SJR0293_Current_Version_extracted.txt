SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 293
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to congratulate Cocke County Farm Bureau
upon its receipt of the Tennessee Farm Bureau
Federation's President's Award.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize an agricultural organization that works with
purpose and conviction to develop, foster, and promote programs to ensure the economic,
social, educational, and political well-being of those in the agricultural industry throughout
Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Cocke County Farm Bureau was recently honored with the Tennessee
Farm Bureau Federation's President's Award at the 103rd annual meeting of the Tennessee
Farm Bureau Federation, the largest Farm Bureau in the nation with a membership of more than
695,000; and
WHEREAS, this award is a most befitting recognition of the Cocke County Farm
Bureau's exemplary program and service activities in the areas of advocacy, organizational
advancement, and leadership development; and
WHEREAS, Cocke County Farm Bureau has proven itself one of the standout county
organizations in Tennessee and should be applauded for the well-earned success of its
impressive, hard-working membership; and
WHEREAS, the organization has implemented several new programs and projects to
better promote agriculture, to bring greater awareness to state and federal elected officials of
the needs of the agriculture community, and to ensure policy development meetings are
recognized as meeting the needs of the entire agriculture industry; and
WHEREAS, Cocke County Farm Bureau President Bettye Carver and the members,
staff, and leadership of Cocke County Farm Bureau have distinguished themselves as stalwart

protectors of the farming community, and they are most worthy of our approbation; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the Cocke County Farm
Bureau upon receiving the Tennessee Farm Bureau Federation's President's Award, extending
our best wishes for the organization's continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
