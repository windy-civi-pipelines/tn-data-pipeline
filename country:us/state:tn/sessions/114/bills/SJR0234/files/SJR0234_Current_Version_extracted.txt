SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 234
By Southerland
A RESOLUTION to honor Randi Delong on being named NJCAA
Softball Player of the Week.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that we should recognize those incredibly successful student
athletes who have distinguished themselves as inspirational leaders among their peers; and
WHEREAS, Randi Delong of the Walters State Community College Lady Senators was
recently named NJCAA Softball Player of the Week in recognition of her superior offensive
performance on the diamond on February 7-8, 2025; and
WHEREAS, Randi Delong impressed everyone by recording eight hits, four home runs,
and nine RBIs during a four-game series versus Gordon State; and
WHEREAS, the freshman was a perfect 5-5 at the plate with two home runs and four
RBIs in the final game of the series; and
WHEREAS, on February 25, 2025, Brandi Delong was named TCCAA Softball Player of
the Week for the second time this season for her awe-inspiring hitting spree during a seven-
game stretch in the third week of the month; and
WHEREAS, for these seven games, Randi Delong batted .428 with three home runs, 14
RBIs, a double, and a triple; she smacked a grand slam and drove in five runs in her biggest
game of the week against Pearl River; and
WHEREAS, as of March 5, 2025, she is batting a remarkable .458 for the season with
nine home runs and 34 RBIs; and
WHEREAS, Brandi Delong exemplifies the spirit, determination, and perseverance for
which Tennesseans are notable, and she is richly deserving of our approbation on this special
occasion; now, therefore,
SJR0234
004688
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Randi Delong on being
named NJCAA Softball Player of the Week and twice being honored as TCCAA Softball Player
of the Week this season and wish her much continued success both on and off the diamond.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
