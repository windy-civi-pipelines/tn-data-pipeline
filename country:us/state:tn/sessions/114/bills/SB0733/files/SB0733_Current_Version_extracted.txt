Section 1:
By Boyd
SENATE BILL 733
By Pody
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49,
relative to testing in education.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-1-907, is amended by deleting the
first sentence of the section and substituting:
By December 31 of each year, the department of education shall submit an early grades
reading report to the education committee of the senate and the committees of the
house of representatives with jurisdiction over subject matter pertaining to education.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
