Briggs
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting all language after the enacting clause and substituting:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. This act is known and may be cited as the "Veterans Assistance for
Livelihood, Opportunity, and Relief (VALOR) Act."

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 67-5-704, is amended by deleting the
language "one-hundred seventy-five thousand dollars ($175,000)" wherever it appears and
substituting instead "two hundred fifty thousand dollars ($250,000)".

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect January 1, 2026, the public welfare requiring it.
