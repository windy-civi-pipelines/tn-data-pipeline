AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 40,
Chapter 11, relative to surrender of defendants.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 40-11-132, is amended by
redesignating the current language as subsection (a) and adding the following new subsection:
(b) The sheriff must accept surrender of a defendant by a bail bondsman or
surety, pending a hearing under ยง 40-11-137 to determine whether there is good cause
for the surrender.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
