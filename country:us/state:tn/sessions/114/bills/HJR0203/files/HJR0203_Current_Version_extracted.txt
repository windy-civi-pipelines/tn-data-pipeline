HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 203
By Farmer
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Dr. John Robinette on
his selection as the 2024–2025 Sevier County
School System Supervisor of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding professionals who have devoted their careers to providing
the highest quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Dr. John Robinette is one such estimable administrator, who has
distinguished himself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Robinette has been selected as the 2024–2025 Sevier County School
System Supervisor of the Year, a most befitting honor that recognizes the positive influence he
has on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, since 2022, Dr. John Robinette has served with acumen and alacrity as the
Data and Accountability Supervisor and Testing Coordinator for the Sevier County School
System, in which position he is proud to support and enable educators to focus on their critical
work in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Robinette has a passion for education that is rooted in a family legacy,
with more than a dozen educators among his relatives; and
WHEREAS, his professional accomplishments aside, Dr. John Robinette is most grateful
for the love and support of his wife and Informational and Data Management Specialist for the
Sevier County School System, Anna, and his children, Jake and Claire; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Robinette is an extremely talented and compassionate administrator
who works assiduously to motivate his students, teachers, and staff to reach their full potential
as scholars, professionals, and citizens; and

WHEREAS, Dr. John Robinette serves as an inspiration to the Sevier County School
System, and his friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are fortunate to know
him; and
WHEREAS, Dr. Robinette truly epitomizes the dedicated professional who is wholly
committed to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and he should be
specially recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Dr. John Robinette on being chosen as
the 2024–2025 Sevier County School System Supervisor of the Year, commend his exceptional
service to the children of Tennessee, salute his professional expertise and personal excellence,
and wish him all the best in his future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
