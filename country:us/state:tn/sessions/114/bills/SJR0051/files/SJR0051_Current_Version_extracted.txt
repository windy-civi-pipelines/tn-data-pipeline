SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 51
By Powers
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Shane McCarrell,
2024 International Auctioneer Champion.
WHEREAS, it is the privilege of the members of this General Assembly to recognize
those dedicated citizens who have excelled in their field, bringing great pride to this State; and
WHEREAS, one such individual is Clarksville's Shane McCarrell, who won the 2024
International Auctioneer Championship, the culmination of years of devoted excellence to the art
and business of auctioneering; and
WHEREAS, Mr. McCarrell convened in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, along with seventy-
eight fellow competitors from across the globe; participants were judged on their bid calling,
presentation skills, and overall performance; and
WHEREAS, in addition to his recent victory, he has been recognized in various
auctioneering competitions over the years, including the Tennessee Rookie Championship,
Volunteer Open Championship, and the 2019 TN State Championship; he has also garnered
multiple top-ten finishes at the International Auctioneer Championship; and
WHEREAS, with more than twelve years of active-duty military service in the U.S.
Marines and U.S. Army, Shane McCarrell brings a unique perspective to the auctioneering
profession that incorporates discipline and the ability to succeed even in the face of adversity;
and
WHEREAS, Mr. McCarrell graduated from the Nashville Auction School, and he
previously served as president of the Tennessee Auctioneers Association; as the current co-
owner and education director of the Nashville Auction School, he is dedicated to educating the
next generation of auctioneers; and

WHEREAS, Shane McCarrell has proven himself elite in his field; his victory is a great
source of pride for his family, his community, and this State; and
WHEREAS, it is most fitting that we recognize this extraordinary Tennessean on this
auspicious occasion; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate 2024 International
Auctioneer Champion Shane McCarrell, extending our best wishes for every continued success.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
