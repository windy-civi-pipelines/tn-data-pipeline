HOUSE BILL 1173

AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 12,
Chapter 3, relative to public purchases.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 12-3-201(8)(B), is amended by
deleting subdivision (8)(B) and substituting:
(8)
(B) "Goods" also means all improvements to real property, including, but
not limited to, buildings, structures, equipment, fixtures, and anything else
permanently attached to real property, but does not include real property;

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 12-3-1201(c), is amended by
deleting the subsection and substituting:
(c) To the extent permitted by federal law or regulations, local governments may
make purchases of:
(1) Goods, except motor vehicles other than those manufactured for a

purchase contracts either directly or through the appropriate state agency;
provided, that no purchase under this section shall be made at a price higher
than that which is contained in the contract between the general services
administration and the vendor affected; and
(2) Goods and services directly, without bid or quotations, from vendors
awarded general services administration (GSA) schedule contracts at prices
