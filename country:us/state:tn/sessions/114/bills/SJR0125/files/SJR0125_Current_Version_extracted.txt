SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 125
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to honor the memory of Barbara Joyce Hedrick
of Jefferson City.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly were greatly saddened to learn of
the passing of Barbara Joyce Hedrick; and
WHEREAS, Barbara Hedrick was an exemplary public servant and consummate
professional who worked assiduously to improve the quality of life for her fellow citizens in
numerous capacities; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Hedrick served with acumen and alacrity at Howmet for twenty-five
years, in which position she earned the respect of peers and superiors alike for the exemplary
quality of her work; and
WHEREAS, Barbara Hedrick was a member of Emmanuel Baptist Church, which she
served faithfully for many years; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Hedrick was also deeply devoted to her family, and she always
endeavored to remain true to family values of the highest order; and
WHEREAS, Barbara Hedrick enjoyed the loving companionship of her husband, James
Eddie Hedrick, for sixty years and was the proud mother of Tammy Kearney (Tim Solomon),
Tracey (Rachael) Hedrick, and Kim (Todd) Kesterson and grandmother of Cynthia, Kaylee,
Mackenzie, and Alyssa; and
WHEREAS, Mrs. Hedrick is further survived by her sisters, Linda (Ronnie) Jones and
Sue Winstead; several nieces and nephews; and her special caregiver, Denene Long; and
WHEREAS, in addition to her parents, Charles and Mary Ruth Kelley, Mrs. Hedrick was
preceded in death by four brothers, one sister, and a son-in-law, Mike Kearney; and
WHEREAS, Barbara Hedrick leaves behind an indelible legacy of integrity and probity in
public life, compassion and loyalty in private life, and diligence and dedication in all her chosen
endeavors; and
SJR0125
003436
- 1 -
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
remember the bountiful life of this exceptional public servant and human being; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor the memory of Barbara Joyce Hedrick,
reflecting fondly upon her impeccable character and her stalwart commitment to living the
examined life with courage and conviction.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that we express our sympathy and offer our condolences
to the family of Mrs. Hedrick.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
