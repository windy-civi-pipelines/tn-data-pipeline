SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 168
By Seal
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Lakeway Christian
Academy on winning the TSSAA Division II,
Class A-AA duals wrestling state championship.
WHEREAS, we take great pleasure in paying tribute to those exemplary sports teams
and athletes who have attained the pinnacle of competition, bringing great honor and acclaim to
Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, one such exceptional group of young athletes is the Lakeway Christian
Academy wrestling team, whose members have all proven themselves to be the fiercest of
competitors and truly worthy of admiration by winning the TSSAA Division II, Class A-AA duals
wrestling state championship; and
WHEREAS, this was the second consecutive duals state championship for the Lions and
their third championship in four years, as they trounced Battle Ground Academy in the
semifinals, 56-21, before defeating Chattanooga Christian, 51-30, to claim the state title; and
WHEREAS, the members of the Lakeway Christian Academy wrestling team epitomize
all that is good in today's student athletes, as they have achieved a premier level of success in
competition while exhibiting the highest degree of character in all aspects of life; and
WHEREAS, the perseverance, determination, work ethic, and talent of this group of
young men not only made possible a championship season but also surely bode well for their
future success; and
WHEREAS, to win a state championship is an extraordinary achievement, and the
members of the Lakeway Christian Academy wrestling team should be recognized for their
accomplishments on the wrestling mat; now, therefore,
SJR0168
003798
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate the coaches and
members of the Lakeway Christian Academy wrestling team on winning the TSSAA Division II,
Class A-AA duals wrestling state championship and wish them much continued success in their
future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
