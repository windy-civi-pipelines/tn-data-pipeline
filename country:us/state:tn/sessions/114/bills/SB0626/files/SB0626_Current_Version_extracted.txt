Section 1:
By Raper
SENATE BILL 626
By Briggs
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 2,
relative to elections.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 2-5-151(c), is amended by adding
the following new sentence between the first and second sentences:
The commission may notify the individual or individuals filing the petition of a defect in
the documentation.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
