Section 1:
SENATE RESOLUTION 11
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to confirm the appointment of Nisha Powers of
Memphis to the Tennessee board of regents.
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, provides for the creation of
the Tennessee board of regents; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, further provides that the
governor shall appoint twelve members to the Tennessee board of regents; and
WHEREAS, Governor Bill Lee has appointed Nisha Powers of Memphis to the
Tennessee board of regents as a member representing the 9th Congressional District to a term
that will expire on June 30, 2030; and
WHEREAS, a highly successful businesswoman, Mrs. Powers is the president of
Powers Hill Design, LLC, a civil engineering firm she established in 2005; she holds a Bachelor
of Science degree in Civil Engineering from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville; and
WHEREAS, Nisha Powers is a person of integrity and an active citizen whose
benevolence, acumen, and strong commitment to public service have helped to enhance the
well-being of this State and its citizens; and
WHEREAS, Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 49-8-201, provides that each member
appointed by the governor to the Tennessee board of regents shall be confirmed by the senate;
now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that this body does hereby confirm
the appointment of Nisha Powers to the Tennessee board of regents for a term that will expire
on June 30, 2030.
