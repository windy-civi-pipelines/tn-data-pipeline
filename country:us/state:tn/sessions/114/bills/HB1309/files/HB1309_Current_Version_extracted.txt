Section 1:
By Johnson
HOUSE BILL 1309
By Lamberth
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Section
64-9-107, relative to the management of utility
systems.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 64-9-107, is amended by inserting
the following as a new subsection:
(e) Interest income accruing on investments and deposits as a result of
subsection (a) must be credited to a separate account for the purpose of carrying out
this section, must not revert to the general fund, and must be carried forward into each
subsequent fiscal year.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
