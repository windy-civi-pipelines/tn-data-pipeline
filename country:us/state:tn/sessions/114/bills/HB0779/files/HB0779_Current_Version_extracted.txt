Section 1:
By Pody
HOUSE BILL 779
By Maberry
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 48,
Chapter 101, Part 5, relative to registration
exemptions for educational institutions.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 48-101-502(b), is amended by
deleting "a local board of education, the achievement school district, or the state board of
education" and substituting "a local board of education, the achievement school district, or the
Tennessee public charter school commission".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
