Section 1:
By McKenzie
SENATE BILL 1175
By Akbari
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49,
relative to school districts.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 49, Chapter 2, Part 7, is amended by
adding the following as a new section:
By July 31, 2025, and by each July 31 thereafter, the department shall list on its
website each LEA designated as a high performing school district for the upcoming
school year and how long the LEA will be designated a high performing school district
before having to meet the criteria for re-designation pursuant to this part.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
