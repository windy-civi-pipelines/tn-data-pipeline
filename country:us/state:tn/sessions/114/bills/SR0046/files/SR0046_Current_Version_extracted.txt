SENATE RESOLUTION 46
By Walley
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Caleigh McGowan,
Victory McKinnie, Morgan Crutcher, and
Ke'Miyah Buggs of Fayette-Ware
Comprehensive High School on being named to
the Class 3A, District 14 All-District team.
WHEREAS, we are pleased to recognize and commend those successful high school
athletes who, through their magnificent exploits, bring honor to Tennessee and serve as
exceptional ambassadors of this State; and
WHEREAS, four such estimable individuals are Caleigh McGowan, Victory McKinnie,
Morgan Crutcher, and Ke'Miyah Buggs of Fayette-Ware Comprehensive High School, athletes
of extraordinary talent, skill, and dedication, who have proven themselves to be formidable
competitors on the basketball court; and
WHEREAS, these four exceptional young women were recently named members of the
Class 3A, District 14 All-District team in recognition of their excellence on the hardwood this
past season for the Fayette-Ware Lady Wildcats; and
WHEREAS, Caleigh McGowan, Victory McKinnie, Morgan Crutcher, and Ke'Miyah
Buggs epitomize all that is good in today's student athletes, as each one has achieved a high
level of success in sports while evincing great character both on and off the court; and
WHEREAS, their notable achievements are a source of great pride for their families,
their community, and their school, and it is appropriate that we recognize these accomplished
athletes; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we honor and congratulate
Caleigh McGowan, Victory McKinnie, Morgan Crutcher, and Ke'Miyah Buggs of Fayette-Ware

Comprehensive High School on being named to the Class 3A, District 14 All-District team,
wishing them much success and happiness in their future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
