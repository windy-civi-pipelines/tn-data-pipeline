Section 1:
By Lamberth
SENATE BILL 1298
By Johnson
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Section
65-6-133, relative to the commissioning of railroad
police officers.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 65-6-133(a), is amended by deleting
the language "governor" and substituting instead the language "commissioner of safety".

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 65-6-133(b), is amended by deleting
the language "governor" wherever it appears and substituting instead the language
"commissioner".

Section 4:
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.

HOUSE BILL 121
By Lamberth
SENATE BILL 1298
By Johnson
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Section
65-6-133, relative to the commissioning of railroad
police officers.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 65-6-133(a), is amended by deleting
the language "governor" and substituting instead the language "commissioner of safety".
SECTION 2. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 65-6-133(b), is amended by deleting
the language "governor" wherever it appears and substituting instead the language
"commissioner".
SECTION 3. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
