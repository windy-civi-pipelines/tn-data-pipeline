SENATE RESOLUTION 92
By Oliver
A RESOLUTION to recognize Jade Wilkison on her selection as
the 2024–2025 Nashville School of the Arts
Teacher of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this legislative body should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest-quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Jade Wilkison is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished
herself as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, a teacher at Nashville School of the Arts, Ms. Wilkison was recently named
the school's 2024–2025 Teacher of the Year, a most befitting honor for her tenure as an
educator, as it recognizes the positive influence she has on so many young lives; and
WHEREAS, Jade Wilkison is an extremely talented and compassionate educator who
has worked assiduously throughout her career in education to motivate her students, fellow
teachers, and staff to reach their full potential as scholars, professionals, and citizens; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Wilkison truly serves as an inspiration to the faculty and staff at
Nashville School of the Arts, and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are
fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Jade Wilkison epitomizes the dedicated educator who is wholly committed
to the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, that we hereby honor and
congratulate Jade Wilkison on being chosen as the 2024–2025 Nashville School of the Arts

professional expertise and personal excellence, and wish her all the best in her future

for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy.
