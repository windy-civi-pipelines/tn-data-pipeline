Amendment No. 2 to SB1083

by designating subsection (b) as subdivision (b)(1) in Sections 2-7 and by adding the following
new subdivision (b)(2) in Sections 2-7:
(2) As used in this subsection (b), "lawful eligibility criteria" does not include:
(A) A familial relationship between the applicant or employee and any elected
official, public employee, or contractor of the governmental entity;
(B) Past or present political activity, campaign involvement, or service as an
elected official by the applicant, employee, or their immediate family;
(C) Financial contributions or donations made by the applicant, employee, or
their immediate family to any political campaign, political action committee, any entity of
the government, or a nonprofit or charity; or
(D) Any recommendation or referral by an elected official, political appointee, or
campaign representative that is not based on objective merit or qualifications.
AND FURTHER AMEND by designating subsection (g) as subdivision (g)(1) in Section 8 and
adding the following new subdivision (g)(2):
(2) As used in this subsection (g), "lawful eligibility criteria" does not include:
(A) A familial relationship between the applicant or employee and any elected
official, public employee, or contractor of the governmental entity;
(B) Past or present political activity, campaign involvement, or service as an
elected official by the applicant, employee, or their immediate family;
