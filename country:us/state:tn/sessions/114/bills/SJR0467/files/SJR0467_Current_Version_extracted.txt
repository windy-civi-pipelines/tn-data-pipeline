SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 467
By Briggs
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Yeshi Hammann, 2025
Valedictorian of Knoxville Christian School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Yeshi Hammann is one such exceptional student, who was recently named
Valedictorian of the 2025 graduating class at Knoxville Christian School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Yeshi Hammann has achieved outstanding academic
success and joined the ranks of the best and brightest students in Tennessee and our country;
and
WHEREAS, in her remarkable high school career, Yeshi Hammann has earned an
exemplary 3.93 grade point average while participating in numerous extracurricular activities,
including the girls' volleyball team, which she served as captain for four years, the National
Honor Society, and student council; and
WHEREAS, in addition to her superlative scholastic endeavors, Ms. Hammann has
already proven herself capable in the workforce by holding multiple jobs, and she has
generously given of her time as a childcare volunteer at her church; and
WHEREAS, Yeshi Hammann's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes
well for her future success as a student at East Tennessee State University; and
WHEREAS, Yeshi Hammann's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride
for her parents, Ed and Wendy Hammann, her school, and her community; and
SJR0467
007824
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Yeshi Hammann's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the
respect, admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor the 2025 Knoxville
Christian School Valedictorian, Yeshi Hammann, on her impressive academic achievement and
extend to her our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
