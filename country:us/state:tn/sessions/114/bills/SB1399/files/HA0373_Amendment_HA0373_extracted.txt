Section 1:
House Judiciary 1
Amendment No. 1 to HB1353
Farmer
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting subdivision (a)(2)(B) in SECTION 1 and substituting:
(B) "Individual involved in the judicial process" means:
(i) A witness;
(ii) A judge or former judge;
(iii) An employee of the judge;
(iv) A district attorney general or former district attorney general;
(v) An assistant district attorney general or former assistant district
attorney general;
(vi) An employee of the district attorney general;
(vii) A law enforcement officer or former law enforcement officer;
(viii) A clerk or former clerk;
(ix) An employee of the clerk;
(x) A juror or former juror;
(xi) A district public defender or former district public defender;
(xii) An assistant district public defender or former assistant district public
defender; or
(xiii) An employee of the district public defender.
