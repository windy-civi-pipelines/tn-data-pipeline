HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 387
By Terry
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Carley Jaymes Vogel,
a Middle Tennessee State University senior.
WHEREAS, the members of this General Assembly are pleased to specially honor and
commend Carley Jaymes Vogel for her magnificent exploits and dedication to bringing honor to
Tennessee and serving as an exceptional ambassador for this State; and
WHEREAS, Carley Jaymes Vogel is a Middle Tennessee State University senior
pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Music Business with a Music Theatre minor; and
WHEREAS, crowned 2024 Miss Tennessee, Ms. Vogel achieved the extraordinary
honor of placing third in the 2025 Miss America Pageant out of fifty-two contestants, the first
time a Miss Tennessee contestant has been in the top five since 2010; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Vogel also earned a preliminary talent award for her vocal performance
of "Climb Every Mountain" in the talent portion of the Miss America Pageant, dedicating the
performance of the song to her grandmother who recently passed away; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Vogel is a talented singer, having sung the national anthem more than
200 times, including at a Nashville Predators game and a Nashville Sounds game; and
WHEREAS, diagnosed with chronic liver disease when she was twelve years old, Ms.
Vogel has become a community figure and a determined advocate for liver health and organ
donation, partnering with Donate Life Tennessee and completing a campaign with the
Department of Safety and Homeland Security in Tennessee; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Vogel's singular achievements are a result of hard work, dedication,
and determination, which qualities will ensure her future success as an ambassador for
Tennessee; and
HJR0387
005515
- 1 -
WHEREAS, we find it appropriate to acknowledge and applaud Ms. Carley Jaymes
Vogel of Middle Tennessee State University for serving as an example of a role model and
community servant to others in the State of Tennessee; now, therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Carley Jaymes Vogel upon her many
achievements, applaud her successes at the 2024 Miss Tennessee and 2025 Miss America
pageants, commend her service as an advocate for liver health and organ donation, and extend
to her our best wishes for much continued success in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
