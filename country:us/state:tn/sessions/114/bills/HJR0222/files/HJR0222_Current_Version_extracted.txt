HOUSE JOINT RESOLUTION 222
By Keisling
A RESOLUTION to honor and congratulate Brandon Boone upon
his induction into the Clay County Sports Hall of
Fame.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that this General Assembly pay tribute to those gifted athletes,
coaches, and administrators who have attained the very pinnacle of sporting achievement; and
WHEREAS, Brandon Boone is one such legendary athlete, who was recently inducted
into the Clay County Sports Hall of Fame; and
WHEREAS, an iconic figure within the annals of both the football and baseball programs
at Celina High School, Mr. Boone is considered by many to be one of the best two-way football
players in the school's history; and
WHEREAS, Brandon Boone was a four-year starter at lineman for the Celina High
School football program from 1992 to 1995, using his imposing frame to open up running lanes
for the Bulldogs' outstanding rushers; and
WHEREAS, on the defensive side of the ball, he put together a consistent and
impressive body of work; Brandon Boone's 203 career tackles place him among the Top 20
tacklers of all-time at Celina and distinguish him as one of the best defensive lineman in
program history; and
WHEREAS, Brandon Boone received All-Region honors in football and was the recipient
of the Carmon C. Brown Award, which honors Celina's most prestigious male athlete; several
colleges, including the University of Tennessee, expressed interest in him playing at the next
level; and
WHEREAS, in addition to the gridiron, Brandon Boone enjoyed a stellar career on the
baseball diamond and was a two-year member of the Celina basketball team; and
HJR0222
003508
- 1 -
WHEREAS, Brandon Boone demonstrated leadership, skill, and athleticism throughout
his athletic career, and it is fitting that we should honor him on this special occasion; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE ONE HUNDRED
FOURTEENTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE SENATE
CONCURRING, that we honor and congratulate Brandon Boone upon his induction into the
Clay County Sports Hall of Fame and extend to him our best wishes for much continued
success and happiness.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
