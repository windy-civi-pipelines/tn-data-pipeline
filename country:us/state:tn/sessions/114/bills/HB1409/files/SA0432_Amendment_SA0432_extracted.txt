Section 1:
Amendment No. 9 to SB1431
Akbari
Signature of Sponsor
by deleting the item in SECTION 61 that contains the following language and renumbering the
remaining items accordingly:
In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act, there is
appropriated the sum of $4,500,000 (recurring) to the Attorney General and Reporter for
the sole purpose of establishing ten (10) positions, implementing a salary market rate
adjustment, and providing additional funding for the special litigation program.
AND FURTHER AMEND by inserting the following as new, appropriately designated items at
the end of SECTION 61:
Item __. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $1,500,000 (recurring) to the District Public Defenders
Conference for the sole purpose of salary increases for district public defenders,
including the twentieth and thirtieth judicial district assistant public defenders.
Item __. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $1,500,000 (recurring) to the District Attorneys General
Conference for the sole purpose of salary increases for district attorneys general.
Item __. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $1,500,000 (recurring) to the Court System for the sole
purpose of improving safety and efficiency in the court system.
