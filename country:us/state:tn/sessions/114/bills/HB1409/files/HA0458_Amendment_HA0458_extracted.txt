by adding the following as new, appropriately designated sections immediately preceding the

Section 2:
SECTION __. Appropriations in this act to the Office of the Attorney General and
Reporter are hereby reduced by the sum of $14,280,310.

Section 3:
SECTION __. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $3,000,000 (recurring) to the Department of Agriculture for the
sole purpose of supporting the AgLaunch program.

Section 4:
SECTION __. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $500,000 (recurring) to the Department of Finance and
Administration for the sole purpose of making a grant in such amount to Big Brothers Big Sisters
of Middle Tennessee, to be used for programs and services in this state.

Section 5:
SECTION __. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $500,000 (recurring) to the Department of Commerce and
Insurance for the sole purpose of funding the GoBuild Tennessee program.

Section 6:
SECTION __. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $1,000,000 (recurring) to the Department of Education for the
sole purpose of supporting the Jobs for TN Graduates program.

Section 7:
SECTION __. In addition to any other funds appropriated by the provisions of this act,
there is appropriated the sum of $1,000,000 (recurring) to the Department of Human Services
for the sole purpose of supporting the Nashville Anti-Human Trafficking program.

programs and services in this state.
