Section 2:
SECTION __. From funds available to the Department of Education for the
Education Freedom Scholarship Program, there is earmarked a sum sufficient for the
sole purpose of making a grant in such amount to the Department of Transportation, to
be used to fund public transit improvements in this state; provided, that at least fifty
percent (50%) of such funding be distributed to the Memphis Area Transit Authority for
such purposes.
