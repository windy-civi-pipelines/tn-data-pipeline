Section 1:
By Walley
HOUSE BILL 94
By Shaw
AN ACT to amend Tennessee Code Annotated, Title 38,
Chapter 7; Title 41, Chapter 21 and Title 68,
Chapter 4, relative to deceased inmates.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. Tennessee Code Annotated, Section 41-21-221, is amended by adding
the following as a new subsection (c):
(c) This state shall pay for the examination required under subsection (a) and, if
necessary, any transportation costs associated with the examination.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. This act takes effect upon becoming a law, the public welfare requiring it.
