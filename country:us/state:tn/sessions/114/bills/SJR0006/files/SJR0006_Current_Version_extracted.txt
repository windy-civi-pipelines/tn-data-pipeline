SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 6
By Johnson
A RESOLUTION to honor and commend the October 7th Coalition
and Patricia Heaton.
WHEREAS, founded by actress and activist Patricia Heaton, along with executive
director Elizabeth Dorros, the October 7th Coalition has established itself as a commendable
movement of Christian shepherds, leaders, influencers, and neighbors dedicated to fostering
public and private support and protection of the Jewish community; and
WHEREAS, the Coalition recognizes the profound bond shared by Christians and Jews,
a kinship rooted in Judeo-Christian heritage and tradition and the shared ancestors of Abraham,
Isaac, and Jacob/Israel; and
WHEREAS, upholding the sovereign right of Israel to exist, the October 7th Coalition
stands firmly against all forms of antisemitism; and
WHEREAS, the Coalition's mission is to unite, promote, and encourage meaningful
public and private action in the Christian community to strengthen the bonds of friendship and
understanding with the Jewish community; and
WHEREAS, the October 7th Coalition seeks to achieve its noble goals through various
ecumenical opportunities that aim to educate, discover commonalities, and inspire Christians to
actively support the Jewish community within their realms of influence; and
WHEREAS, Patricia Heaton, an esteemed member of the entertainment community and
a person of deep faith, has exemplified the Coalition's values through her unwavering support
and advocacy for the Jewish people; and
WHEREAS, since October 7, 2023, Ms. Heaton has consistently spoken out for Israel
and called for the release of the hostages held by Hamas, and she recently launched a

mezuzah campaign encouraging non-Jews to affix mezuzahs to their doorposts in solidarity with
Israel and the Jewish people; and
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should specially
recognize the October 7th Coalition and Patricia Heaton for their noble endeavors; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we honor and commend the October 7th Coalition
and Patricia Heaton for their dedication to fostering understanding and support between the
Christian and Jewish communities and their steadfast rejection of antisemitism in all its forms.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
