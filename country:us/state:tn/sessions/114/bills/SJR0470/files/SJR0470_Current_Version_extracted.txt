SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 470
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to honor and recognize Jacob Slaughter of Mt.
Juliet High School.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to pay
tribute to those students who, through their natural talents and unflagging commitment to
academic excellence, have distinguished themselves in the classroom; and
WHEREAS, Jacob Slaughter is one such exceptional student, who will soon graduate
cum laude from Mt. Juliet High School; and
WHEREAS, through diligent study, steady composure during examinations, and
steadfast commitment of time and talent, Mr. Slaughter has maintained a 3.8 grade point
average while working at the Mt. Juliet Kroger, participating in FFA, and assisting a special
needs class through the Nature and Needs Exceptional Learning class at Mt. Juliet High School;
and
WHEREAS, his interest in mechanics led him to work with his father to build a new
motor for his truck; he also enjoys camping with his family; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Slaughter attended Elzie Patton Elementary School, Stonercreek
Elementary School, and West Wilson Middle School, participating in the Seek program and
weathering the 2020 tornadoes that hit West Wilson Middle; and
WHEREAS, Jacob Slaughter's display of intellectual aptitude and ambition surely bodes
well for his future success at trade school, as he begins his journey toward becoming an
electrician; and
WHEREAS, Mr. Slaughter's noteworthy achievement is a tremendous source of pride for
his family, including his parents, Ronald Eugene "Gene" and Jessica Slaughter, as well as his

younger twin siblings, Andrew and Carleigh; his school, his community; and his church
community at Shiloh Baptist Church; and
WHEREAS, Jacob Slaughter's dedication to excellence is clearly deserving of the
respect, admiration, and commendation of the members of this General Assembly; now,
therefore,
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby commend and honor Jacob Slaughter,
2025 Mt. Juliet High School graduate, on his impressive academic achievement and extend to
him our best wishes for a happy, successful future.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
