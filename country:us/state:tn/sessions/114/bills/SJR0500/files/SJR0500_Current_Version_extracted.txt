SENATE JOINT RESOLUTION 500
By Pody
A RESOLUTION to recognize Melissa Cole on her selection as
the 2024–2025 Lakeview Elementary School
Teacher of the Year.
WHEREAS, it is fitting that the members of this General Assembly should pause to
specially recognize those outstanding teachers who have devoted their careers to providing the
highest-quality education for Tennessee's students; and
WHEREAS, Melissa Cole is one such estimable educator, who has distinguished herself
as a professional of the greatest dedication, ability, and integrity; and
WHEREAS, a third-grade math and science teacher at Lakeview Elementary School,
Ms. Cole was recently named the school's 2024–2025 Teacher of the Year, a most befitting
honor for her tenure as an educator, as it recognizes the positive influence she has on so many
young lives; and
WHEREAS, Melissa Cole is an extremely talented and compassionate educator who
has worked assiduously throughout her fourteen-year career in education to motivate her
students to reach their full potential as scholars and citizens; and
WHEREAS, steeped in pedagogical formation, Ms. Cole has earned a Bachelor of
Science degree in Education; and
WHEREAS, Ms. Cole truly serves as an inspiration to the faculty and staff at Lakeview
Elementary School, and her friendship and counsel are highly valued by those who are
fortunate to know her; and
WHEREAS, Melissa Cole epitomizes the dedicated educator who is wholly committed to
the highest ideals of public service and quality education for all, and she should be specially
recognized; now, therefore,
SJR0500
007844
- 1 -
BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE ONE HUNDRED FOURTEENTH
GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF TENNESSEE, THE HOUSE OF
REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING, that we hereby honor and congratulate Melissa Cole on
being chosen as the 2024–2025 Lakeview Elementary School Teacher of the Year, commend
her exceptional service to the children of Tennessee, salute her professional expertise and
personal excellence, and wish her all the best in her future endeavors.
BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED, that an appropriate copy of this resolution be prepared
for presentation with this final clause omitted from such copy and upon proper request made to
the appropriate clerk, the language appearing immediately following the State seal appear
without House or Senate designation.
